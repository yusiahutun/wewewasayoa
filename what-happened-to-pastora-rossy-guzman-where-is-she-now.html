What Happened to Pastora Rossy Guzman? Where Is She Now?

 Posted on August 24, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  443 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

Who is Pastora Rossy Guzman?

Since its establishment in 2008, Pastor Rossy Guzman has taken on the role of head of the Shalom Tierra de Paz church and has devoted her time to spreading the word of God to the citizens of her locality.

She has made great efforts to make the church a safe and hospitable place for everyone. She used to be its minister in the past.

In April 2021, Major General Adán Cáceres’ Coral case involving corruption against the state resulted in the arrest of Rossy Guzman and her son Tanner Flete for their suspected participation. Unfortunately, she was sent to prison for 18 months in 2021. On 9th December 2022, she was released on bail.

What did Pastora Rossy Guzman do?

Pastora Rossy Guzman found herself in hot water when one of her personal videos was uploaded to the web without her knowledge or approval. She was taken into custody due to her supposed link to the dissemination, even though she maintained her innocence.

During the interrogation, Guzmán Sánchez was questioned regarding her involvement in the purported video. She denied any guilt and furnished papers that she asserted backed up her account of what happened.

Pastora Rossy Guzman’s private video has gone viral, causing a great deal of debate. Some people are of the opinion that her privacy has been violated and that the video was released without her permission.

Others, however, think that it serves as a demonstration of the different standards society holds when it comes to women’s sexual behavior.

The fact that the woman is a female leader brings attention to the gender inequality that is present in society, as well as the idea that women are often given stricter rules to follow than men. Had she been a man in a similarly powerful position, the ethical and social implications of the situation would not have been as severe.

Is Pastora Rossy Guzman married?

Pastora Rossy Guzman is a married woman, though her husband’s name is not widely known. She has been successful in her career but has chosen to keep her private life away from public scrutiny in order to maintain her reputation. Consequently, her partner’s identity is not a matter of public knowledge.

Where is she now?

Pastora Rossy Guzman is renowned as the head pastor of the First Christian Church in Bogotá, Colombia. Admired by the Christian community, her sermons are renowned for their charisma and inspiration.

Journalist Nuria Piera uncovered in a report that the evangelical preacher is the proprietor of 10 businesses, 9 pieces of real estate totaling 4,000 square meters in area, worth $709,000, and a variety of luxury vehicles.
