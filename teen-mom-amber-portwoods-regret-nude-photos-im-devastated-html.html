    Regret: Amber Portwood has spoken of her 'devastation' after photos of the Teen Mom star were leaked onto the internet

    Teen Mom's Amber Portwood has spoken of her regret over a set of nude photos which have been leaked on the internet.

    The images show the  20-year-old completely naked in a friend's bedroom, striking a series of provocative poses.

    But she claims the pictures were 'stolen' from her phone and released by 'someone I trusted'.

    The photos appear to have been taken recently as Portwood, who lost 65lbs following her daughter's birth, shows off her December tattoo of baby Leah in the pictures.

    The lifesize image stretches across her torso.

    Portwood, who starts in MTV's reality show Teen Mom, released a statement through the channel.

    'I'm devastated that these pictures were stolen from my phone and sold by someone I trusted,' she said.

    'It makes me sad that anyone would do this, and I'm horrified that something so private is being shared publicly.'

    The past year has been a difficult one for Portwood, who was arrested and charged with domestic violence in relation to scenes screened as part of the show.

    Troubled Portwood was seen hitting and slapping Gary Shirley in front of their two-year-old daughter Leah.

    The two are now reconciled, although both dated over people during a break in their relationship.

    But the legal case against her continues.

    Portwood referred to her troubles in her statement.

    'It's been a difficult year, and I just want to focus on what's important in my life - my daughter,' she said.
