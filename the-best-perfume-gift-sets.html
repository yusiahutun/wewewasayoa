    Dior J'adore Eau de Parfum & Body Milk

    Gilded Glory

    Dior J'adore Eau de Parfum & Body Milk

    Credit: Ulta

    There's no bad time to unwrap a gilded box from Dior. Not only will your friend or family member discover a full-size bottle of Dior's iconic J'adore eau de parfum, but they'll also receive a companion body milk version of the scent that's perfect for layering.

    Fenty Eau De Parfum Travel Set

    Celebrity-Approved

    The Set of Their Dreams

    Tory Burch Essence of Dreams Fragrance Discovery Set

    The Set of Their Dreams

    Tory Burch Essence of Dreams Fragrance Discovery Set

    Credit: Nordstrom

    Your giftee will be delighted to spritz on these limited-edition fragrance selects from Tory Burch's Essence of Dreams collection. From the cactus flower- and lavender-noted Electric Sky, to the honey- and citrus-scented Divine Moon eau de parfum, we're betting these favorites will nicely round out a friend's fragrance wardrobe.

    By Far Daydream of Someone I Knew Gift Set

    By Far Daydream of Someone I Knew Gift Set

    Credit: Bloomingdale's

It also contains a powdery, floral blend of earl grey tea accord, oakmoss, and rose essence to minimize its spiciness. And since the fragrance bottle doubles as an accessory, it can nicely clip on to their purse or belt loop.

    Vyrao Love Bundle

    Fan Club

    Billie Eilish Eilish & Eilish No. 2 Mini Duo Gift Set

    Surprise the Billie Eilish lover in your circle with this set of the singer's first and second fragrances. The original Eilish eau de parfum features a blend of gourmand notes, like warm berries, and creamy vanilla, while the second iteration contains woodier floral ones like wild poppy flower, palo santo, and skin musk.

    YSL Beauty Black Opium Gift Set

    Clean and Serene

    Lake & Skye Discovery 6-Piece Set

From travel-size versions of their woody and citrus Echo Lake fragrance to woody and floral Saffron Dusk, there are plenty of opportunities to mix and match scents this season—and next.

Brand ambassador Miley Cyrus previously told Bazaar that Flora Gorgeous Magnolia in particular, "Reminds me of innocent sensuality. There is a sweetness that smells like young love. It reminds me of falling in love for the first time."
