    I’m a naturally happy person. I find joy in the little things like sparkly shoes, fluffy kittens, and chocolate chip cookies. But there’s one thing that fills me with that bit of extra excitement. It’s accessible, fun, and splurge-worthy; I’m talking about sales, of course, and there’s no better time to snag a deal than right now, since Presidents Day deals are happening as we speak.

    One brand that’s jumping in on the fun? J.Crew. The Kate Middleton and Kerry Washington-worn brand is offering an extra 50 percent off select sale styles and 30 percent off full-priced items with code SHOPNOW. There’s one item I knew I was adding to my cart immediately, though, and that’s the brand’s Quilted Excursion Vest, which is as low as $66 right now.

    Quilted Excursion Vest

    Offering a spacious fit, high collar, and adjustable back tab, this top layer is practical and chic. Further details include snap buttons, patch pockets, an interior loop, and rounded hemlines. Not only does it look stylish and come in three colors, but one of my all time favorite fashionistas, aka Meghan Markle, owns it, too. Markle wore an earlier version of the exact style alongside Prince Harry back in 2022. If that shows me anything, it’s that the vest is sophisticated and modern in the best way possible.    

    This quilted vest can easily be worn with a cozy sweater or a thermal turtleneck underneath for a layered approach. Another option is to style it under a larger puffer jacket. Because of the vest’s slimmer silhouette, it’s perfect for layering without compromising on style or warmth. Finish the look off with slacks for a day at the office, leggings for a cozy morning out, or jeans for a laid-back look. But don’t let the many styling possibilities boggle you down. It’s simple to throw on and go, taking the guesswork out of getting ready, whether you’re headed on a morning jog or to an elevated lunch. This is especially true if you opt for a more neutral hue like brown or black. 

    One shopper agreed, calling the Excursion vest a “good investment” because it “seems to go with everything.” The same reviewer claimed that it’s “comfortable” and can be “worn all day.” But one buyer in particular had super high praise for the Markle-worn piece. “This is my favorite puffer vest, period,” they said. The shopper continued to explain that the “length is perfect,” as it hits right at the hips creating a “flattering silhouette.” The reviewer even shared that you can feel the “warmth on the vest that it keeps around your core” when you take it off — and if that doesn’t show you how toasty and stylish this pick is, I don’t know what does.

    I doubt this staple piece will stay in stock long being that it’s versatile, flattering, Markle-worn, and up to 55 percent off. So, if you want your own Excursion vest before this unbeatable deal wraps up (or sells out), I’d snag one sooner rather than later.
