    When the news broke that Hermes had designed a $12,900 basketball, it got us thinking... are there any sports we can't play with chic designer gear? After all, Chanel has created every piece of kit under the sun, Gucci design for equestrian champions and even Sir Paul Smith has designed a football. Here's our guide to the designers to go to for your favourite sport.

    Hermes

    There's no doubt about it, the Hermes basketball is the chicest of its kind. Using the same hand-stitching technique as for the legendary Birkin bag, the ball comes in cool blue calfskin and is guaranteed to up your style game on the court.
