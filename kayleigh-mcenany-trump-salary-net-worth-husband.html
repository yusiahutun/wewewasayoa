Who Is Kayleigh Mcenany?

Kayleigh Mcenany, age 31, is a new and fourth press secretary to President Donald Trump. She got appointed on 7 April 2020 as a press secretary.

Kayleigh comes from the media and Republican communications background. She has served as a national spokesperson to the Republican National Committee in 2017. Also, she was a CNN contributor and a writer as well with her books, including The New American Revolution: The Making of a Populist Movement.

She graduated from Georgetown University (2010) and Harvard University (law degree) before she joined Fox News.

How Much Is Kayleigh's Net Worth And Salary?

As President Trump's press secretary, Kayleigh Mcenany is set to garner an average salary of $179,700 per year. Apart from that, she has not made her net worth public to this date.

During the 2016 Presidential election, Kayleigh served as a paid CNN political commentator, from where she became the close personality to then-candidate Donald Trump. She defended Trump on CNN during his early days of the presidency.
