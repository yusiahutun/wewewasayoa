5SOS Release Room-Spinning Want You Back Video

 Posted on April 24, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  194 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

The James Larese-directed clip finds the four members (Luke Hemmings, Calum Hood, Michael Clifford and Ashton Irwin) each sequestered in tiny, color-washed rooms as the walls twist in around them, mimicking the disorienting feeling that often follows heartbreak.

"Want You Back," released in February, is the first to come from the Australian group's forthcoming third album, an as-yet-untitled LP due out later this year. They last released 2015's Sounds Good Feels Good, and spent the last year writing and recording their new project.

5SOS recently kicked off the European leg of their summer tour. They're next set to head stateside for a string of shows in April.
