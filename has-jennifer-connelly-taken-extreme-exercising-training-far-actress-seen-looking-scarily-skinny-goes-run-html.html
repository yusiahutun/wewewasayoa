    Has Jennifer Connelly taken her extreme exercising too far? Actress seen looking scarily skinny as she goes for a run

    Published: 14:05 EST, 31 August 2012 | Updated: 19:21 EST, 31 August 2012

    She has always been known for her slender frame.

    But Jennifer Connelly shocked onlookers yesterday when she went for a run in New York City.

    The 41-year-old displayed her pencil thin pins and tiny arms as she exuberantly pounded the pavement.

    Extreme: Jennifer Connelly shocked onlookers yesterday with her rail thin frame as she went for a run in New York City 

    The actress also appeared noticeably slimmer around the jawline, her face appearing pale and gaunt.

    Looking intent on burning the calories, the Beautiful Mind star jogged along with a pair of headphones on her head.

    Jennifer's tight-fitting black leggings particularly highlighted her legs, which have become so slim that her knees appear wider than her actual lower thigh.

    Big change: The actress's appearance is a far-cry different from her curvier look back in May 2007

    While always known for her slender frame, Jennifer has been looking increasingly slimmer over the past few months.

    She is known to be a fan of exercise, but unlike other keen fitness fanatics, such as Madonna and Cameron Diaz, she appears to continue to shrink in size whilst gaining not as much muscle tone.

    Jennifer started her career as a model, but she looks to be currently at her tiniest.

    Gaunt: The actress also appeared noticeably slimmer around the jawline, her face appearing pale and gaunt

    Jennifer is currently filming Darren Aronofsky-directed biblical epic Noah, which stars Emma Watson.

    And she has just finished work on the Josh Boone-directed Writers.

    Talking about the characters she chooses to portray recently the Labyrinth actress said: 'Sometimes they’re wonderful, sometimes they’re painful and sometimes right in between.

    Tiny pins: Jennifer's tight-fitting black leggings particularly highlighted her very slim legs

    'But there’s always something to be learned. And they wind up being meaningful.'

    The brunette star is also the mother of three children.

    She has two children with husband Paul Bettany, Agnes, 15 months, and eight-year-old son Stellan.

    She also has a son Kai, 14, from her relationship with photographer David Dugan.

    Jennifer, who married Bettany in 2003, has said she thinks her family is now complete.

    More exercise: Earlier this week Jennifer was seen taking a bike ride with her husband Paul Bettany and their two children

    'They never fight, they look after each other. They are loving,' she said.

    'Maybe it has something to do with the huge age gap between them, there is no competition and they all have so much attention and focus.'

    And she added: 'I think we should call it quits. It’s like trying to move an army getting out of the house and we go everywhere together, so it’s a lot of bodies to move.'

    Cute: Her 15-month-old daughter Agnes was strapped into a baby capsule at the back of her bike
