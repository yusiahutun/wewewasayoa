David Dangerfield On 90 Day Fiance: Before The 90 Days

David Dangerfield was the first deaf man to be featured on 90 Day Fiance: Before The 90 Days. The show saw him pursue Sheila, a single mother from Cebu, Philippines, who is also completely deaf. However, unlike David, Sheila was born hearing but began using hearing aids after she had “problems with her ears” at age six.

These lovebirds first met online through a Facebook group for deaf singles. However, as David mainly communicated in the American sign language, which Sheila was not well-versed in, the couple found it hard to communicate over video calls. Thus, they worked around this by talking only through texts in English.

Now, this may cause a major issue as their meet face to face. But then again, Sheila was trying her best to learn ASL, as they say, learning sign language is a cup of tea for hearing-impaired people. And with over 2 years to their name, Sheila might have already got the hang of it.

Prior to Sheila, David had been in a couple of relationships, the last of which ended due to infidelity. “Six years ago I fell in love with a deaf woman,” he explained. “I felt so much better not being alone. But we broke up because she cheated on me. It broke my heart.”

The woman who cheated on David was named Ashley Labs. He caught her red-handed with another man (who was hidden in her closet).

Reports had that David and Ashley were engaged since December 2009

Are David Dangerfield And Sheila Still Together?

David Dangerfield and Sheila kept their relationship status hidden as 90 Day Fiance was still on-air. But if you ask us, their unique relationship makes us believe they will be the couple least susceptible to drama and altercations.

Besides the ASL problem, their only concern was if Sheila’s son would connect with David or not.  Since David cannot speak and does not understand anything other than American Sign Language, it definitely was a challenge to communicate with him.

“What if my son cannot connect with David because he is deaf?” Sheila signed in the trailer.

But where there’s a will, there’s a way. David was already head over heels for the Philippines native.

“When I first saw Sheila’s picture, it got my heart beating. Sheila is better than any other woman I’ve dated because Sheila is a sweet person,” David said. “She is the perfect match for me. I’ll love Sheila forever.”

Also, he remarked, “I will have a new family with Sheila in the future.”
