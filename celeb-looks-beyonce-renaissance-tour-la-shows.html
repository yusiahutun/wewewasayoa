    28 Jul 2024, 00:00

    Beyoncé’s Renaissance Tour has arrived in Los Angeles, with stars flocking to the shows in their fabulous on-theme looks.

    For the past few days, celebs have showcased their finest silver looks all over social media, ranging from sparkling metallic dresses to surprising twists on the singer’s Virgo-season dress code. Scroll ahead to see a roundup of the most fabulous ensembles.

    Duchess Meghan and Prince Harry

    The couple attended Friday’s show together, alongside Meghan’s mom, Doria Ragland.

    In videos captured by a nearby fan, Meghan looked gorgeous in a white tank top tucked into a high-waisted silver sequined skirt by SPRWMN. Ragland coordinated with her daughter in a metallic sleeveless top and white pants. Meanwhile, Harry layered a gray blazer over a gray T-shirt and white slacks.

    Meghan returned to the arena for Bey’s birthday show on Monday, where she wore another dazzling silver outfit while posing for a photo with Kelly Rowland and Kerry Washington. This time, she paired a sleeveless black blouse with a different silver sequined skirt.

    Kevin Mazur//Getty Images

    Kelly Rowland

    Rowland supported her bestie in three stylish looks. For the first outfit, the singer paired a luxe mirrored breastplate top by Buerlangma with silver high-waisted pants and a metallic puffer coat. She added layers of diamond necklaces and an array of chunky rings.

    For night two, the star dressed in a mirrored crop top that revealed her glitter-covered abs, adding a pair of slightly oversize jeans and tons of diamond jewelry.

    To attend a third show, Rowland wore a glossy black strapless corset with black jeans. She accessorized the look with diamond-studded chain necklaces and matching facial jewelry.

    Gabrielle Union

    Union’s stunning look for the the Friday night show included a see-through chainmail dress with an attached hood by New Arrivals, which she wore over an underwire bikini top and matching metallic bottoms by Melissa Simone. She added silver Tiffany & Co. link earrings and stacks of bracelets while carrying a glittery Valentino bag.

    While attending another show, Union wore a crystal chandelier halter top with baggy acid-wash jeans, which featured large cutouts on the back of the pant legs. She accessorized with bedazzled jewelry and a black silver-studded bag.

    Meanwhile, husband Dwyane Wade looked suave in a white tank top with a yellow-and-blue 24 print (which may have been in honor of his friend, Kobe Bryant of the L.A. Lakers, who died in 2020), high-waisted black pants, white sneakers, and a black baseball cap. Wade’s 16-year-old daughter Zaya honored the theme in a black corset-and-shorts set paired with silver cowboy boots and a silver moon bag from Giarité. The couple’s four-year-old, Kaavia, looked cute in a velvet basketball jersey, a light-up tutu, sparkly black boots, and a mini version of her actor mother’s bag.

    Tracee Ellis Ross

    For Ross, the theme called for a fun micro-mini dress by Paco Rabanne, which she paired with black platform sandals. The actor showed off the outfit in several photos from the Friday night show, including snaps where the large circular sequins shone brightly in a dimmed room.

    “She said wear silver,” Ross captioned the post.

    The Kardashians

    Members of the Kardashian-Jenner clan attended Monday's show. Kim Kardashian appeared in a bedazzled crystal chain top designed by Swarovski creative director Giovanna Engelbert, distressed high-waist jeans, and a bedazzled purse. Khloé Kardashian also honored the silver dress code in a pair of glittery high-waist tights, which she styled with a cropped white tank top, a sparkling silver chain choker necklace, a metallic silver bag, and black sunglasses. Kris Jenner coordinated in a metallic trench coat layered on top of a gray ribbed shirt.

    (Kendall and Kylie Jenner—with boyfriend Timothée Chalamet—also attended Bey's birthday show, but photos of their full looks haven't been published just yet.)

    Kids North West and Penelope Disick also showed out in striking silver ensembles, with each wearing stretchy silver dresses from Diesel and jewelry by Swarovski.

    Kevin Mazur//Getty Images

    Kevin Mazur//Getty Images

    Earlier in the weekend, the Kardashian-Jenner matriarch shared a brief look at her concert outfit, which featured a black blazer embellished with an intricate silver sequin design.

    Hailey and Justin Bieber

    Joining the Kardashian-Jenner clan were the Biebers. Hailey and Justin were photographed at Bey's birthday show in the same section as their reality star friends (and Jeff Bezos). For the occasion, the Rhode founder wore a black crop top and matching miniskirt with silver jewelry, including large teardrop earrings, and shimmery silver makeup. Justin wore a gray hoodie and black pants.

    Kevin Mazur//Getty Images

    Keke Palmer

    Palmer focused the silver theme for her accessories and glam. The multi-hyphenate wore a black strapless bodycon dress by Sergio Hudson and mirrored ankle-strap sandals, adding a bedazzled black denim jacket which she wore as a shawl. She completed the look with a sparkling mirror-ball choker and large silver hoops, while bright silver highlights adorned her curly ’do. She topped off the look with shimmering silver eye shadow and a glossy nude lip.

    She attended a second show in a collared white button-up tucked into a metallic silver corset, which she styled with tight black pants. She accessorized with layers of silver bracelets and large silver hoop earrings.

    Issa Rae

    The Insecure star wore a crop top and miniskirt set made out of silver square-shaped reflective panels. She finished off the look with matching silver heels.

    Sofía Vergara

    The Modern Family actor danced the night away in a black tube top and a silver sequined maxi skirt.

    Elle Fanning

    Fanning attended the show in a monochromatic black ensemble by H&M. The outfit consisted of a lacy sheer camisole, black high-waist trousers, and a boxy black blazer. She finished off the look with metallic pumps and matching purse.

    Jessica Alba

    Alba dressed for the Renaissance Tour in a chic boxy black blazer and a large black cowboy hat, the latter adorned with hanging crystal trim.
