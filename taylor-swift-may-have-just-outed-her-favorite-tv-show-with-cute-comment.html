    Taylor Swift may have revealed her favorite comedy show. The singer broke her X/Twitter silence after congratulating a TV show and fans are loving their two worlds colliding.

    Pop music queen Taylor Swift‘s love for grippy TV dramas is well-known among fans. The singer’s appreciation for shows like Grey’s Anatomy and Law and Order: Special Victims Unit prompted the hitmaker to name two cats after her favorite characters from the shows.

    She also said previously how she identifies with the character Arya Stark on Game Of Thrones and fangirled over Killing Eve. Now in 2024, Swifties got a subtle recommendation of another TV show that they might want to add to their watchlist if they are not a fan of it already!

    Taylor Swift congratulates The Bear team after getting shout-out

    American TV show The Bear is having a grand start to the year, snagging major wins at multiple award shows. The comedy drama’s actor Ebon Moss-Bachrach thanked Taylor Swift in his Critics Choice Awards speech saying, “I’ve got to thank my two special scene partners, Olivia Colman and Taylor Swift.”

    He was referring to a scene where the celebrity was belting out the song Love Story Taylor’s Version on his way home. Later, Tay Tay found out about the sweet shoutout and left a comment saying ‘congratulations’ with hand-making heart emojis.

    “Taking this as confirmation that she watches The Bear”

    Taylor Swift’s tweet is taken by fans as a confirmation that she watches The Bear.

    One fan wrote on X (formerly Twitter): “Please tell me this means she’s seen The Bear.”

    Another fan posted: “The Bear Swifties stand up.”

    A third fan shared some wishful thinking: “Taylor about to be on The Bear next season and then be seen having dinner with the cast next week in New York.”
