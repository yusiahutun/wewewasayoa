    The internet's latest Barack Obama obsession? His unbuttoned shirt.

    We've previously seen him in the classic suit and tie (including that famous tan look), mom jeans, flip flops, leather jackets, backwards dad hats and even swim trunks and a life vest. But now that he's returned to the spotlight, the former POTUS is teetering between professional and casual with his no-tie, unbuttoned shirts, and he's been sporting the style frequently since leaving the White House.

    The latest iteration took place yesterday at the Seeds&Chips Global Food Innovation Summit, where he spoke about the dangers of climate change, which sent Twitter into an obsessed freakout.
