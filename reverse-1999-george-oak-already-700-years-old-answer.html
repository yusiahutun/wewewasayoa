In Reverse: 1999, there are puzzles in which you need to figure out the correct answer. For example, the “George the Oak is Already 700 Years” puzzle, which has quite a few players scratching their heads. This isn’t just because the answer itself is difficult to figure out, as there is another reason that makes the puzzle hard. Here is the answer to the Reverse 1999 George the Oak is Already 700 Years Old puzzle.

Reverse 1999 George the Oak is Already 700 Years Old Answer

Reverse 1999 George the Oak is Already 700 Years Old, Which Animals in the Tree Hole Listened to That Song With You?

The complete puzzle reads: “Unexpected Audience. George the Oak is already 700 years old. Which animals in the tree hole listened to that song with you?” As they say, a real puzzler. So, what is the correct answer that you are supposed to enter here? The answer is Red squirrels and woodpeckers. Note that these Reverse 1999 puzzles are case-sensitive, so you need to start the answer with a capital R. Otherwise, the answer won’t register. Also, make sure that you put and in the answer, and not the & sign. So simply tap the text box and then input this correct answer. Once you have answered it correctly, you will get x20 Clear Drops and x1 Tiny Acorn.
