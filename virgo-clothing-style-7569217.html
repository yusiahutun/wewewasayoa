    Letting your zodiac sign point you in the direction of new outfit ideas is hardly an exact science, but it is lots of fun. Especially when Lisa Stardust, fashion and astrology expert is guiding the way. Stardust knows her stuff, a fact sure to be appreciated by Virgo, the zodiac's meticulous planner. "Virgos are reflective and analytical when they're preparing their outfits," Stardust tells InStyle. "They put a lot of care and love into what they present to the world. They're always very well put together."

    To get an idea of the quintessential Virgo style, look no further than Queen Bey. "Beyoncé is our ultimate Virgo queen," says Stardust. From head to toe, she looks perfect." The level of attention Beyoncé paid to her Renaissance Tour looks is a great example of how Virgos approach fashion.

    This autumnal earth sign "put a lot of thought into who they're representing and how they're representing themselves," according to Stardust. Given that Beyoncé's custom looks often contain meaning beyond adhering to her opulent aesthetic, she really is a classic Virgo.

    What Is Virgo Clothing Style?

    When it comes to Virgo style in general, Stardust has the rundown. Virgos crave structure in all things, but their taste in fashion is especially illustrative of this fact. From sharp jackets to wide-leg bottoms, Virgos have an eye for shape and silhouette almost unmatched by the rest of the zodiac.

    This doesn't mean that you'll catch this earth sign exclusively rocking avant garde looks. According to Stardust, Virgos "like to wear basics, but stylized and perfected." Cohesion is also important to Virgos. "They put together a whole look and aesthetic, not just an outfit," Stardust explains meaning that, to this sign, accessories are key.

    While they aren't trend-chasers, they will incorporate trends into their own aesthetics, creating looks that feel fresh, but carry a quality of timelessness about them. Looking for more insights? Ahead, we've rounded up 16 ways to master dressing like a Virgo so good you'll feel destined to try them.

    Slip into a Skirt Set

    Analytical Virgos style themselves from head to toe. "When they're putting a look together, it all has to align," Stardust says. One hassle-free approach to Virgo style is to go with a matching skirt set.

    Pair Printed Separates

    If a full matching skirt set isn't quite your speed, try pairing printed pieces to create the cohesion that Virgos crave. A pair of jeans and a coordinated jacket are great for creating a fully polished look with a more casual air.

    Opt for Olive Green

    "Virgos don't shy away from color," says Stardust, "but they prefer neutrals and earth tones." When looking for Virgo colors, let your earth sign status guide you toward shades of green, clay, and stone. One trending fall shade that speaks to Virgos is olive oil green, a close match to the olive green spotted in the dress above. In the words of our astro-expert, "The Prada olive green slip dress is a very Virgo dress."

    Add Mary Janes

    The Mary Jane was given a high-fashion, flower-power update on the Etro runway and is perfect for this timeless but trend-aware sign. Stardust recommends Virgos, who have a preference for luxe accessories, opt for a black patent leather pair.

    Try a Mod Mini Skirt

    "An A-line mini skirt feels very Virgo, especially for fall," says Stardust, who recommends the earth sign style this 'fit with a tucked-in cardigan and chunky shoes.

    Opt for an Oversized Blazer

    Due in part to their love of structure, Virgos are likely to find themselves creating outfits with a focus on sharp silhouettes. As such, an oversized blazer is likely to become this sign's go-to fall jacket.

    Dive into Details

    Stardust characterizes Virgo as ye ole' "Kitten with a whip," meaning that they love to add a bit of sensuality to their 'fits — without getting into vulgar territory. Rather than going all-out in a bodycon dress, Stardust thinks Virgos prefer to express this side of their style through subtle details.

    Weatherproof a Slip Dress

    Another very Virgo outfit is a maxi slip dress layered over a turtleneck or long-sleeve shirt. The extra layer won't detract from the sleekness of your maxi, but will add a bit of timelessness and polished vibe beloved by Virgos.

    Try Sultry Suiting

    When it comes to a night out look, Stardust believes Virgos will love the structure and suggested, but not overt, sensuality of a blazer wore with a statement necklace instead of a shirt. Thank you as always, Jenna Lyons (who, for the record, is a Gemini.)

    Accessorize Your Outerwear

    "Virgos love little accents and detailing," says Stardust, who believes this sign will gravitate towards belted puffer coats come wintertime.

    Size Up Your Tote

    Go for a big tote or go home. "Virgos love big bags and they love tote bags," says Stardust, adding that Virgos like to carry around all of their essentials in a single, go-to bag. Bigger is always better when it comes to totes for this sign, who is also willing to spent a bit more money to ensure their accessories are luxe and long-lasting.

    Lean into Luxe Fabrics

    As an earth sign, Virgos favor ‘fits that feel as good as they look. Stardust also points out that the sign values quality over quantity and doesn't shy away from investment pieces. Keep this in mind when refining your Virgo style, and let yourself splurge on pieces in fabrics like silk, velvet, and cashmere.

    Pair Neutrals with Earth Tones

    As pointed out earlier, Virgos aren't afraid of color, but they believe it ought to be incorporated with intention. For a classic Virgo outfit, turn an earth-toned pair of pants into a statement piece by wearing with subtle, off-white neutrals.

    Wear Structured Separates

    Feed this earth sign's structure hunger pains by keeping appropriately structured separates on hand. Not only will interesting shapes delight your fashion sensibilities, but you can also organize your closet around shape and combine pieces you wouldn't normally think of as cohesive.

    Celebrate Your Curves

    While they don't share, say, Leo's confidence, Virgos won't say no to attention, according to Stardust. That said, comfort is also key. "They're not going to wear something that might make them feel self-conscious, but they do often enjoy showing off their bodies," she says.
