    Conleth Hill Career

    The Ireland-born actor Conleth Hill made his Broadway debut by appearing in Stones in His Pockets by Marie Jones.     Additionally, Conleth co-starred next to Geraldine McQueen in Peter Kay’s Britain’s Got The Pop Factor… and Possibly a New Celebrity Jesus Christ Soapstar Superstar Strictly on Ice. This show was a talent show program. Similarly, Conleth also appeared in the TV series Suits playing Edward Darby.

    Moreover, Hill’s most renowned role is in the HBO hit TV series Game Of Thrones which aired from 2011 and is currently airing its last season. Hill played the role of Lord Varys in the George R.R. Martin‘s novel series. Hill plays a major character in this series who should not be trusted by anyone.
