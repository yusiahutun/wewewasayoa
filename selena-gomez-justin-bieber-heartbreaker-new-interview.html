    Selena Gomez: Justin Bieber’s ‘Heartbreaker’ Is ‘Beautiful’

    In an interview with Buffalo, N.Y., station KISS 98.5, the interviewee asked Selena what she thought about “Heartbreaker” — and it turns out she thinks it’s beautiful:

    I think it’s beautiful music. Especially since he is super talented, able to use it and interpret feelings through music. It’s what you do to grow as an artist, that’s what I did and what many people do. It’s sweet.

    The lyrics in “Heartbreaker” are clearly about Selena. In fact, before the song’s release, Justin publicly revealed it’s a song about when he had his heart broken! It’s totally cool how Selena is so supportive of Justin expressing his emotions through his music.

    The interviewee went on to ask if there’s any hope of them getting back together, by asking what to “bet” on — back together, or staying separated — if he went to Las Vegas.

    “You should bet that I enjoy all the success that I have to be independent and single,” she said. “I know what I need to do with my life.”

    Justin Bieber Trying To Win Back Selena Gomez?

    Justin and Selena have been dating other people, but it seems like Justin is ready to give their relationship another shot — more so than Selena.

    “As much as Selena loves Justin, she just can’t put herself through more stress by committing herself to restarting their relationship,” a source told Hollywoodlife.com EXCLUSIVELY. “Their on-and-off relationship was “making her sick all the time.”

    We’re sure with time they’ll figure it out, but it’s great that they’ve remained friends and are so supportive of each other’s music!
