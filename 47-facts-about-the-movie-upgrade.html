    FAQs

    1. What is the genre of Upgrade?

    Upgrade is a combination of science fiction, action, and thriller genres.

    2. Who directed Upgrade?

    Upgrade was directed by Leigh Whannell, who is known for his work in the horror genre, particularly as the co-creator of the Saw and Insidious franchises.

    3. When was Upgrade released?

    Upgrade was released on June 1, 2018.

    4. What is the premise of Upgrade?

    The movie follows the story of Grey Trace, a technophobe who is paralyzed during a violent robbery. He is offered an experimental implant called STEM, which gives him extraordinary abilities and ultimately seeks revenge on those who harmed him.

    5. Who are the main actors in Upgrade?

    The lead role of Grey Trace is played by Logan Marshall-Green, and other notable cast members include Betty Gabriel, Harrison Gilbertson, and Simon Maiden (as the voice of STEM).

    6. Is Upgrade a standalone film or part of a series?

    Upgrade is a standalone film and does not have any direct sequels or prequels.

    7. What are some notable themes explored in Upgrade?

    Upgrade delves into themes such as the advancement of technology, the potential consequences of artificial intelligence, the ethics of human enhancement, and the nature of free will.

    8. How was the critical response to Upgrade?

    Upgrade received positive reviews from critics, who praised its unique blend of genres, innovative action sequences, and engaging storytelling.

    9. Does Upgrade have any awards or nominations?

    Upgrade was nominated for several awards, including the Saturn Award for Best Science Fiction Film and the Australian Academy of Cinema and Television Arts Award for Best Visual Effects.

    10. Is Upgrade suitable for all audiences?

    Upgrade is rated R for strong violence, grisly images, and language, so it may not be suitable for younger viewers or those sensitive to intense action sequences and graphic content.
