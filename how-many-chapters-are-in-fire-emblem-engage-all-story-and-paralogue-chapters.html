Fire Emblem Engage is here to make all old-school franchise fans feel nostalgic. From one of the first protagonists in the game’s history to the most recent heroes from Fire Emblem: Three Houses, FE Engage will be a story to remember. However, as you prepare to enter this world and engage with your favorite protagonists, you might wonder how long this experience will be. Here is how many chapters are in Fire Emblem Engage and a list of all story chapters and paralogues.

Main story chapters list for Fire Emblem Engage

There are 26 chapters for the main storyline in Fire Emblem Engage. There are also 15 additional paralogues you can play while doing the main quest.
