    One of the least featured yet most appreciated groups in Hollywood is the black community despite earlier barriers to entry. They have since been embraced into the art and ended up receiving good perks while at it. The highest-paid black actors are worth millions of dollars and take home huge sums every month.

    Over the past few years, a good number of black actors have been making waves in Hollywood. They are also paving the way for the young and up-coming African-American entertainers following in their footsteps. If you love Hollywood movies, then you probably have a favourite black actor.

    10 highest-paid black actors

    The list of highest-paid actors cuts across different movie genres and across time. While most of them are legendary, some are new entrants in the list whose works have earned them accolades.

    1. Tyler Perry - $800 million

    Who is the highest-paid black actor? Tyler Perry takes the lead. The 50-year-old American is a jack of many trades, working as a writer, producer, film director, and comedian. He is also applauded for being the first African American to open an all-African movie studio. He has an estimated net worth of $800 million as of 2021.

    2. Bill Cosby - $400 million

    Bill Cosby is an American stand-up comedian, actor, musician, and author. He landed a starring role in the television show I Spy, followed by his own sitcom The Bill Cosby Show, which ran for two seasons from 1969 to 1971.

    Even after The Cosby Show ended its run, its star and creator continued to rake in the money. The show earned an estimated $1.5 billion in syndication in the decades since it went off the air. He is still one of the richest black actors in 2021. His net worth is $400 million.

    3. Will Smith - $350 million

    Willard Carroll Smith Jr. is an American born rapper, producer, and actor. Some of the famous movies he has appeared in include Men in Black, Bad Boys, Hancock, I Am Legend, and The Pursuit of Happiness. He has grossed over $8 billion in sales since his acting debut. He is one of the young black male actors with an estimated net worth of $350 million as of 2021.

    4. Denzel Washington - $280 million

    Denzel is one of the top-earning black actors in Hollywood, having spent more than 35 years of his life in the showbiz industry. The 61-year-old is one of the best actors with awards decorating his life all around. He has won two academy awards, one Tony Award, Golden Globe Awards, and six Oscar nominations. He has an estimated net worth of $280 million as of 2021.

    5. Morgan Freeman - $250 million

    Morgan Freeman is an American actor, television producer, and director. He is another highest-paid black actor in Hollywood who have spent more than 50 years in the industry. He has won an Oscar Award and a Screen Guild Award. He is one of the top paid black actors with an estimated net worth of $250 million as of 2021.

    He has a combined total box office gross of $4.57 billion, with an average of $71.5 million per film. Freeman and others formed the Grenada Relief Fund to aid people affected by Hurricane Ivan on the island of Grenada in 2004.

    6. Samuel L. Jackson - $250 million

    Samuel Jackson is one of the highest-paid actors in Hollywood. The highly prolific entertainer and film producer is an icon having featured in more than 150 films. His films have an estimated gross of $27 billion boosted by movies like Goodfella and Jurassic Park. He is one of the richest black celebrities with an estimated net worth of $250 million as of 2021.

    5. Eddie Murphy - $200 million

    Eddy is one of the most acclaimed black comedians to grace the Hollywood showbiz industry. His films have grossed about $3.5 billion since his debut making him the sixth highest-grossing black actor in Hollywood. He has featured in numerous films including Vampire in Brooklyn, Coming to America, and The Nutty Professor films. He has an estimated net worth of $200 million as of 2021.

    8. Ice Cube - $160 million

    Ice Cube is an American actor, comedian, and rapper. He is one of the richest black actors. His rise to fame started as a rapper and later joined acting under different roles. Most of his wealth is associated with comedy but has also been a well-known film actor earning a sizeable amount for his work. He has an estimated net worth of $160 million as of 2021.

    9. Jamie Foxx - $150 million

    Jammie Fox is a celebrated black American actor, singer, producer, and comedian. Apart from this, he is also an award-winning artist. He won the Academy Award from his role Ray Charles in the 2004 biographical film Ray, BAFTA, Critics’ Choice Movie Award, and the Screen Guild Award. He is among the richest Hollywood black actors with an estimated net worth of $150 million as of 2021.

    10. Martin Lawrence - $110 million

    Martin Lawrence is a German-born black American actor. His net worth is a cumulative sum of the money he earned during his early days as a film, stand-up comedian, scriptwriting, boxing talk show hosting, and movie directing. He has an estimated net worth of $110 million as of 2021.
