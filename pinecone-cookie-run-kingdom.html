Pinecone Cookie Toppings in Cookie Run Kingdom

 Posted on September 27, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  460 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

In our Pinecone Cookie Toppings in Cookie Run Kingdom guide, we’ll discuss the last character that the developers will be adding in 2022 and how to build it. It’s fitting that a fir-themed cookie would close the year out. Of course, there are more things to look forward to during the winter holidays. However, the squirrel-tailed cookie is our main focus here, so let’s jump right in.

Best Toppings for Pinecone Cookie in Cookie Run Kingdom

The best choice of toppings for Pinecone Cookie in Cookie Run Kingdom, in our opinion, are five Swift Chocolate. This is because their special skill, Pinecone Bomb, is incredibly useful, and you want to be able to use it as many times as possible. Basically, they heal themselves and two allies with the highest ATK, and they increase their own Max HP. Then, Pinecone starts throwing bombs on enemies, stunning them and dealing AoE damage.

Now, you can go with a combination of three Swift Chocolate and two Solid Almond, if you want to make Pinecone more sturdy. However, I don’t think that’s necessary. The healing Pinecone Bomb does should be enough to keep them around just fine. The decision is up to you, of course.

Cookie Run Kingdom Pinecone Cookie

Pinecone Cookie in Cookie Run Kingdom has been announced in a recent tweet and YouTube video, as I’m sure those of you following the game’s socials have already seen. According to those two sources, the new character is going to come out on December 15th, which presumably means US time. If I’m correct in that assumption, then it’s going to be December 16th in Japan and Korea, and maybe even eastern Europe. We don’t know the exact launch times; if and when the developers reveal them, we’ll be sure to update this guide. Plus, we’ll add a separate section on what the best toppings for Pinecone Cookie are once we have more info, so stay tuned.

That’s not quite everything we know about Pinecone Cookie in Cookie Run Kingdom at the moment. We also know that they are an Epic Cookie, that their type is Bomber, and that they stand in the front row of the party. We can’t really figure out what the best toppings would be from that alone; we need to at least know what the special skill is going to be. Either way, it sounds like Pinecone is going to be an AOE damage dealer kind of thing. We’ll have to wait and see, of course. One thing I do know is that the design of the cookie is adorable, what with the squirrel tail and whatnot. Not to mention the fact that they can apparently summon a fir tree that they then ride into battle.
