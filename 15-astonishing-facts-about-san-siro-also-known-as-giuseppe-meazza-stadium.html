    FAQs

    1. How old is San Siro Stadium?
    San Siro Stadium was officially inaugurated on September 19, 1926, making it over 95 years old.

    2. Which teams play at San Siro Stadium?
    San Siro Stadium is the home ground for two of Italy’s most successful football clubs, AC Milan and Inter Milan. Both teams share the stadium for their home matches.

    3. What is the seating capacity of San Siro Stadium?
    The seating capacity of San Siro Stadium is approximately 80,018. However, for certain events, it can accommodate up to 82,955 spectators.

    4. Is it possible to take a guided tour of San Siro Stadium?
    Yes, guided tours of San Siro Stadium are available for visitors. These tours provide an opportunity to explore the stadium grounds, visit the museum, and even access areas typically restricted to the public, such as the dressing rooms.

    5. Are there any other attractions near San Siro Stadium?
    Yes, in close proximity to the stadium, you can find the San Siro Racecourse and the San Siro district, which offers various entertainment options, including restaurants, bars, and shopping outlets.

    6. Can I attend a football match at San Siro Stadium?
    Yes, it is possible to attend football matches at San Siro Stadium. Tickets can be purchased in advance through the official websites of AC Milan and Inter Milan, as well as through authorized ticket vendors.

    7. Does San Siro Stadium host other events besides football matches?
    Yes, in addition to football matches, San Siro Stadium has also hosted major music concerts, international rugby matches, and even the opening ceremony of the 2009 UEFA Champions League final.
