    1. Early life

    Nicki Minaj was born in the Saint James District of Port of Spain and raised in Queens, New York.

    Her real name is Onika Tanya Maraj-Petty.

    She gained public recognition in the US after she dropped the mixtapes Playtime Is Over in 2007, Sucka Free in 2008, and Beam Me Up Scotty in 2009.

    2. Debut album

    Nicki dropped her debut studio album titled Pink Friday in 2010 after Lil Wayne signed her to his Young Money Entertainment in 2009.

    Outside of rap music, Nicki's film career included voice roles in the animated films Ice Age: Continental Drift which premiered in 2012, and The Angry Birds Movie 2 in 2019.

    The stunner has also bagged supporting roles in the comedy films The Other Woman in 2014 and Barbershop: The Next Cut which was released in 2016.

    4. Accolades and nominations

    The gorgeous rapper has won numerous awards since she became a professional recording artist.

    They include six American Music Awards, twelve BET Awards, four MTV Video Music Awards, four Billboard Music Awards, and two Billboard Women in Music Awards.

    Nicki has also been nominated for 10 Grammy Awards.

    5. Business ventures

    She has a prominent line of fragrances that were first launched in September 2012.

    She partnered with 'Give Back Brands' to introduce her first fragrance called Pink Friday.

    The fragrance was nominated for three 2013 FiFi Awards for Fragrance of the Year, Best Packaging, and Media Campaign of the Year.

    6. Family

    In December 2018, Nicki began dating her childhood friend Kenneth "Zoo" Petty and they filed for a marriage licence in August 2019.

    The Your Love hitmaker announced that they had officially married on October 21.

    In July 2020, she announced on Instagram that she was expecting her first bundle of joy and gave birth to a son on September 30, 2020.
