    Flight of the Phoenix production and reception

    The filming of this movie began on 26th April 1965 at the 20th Century-Fox Ranch and Century-Fox Studios in California, USA. The locations that simulated the desert were Pilot Knob Mesa and Buttercup Valley in the Algodones Dunes, both in California.

    What aircraft were used? Several aircraft were used during the filming, including:

    • Flying shots - Fairchild C-82A Packet, N6887C
    • Outdoor location wreck - Fairchild C-82A Packet, N4833V
    • Indoor studio wreck - Fairchild C-82A Packet, N53228
    • Non-flying Phoenix prop - Fairchild R4Q-1 Flying Boxcar
    • Flying Phoenix aircraft - Tallmantz Phoenix P-1, N93082
    • Second flying Phoenix - North American O-47A, N4725V
