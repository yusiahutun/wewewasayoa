    Best Wide-Leg Jean

    Reformation Cary High-Rise Slouchy Wide-Leg Jeans

    Best Wide-Leg Jean

    Reformation Cary High-Rise Slouchy Wide-Leg Jeans

    Credit: Reformation

    “These are the kind of jeans you buy and then proceed to wear every single day after. I ordered two sizes up for an extra baggy feel, which is how I prefer to wear my denim in the fall. The slouchy look and wide-leg silhouette make these jeans such a great shape to play with, when it comes to layering. Basically they’re like the perfect middleman between a big snug sweater and tiny little sexy slingback heels." —Tara Gonzalez, senior fashion editor

    Best ’90s Jean

    Agolde ’90s Mid-Rise Straight Organic Jeans

    Best ’90s Jean

    Agolde ’90s Mid-Rise Straight Organic Jeans

    “I will be wearing these straight-leg jeans all season. There’s something so flattering about a mid-rise: It’s high enough to comfortably cinch at the perfect place on your waist, and also looks flattering with a T-shirt or thin knit tucked in and pulled out slightly with a belt. They’re also great with an oversize button-down shirt or flowy sweater over top. These hit the ankles at the ideal spot to wear cool Mary Janes, flats, and Chelsea boots when it’s colder.” —Jaclyn Alexandra Cohen, senior fashion and accessories editor

    “The first time I wore these Agolde jeans and posted a photo in them, my friends all assumed they were custom or that I had gotten them tailored. In reality, they just fit that perfectly. The mid-rise isn’t too high, and the silhouette isn’t too snug—these are a tried-and-true straight-leg that I’m positive I’ll be wearing on repeat through the rest of the year.” —Bianca Betancourt, culture editor

    Sizes: 23–32

    Wash: Mid denim

    Best Slim-Fit Jean

    A.P.C. Petit Standard Jean

    Best Slim-Fit Jean

    A.P.C. Petit Standard Jean

    “These are the jeans that you marry! That is because owning them takes a lifetime commitment. In return, they will be the only jean you’ll ever want to wear.

    “I love the way they break in, form to your body, and record in their deep blues the life you’ve lived in them. To this extent I never really wash them. When they need a reboot, I put them in the freezer or with a dryer sheet on high in the dryer. (If that’s too extreme, I also recommend rinsing them in cold water with dark Woolite, then hanging to dry.) The slimness and modern cut look great with anything. For that Left Bank Parisian look, pair it with a sweater, tweedy blazer, and a brown loafer. C’est chic!" —Miguel Enamorado, accessories director

    Sizes: 24–36

    Wash: Indigo

    Best Curvy Straight-Leg Jean

    Asket The Standard Jeans

    Best Curvy Straight-Leg Jean

    Asket The Standard Jeans

    Credit: Asket

    “Asket made me a convert to straight-leg jeans—this pair specifically. Before I found them, I was more or less convinced that tapered styles didn’t work with my curvier legs. Turns out, I just wasn’t wearing ones with Asket’s perfectly slim-but-not-too-slim leg, comfortably proportioned hips, and a little hit of stretch. You’ll see me wearing them throughout the fall with a faux-fur-lined aviator jacket, sweater, and loafers, then in the spring with a structured poplin top like Tove’s.” —Halie LeSavage, fashion commerce editor

    Sizes: 23–34

    Washes: Stone wash, stone bleach

    Best Curvy Wide-Leg Jean

    Madewell The Curvy Perfect Vintage Wide-Leg Jean

    “I’ve been wearing Madewell’s curvy-fit jeans for years now, and they’ve never let me down. They also hit at just the right length for my favorite satin ballet flats to peek out from underneath the hem.” —Halie LeSavage, fashion commerce editor
