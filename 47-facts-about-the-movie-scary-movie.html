    Scary Movie was released in 2000.

    Directed by Keenen Ivory Wayans, Scary Movie is a comedy horror film that parodies popular horror movies.

    The movie’s original title was “Last Summer I Screamed Because Halloween Fell on Friday the 13th”.

    The title was later changed to Scary Movie to better reflect the satirical nature of the film.

    Scary Movie was a box office success, grossing over $278 million worldwide.

    The film’s low budget and clever marketing campaign contributed to its financial success.

    It was the first film in the Scary Movie franchise.

    The success of the first film led to the creation of several sequels.

    The Wayans family, known for their comedy, played a major role in the production of the movie.

    Keenen Ivory Wayans directed the film, while his brothers Shawn and Marlon Wayans starred in it.

    Scary Movie parodies multiple horror films including Scream, I Know What You Did Last Summer, and The Blair Witch Project.

    The film’s humor comes from its clever satirical take on popular horror movie tropes.

    Scary Movie was initially rated NC-17, but edits were made to secure an R-rating.

    The movie features an iconic opening scene parodying the popular Scream franchise.

    FAQs

    Q: When was Scary Movie released?

    A: Scary Movie was released on July 7, 2000.

    Q: Who directed Scary Movie?

    A: The film was directed by Keenen Ivory Wayans.

    Q: Is Scary Movie based on a true story?

    A: No, Scary Movie is not based on a true story. It is a parody of various popular horror films.

    Q: Who stars in Scary Movie?

    A: Anna Faris, Shawn Wayans, Marlon Wayans, and Regina Hall are among the main cast members of Scary Movie.

    Q: Are there any sequels to Scary Movie?

    A: Yes, Scary Movie spawned several sequels, including Scary Movie 2, Scary Movie 3, Scary Movie 4, and Scary Movie 5.

    Q: What other movies are parodied in Scary Movie?

    A: Scary Movie parodies multiple horror films, including Scream, The Blair Witch Project, I Know What You Did Last Summer, and The Sixth Sense, among others.

    Q: Is Scary Movie suitable for all ages?

    A: Scary Movie is rated R for strong crude sexual humor, language, drug use, and violence. It is recommended for mature audiences.

    Q: What is the runtime of Scary Movie?

    A: The runtime of Scary Movie is approximately 88 minutes.
