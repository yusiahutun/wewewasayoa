    Jennifer Lopez is looking back on her recent Mediterranean getaway.

    In her latest Instagram post, the multi-hyphenate shared a series of photos taken during the first few weeks of August, which she spent traveling in Italy with husband Ben Affleck and their blended family. The star showed off a streak of luxurious ensembles, including some dramatic, colorful dresses.

    In two poolside images, the Hustlers actor wears a white string bikini, consisting of a triangle top and matching bottoms with large ties at the sides. For another sun-kissed pic, she pairs a tiny red string bikini with a flowing maxi-length cape printed with an orange floral pattern on a navy background. She added metallic silver flip flops and a brown fedora with a green sash, while carrying a small raffia tote.

    For one evening look, the star poses in a loose-fitting chartreuse gown with long balloon sleeves, paired with a matching Valentino clutch. She accessorized the glowing monochromatic look with an array of gold jewelry, including small hoops, a circular pendant necklace, and stacks of bangles.

    Lopez also kept up the colorful vibe in another evening photo, where she perches on a balcony by the sea while dressed in a long-sleeved gown with a swirling multicolored design. The piece featured a high neckline and a thigh-high slit, which revealed the star's towering metallic gold platform heels. She added gold dangling earrings and a cuff bracelet, while wearing her hair pulled back and some glamorous curtain bangs.
