    1. Sheldon J Plankton

    Who is the biggest SpongeBob villain? Sheldon Plankton is SpongeBob’s biggest enemy and arch-nemesis. He is constantly devising new ways to hurt and trick SpongeBob. When they were younger, Mr Krabs betrayed him, and this has been fueling his anger all the years.

    Plankton is on a mission to steal the Krabby Patty recipe, which is the reason behind Mr Krabs' successful restaurant and complete failure of his. However, Sheldon's desire for vengeance eventually grows into the desire to dominate the world. Plankton's evil quest is prone to becoming successful thanks to his abnormally high IQ.

    5. Man Ray

    Who is Man Ray in SpongeBob? Man Ray is one of the evilest villains in SpongeBob. He is another great enemy of Barnacle Boys and Mermaid Man. He is cunningly evil because he doesn't use brute force but is intelligently manipulative.

    He has a wide range of weapons even though he doesn’t necessarily need them because manipulation works well. In addition, even though his biggest asset is his relationship with other villains, he has unique abilities such as:
