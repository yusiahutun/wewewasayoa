    Emily Ratajkowski Parties in a White Tube Top and Rainbow Pants

    Emily Ratajkowski is ringing in her 32nd birthday in style. Last night, the model and writer was seen out and about in New York City, where she was accompanied by friends to celebrate the milestone occasion.

    15 Feb 2024, 00:00

    Emily Ratajkowski is ringing in her 32nd birthday in style.

    Last night, the model and writer was seen out and about in New York City, where she was accompanied by friends to celebrate the milestone occasion.

    For the outing, Ratajkowski looked cool in a white cropped tube top and a pair of vintage low-rise pants from Dolce & Gabbana. The trousers consisted of a vertically striped rainbow pattern made up of patchwork leather and suede panels. She finished off the look with metallic gold Jimmy Choo heels and a glittering silver chainmail bag from Paco Rabanne. She further accessorized with a yellow gemstone pendant necklace and a few rings.

    Gotham//Getty Images

    Gotham//Getty Images

    This past weekend, the My Body author also headed out in New York City in a showstopping outfit. Then, she wore a sheer Burberry slipdress—featuring the brand’s iconic black, tan, and red plaid pattern—with an asymmetrical hem and black lace trim. She finished off the outfit with a pair of black cowboy boots, a caramel leather shoulder bag from the Row, oval-shaped sunglasses, and an array of gold jewelry, including large hoops and a nameplate necklace.

    Gotham//Getty Images

    And last week, Ratajkowski cut a casual figure with another off-duty look that was comprised of a body-sculpting vest shirt in black, which featured a peplum silhouette, hook-and-eye buttons down the center, and a plunging neckline. She coordinated the look with mid-rise straight-leg trousers in a khaki shade and a pair of white Superga sneakers. To accessorize, Ratajkowski wore wiry gold-frame eyeglasses, a black cord choker necklace prominently featuring a large circular pendant in amber, and the same bag from the Row.
