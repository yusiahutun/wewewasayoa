    For weeks, we’ve been watching Hailey Bieber apply a tinted lip balm, teasing us about upcoming launches for her brand, Rhode. Finally, the Peptide Lip Tints are here! There are four fragrance-free shades to choose from: Ribbon (a sheer pink), Raspberry Jelly (a crushed berry), Toast (a rose taupe), and Espresso (a rich brown).

    The sheer but buildable tints hydrate and moisturize with five essential fatty acids and vitamins E, D, A, and allantoin. Shea butter, peptides, cupuaçu, and babassu give lips that cushiony feel that makes you want to apply it throughout the day. If you’re already a fan of the Peptide Lip Treatment and its five flavors (including unscented), you know how delicious they feel. And now, you don’t need to add a lip liner for a little color.

    If you grab all four, you save $6 and get free shipping so it’s basically a no-brainer (girl math).

    Fans and famous friends are freaking out about the new launch. “How many Rhode peptide lip treatments can one have 😍,” wrote one fan on TikTok. “I’ve been waiting for this one,” said TikToker Kensington. “NEED,” wrote fellow beauty girl Meredith Duxbury.

    Over on Instagram, customers are thanking Bieber for making enough stock that, with a little patience, they could get as many lippies as they wanted. “Thank you so much for having enough stock to sustain orders from the new countries added. Seems like a lot of ppl were successfully in snagging some💜” they wrote. “Felt like I was buying concert tickets lol,” said another customer who jumped online at the exact launch time to grab the bundle of four.

    As of right now, there are still shades in stock so if you want to try them, head over to the Rhode Skin website now.
