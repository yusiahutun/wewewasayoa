Miles Teller Buys Pacific Palisades Home for $7.5 Million Photos

 Posted on January 9, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  377 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

As he continues to celebrate the success of Top Gun: Maverick this past year, Miles Teller and his wife Keleigh just purchased their new home, a gorgeous Cape Cod-style mansion in the Pacific Palisades for $7.5 million, Dirt reports.

Related story Winona Ryder Is Allegedly Transitioning Into This Career Path to ‘Grow Her Fortune’

The home, which has five bedrooms and seven bathrooms and spans 6,600 square feet, was built in 2015 so it’s ultra-modern and new. Among its many features, the main level includes an open-concept great room that joins a fireside living room, a dining room, and a high-end gourmet kitchen with a breakfast nook and eat-in island all in one.

On the top floor, there are four ensuite bedrooms including a master bedroom with a private ocean-view balcony, a walk-in closet and a bathroom with dual vanities, a soaking tub and a steam shower. On the bottom floor, however, is where the property really lights up. In addition to an ultra-cozy theater room, where the Tellers can rewatch Top Gun or Whiplash all day long, there’s also a family room with a fireplace, an office, a bonus room for them to customize and a gym.

Outdoors, the grassy backyard has the potential to build a brand new pool and there’s also a roof deck with stunning views of the surrounding hills and Santa Monica Mountains.
