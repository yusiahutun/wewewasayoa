    People often say my style choices reflect that of Meghan Markle, and honestly, it’s such a compliment. There’s something about her chic style that errs on the side of stealth wealth and minimalism, and I’m all about it. But I’m specifically obsessed with one of her most recent looks. 

    Markle attended the 2023 Invictus Games wearing post-Labor Day whites balanced by a black top and Chanel cap-toe flats. But it was her two-toned sweater that caught my attention. Perhaps it’s because I own a very similar style (am I doing the Markle-style inspo right?) or because her exact cardigan is available to shop at J.Crew right now. 

    The cardigan in question is J.Crew’s Giselle V-Neck Sweater Blazer, which is made of a cozy cotton blend including spacious pockets, gold details, and a contrasting design. The separate features a relaxed fit while still looking polished and professional. While a good amount of sizes are still in stock, plenty have already been wiped off of the virtual shelves. So, if you want to snag Markle’s exact sweater, now’s the time to do so. 

    J.Crew Giselle V-Neck Sweater Blazer   

    But there are other great lookalikes that will help you achieve Markle’s style just as well, such as English Factory's Navy Contrast Cardigan or Verdusa’s Button-Front Knitted Cardigan. The key attribute is in the dual-toned design, offering a dark counterpart to the stark white base. Together, the two elements craft a timeless print that never goes out of style. 

    English Factory Contrast Cardigan

    Verdusa Button-Front Knitted Cardigan

    Not to mention, this look is incredibly practical. I would know, as I basically never leave my house without my own version from Lilysilk. Take this Realipopo V-Neck Knit Coat or L’agence Elin Four-Pocket Cardigan for example. Store either one in your bag to throw on when the office gets chilly, or layer your pick over a long-sleeve shirt during the fall. You can even wear your choice sweater around the house, whether you’re snuggling up on the couch or jumping on a Zoom call. 

    Realipopo V-Neck Knit Coat

    L’agence Elin Four-Pocket Cardigan

    Perhaps my favorite part or Markle’s two-tone sweater, though, is its versatility and ease. Wear it with jeans and a coordinating tank for the ultimate uncomplicated ensemble, or layer it over an LBD for a night out. On the other hand, you can make it perfect for fall with tights and knee-high boots.
