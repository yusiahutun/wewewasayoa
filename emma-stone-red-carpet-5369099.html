    When Emma Stone hit the Hollywood scene in 2007's Superbad, she quickly had us enamored by her witty charm and quirky good looks. The adorable redhead went on to star in hits like the fan-favorite romantic comedy Crazy, Stupid, Love, the Golden Globe-winning Hollywood love story La La Land, and, of course, the 2021 live-action retelling of Cruella. Her characters have always had a unique style as well. I mean, who could forget the lacy lingerie she sported in Easy A or the gothic undertones in her version of Cruella de Vil?

    Not only does Stone nail it on the big screen with her costumes but also with her wardrobe IRL. When it comes to her red carpet repertoire, Stone has proven that she knows how to find the perfect balance of adorable, fun, and edgy. We've seen her rock styles like mod-inspired miniskirts to tailored pantsuits in bold prints, statement-making textures, and silhouettes. From one of her earlier red carpet appearances in an elegant nude Prada gown to being added to Louis Vuitton's roster of celebrity ambassadors in 2017, we love the actress's super refined style.

    Ahead, we've rounded up Emma Stone's top red carpet looks.

    Emma Stone in a Louis Vuitton ensemble at the Polo Classic in New Jersey in 2023

    The actress opted for a preppy polo look perfect for the Veuve Clicquot Polo Classic in 2023. The ensemble was comprised of a soft yellow striped knit top and a fitted A-line skirt, both Louis Vuitton.

    Emma Stone in a Louis Vuitton dress at the Met Gala in 2022

    Stone took "reduce, recycle, reuse" to a fashionable level for the 2022 Met Gala, choosing to re-wear a Louis Vuitton dress from her wedding weekend circa 2020. The romantic feathered hem made for a perfect multipurpose look.

    Emma Stone in a Louis Vuitton suit at the 'Cruella' Los Angeles premiere in 2021

    For the 2021 premiere of the fashion-focused film Cruella, Stone channeled her inner de Vil with a tailored suit featuring gold-and-silver hardware, a white pussy bow blouse, and a red Louis Vuitton clutch.

    Emma Stone in a Fendi dress at the BAFTA Tea Party in Beverly Hills in 2019

    Emma Stone in a Louis Vuitton gown at the Golden Globes in 2019

    For the 2019 Golden Globe Awards, the actress wore a custom-made Louis Vuitton blush-colored gown, complete with embroidered diamonds. She kept the LV theme going with her handbag and jewelry from the brand's High Jewelry Collection.

    Emma Stone in a Louis Vuitton suit at the SAG Awards in 2019

    An asymmetric pantsuit is unexpected in any context, but Stone managed to make this work so effortlessly. Celebrating the 2019 SAG Awards, the actress wore a shimmering gold one-shoulder top with perfectly tailored pants.

    Emma Stone in a Louis Vuitton gown at the Oscars in 2019

    Emma Stone in a Louis Vuitton jumpsuit at the Met Gala in 2019

    For the 2019 Met Gala theme "Camp: Notes on Fashion," Stone wore an avant-garde silver foil Louis Vuitton jumpsuit with an asymmetric oversize lapel.

    Emma Stone in a Givenchy gown at the 'Maniac' New York City premiere in 2018

    Emma Stone in a Louis Vuitton gown at the at 'The Favourite' U.K. premiere in 2018

    At the 2018 BFI London Film Festival, Stone opted for another Louis Vuitton number, taking on a classic silhouette in the silver hue with long sleeves and a sophisticated boat neck.

    Emma Stone in a Louis Vuitton dress at BFI London Film Festival in 2017

    The Battle of the Sexes star looked radiant in a white floral Louis Vuitton dress with cutout shoulders at the 2017 BFI London Film Festival.

    Emma Stone in a Givenchy jumpsuit at the 'Killing of a Sacred Deer' U.K. premiere in 2017

    Stone went for drama in a black Givenchy halter jumpsuit covered in bows at the 2017 BFI London Film Festival premiere of Killing of a Sacred Deer.

    Emma Stone in a Givenchy Haute Couture gown at the Oscars in 2017

    The Oscar-winning actress's custom Givenchy gown for the Academy Awards took a whopping 1,700 hours to create. The gold embroidery and fringe skirt had her looking like an Oscar statue herself.

    Emma Stone in an Alexander McQueen gown at the SAG Awards in 2017

    Stone attended the SAG Awards in a half-sheer black floral Alexander McQueen number. The peek-a-boo lacy bustier on one side added a sultry touch to this romantic gown.

    Emma Stone in a Valentino gown at the Golden Globes in 2017

    What better way to embody star power? The actress wore a dreamy star-covered Valentino gown with a plunging neckline at the 2017 Golden Globes. 

    Emma Stone in a Roland Mouret gown at Critics' Choice Awards in 2016

    Stone bared her midriff in a halter-style Roland Mouret gown at the 2016 Critics' Choice Awards. The look featured a shimmery top half with a matte black skirt..

    Emma Stone in a Rodarte dress at Mill Valley Film Festival in 2016

    The actress stunned in a lace Rodarte number with heart detailing on the chest at the Mill Valley Film Festival premiere of her equally romantic movie, La La Land.

    Emma Stone in a Chanel dress at the 'La La Land' L.A. premiere in 2016

    Stone opted for a yellow Chanel dress paired with pointy ankle-strap pumps at the L.A. premiere of her much-anticipated film La La Land. She left her auburn tresses down, styling her lob in loose waves.

    Emma Stone in an Atelier Versace gown at the Venice Film Festival in 2016

    The redhead stunned at the Venice Film Festival premiere of the 2016 film La La Land in a glittery, silver-fringed, custom Atelier Versace gown and matching strappy sandals.

    Emma Stone in a corseted Prada dress at the Met Gala in 2016

    Stone paired her new brunette strands with a plunging white Prada gown and lace-up corset, perfectly matching the Met Gala's theme of "Fashion in an Age of Technology."

    Emma Stone in a Lanvin pantsuit at the Golden Globes in 2015

    "If anyone could pull off wearing pants to the Golden Globes, it's Emma," said her stylist at the time, Petra Flannery, who considers this custom Lanvin jumpsuit a risk that paid off. "Everything from the hand-beaded bodice to the taffeta bow sash was perfect."

    Emma Stone in a Dior Haute Couture dress at the SAG Awards in 2015

    At the 2015 SAG Awards, Stone combined her silk chiffon Dior Haute Couture dress with a tuxedo jacket, a cherry pout (Revlon Ultra HD's Gladiolus shade), and diamond ear cuffs by Repossi.

    Emma Stone in a Christian Dior dress at the 'Saturday Night Live' anniversary event in 2015

    The actress attended the Saturday Night Live 40th anniversary celebration in N.Y.C., wearing a sparking Christian Dior gown paired with black Christian Louboutin heels.

    Emma Stone in an Oscar de la Renta gown at the 'Irrational Man' photocall in Cannes in 2015

    Stone attended a photocall for Irrational Man during the Cannes Film Festival wearing a black lace dress with a skirt made of frothy organza ruffles from Oscar de la Renta's pre-fall 2015 collection.

    Emma Stone in an Atelier Versace gown at 'The Amazing Spider-Man 2 London' premiere in 2014

    When Stone first laid eyes on this Versace gown, she had an "aha!" fashion moment. 

    "Emma had never really worn yellow before, and she was in love with this," said Flannery. 

    The $23,000 emerald and gold David Webb necklace was a last-minute addition. 

    "Emma doesn't like to drown herself in diamonds."

    Emma Stone in a Valentino Haute Couture gown at the Venice Film Festival in 2014

    A sophisticated tulle Valentino gown was ideal for the Venice Film Festival, especially on a night when Stone's buzzy performance in Birdman and her freshly cut bob guaranteed headlines.

    Emma Stone in a Thakoon gown at the Met Gala in 2014

    "This is sporty and young, but there's an elegance to it," said Thakoon designer Thakoon Panichgul. "Emma looked spectacular."

    Emma Stone in a Gucci dress at 'The Croods' Berlin premiere in 2013

    "We often do something different when we go abroad," said Flannery of the Arizona native's silk Gucci number at the Berlin premiere of The Croods. "This has a '60s vibe."

    Emma Stone in a Valentino dress at an 'Elle' Women in Hollywood event in 2012

    For the annual Elle Women in Hollywood Celebration, Stone opted for a Barbie pink Valentino dress with sheer sleeve details and a twee style button-up peeking out of the collar. She paired the look with a mini Valentino clutch and houndstooth Ferragamo pumps.

    Emma Stone in a Calvin Klein gown at the Golden Globes in 2011

    The star first grabbed the fashion world's attention in this Calvin Klein Collection column gown. Creative director Francisco Costa said of the look, "The bright nectar color matched her vibrant personality."
