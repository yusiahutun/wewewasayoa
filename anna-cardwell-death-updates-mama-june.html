    Anna died surrounded by family

    Anna Cardwell died surrounded by her family, according to social media posts by the family.

    The 29-year-old passed away in Mama June's home on Saturday night.

    Family shared last group photo

    On December 9, June, Mama June's husband Justin Stroud, and Anna's sisters revealed the heartbreaking news that she had lost her battle with cancer.

    The family members shared the same group photo of the family sitting on what appeared to be the set of the TLC show.

    That was the last group photo the family would ever take.

    Anna opened up before her death, part two

    Anna went on to reveal that her most recent miscarriage occurred two days before Christmas, and she caused her to need a D&C.

    According to the Mayo Clinic, a D&C is a "procedure to remove tissue from inside your uterus."

    Regarding the D&C, the TLC star said: “It felt like I was giving birth, it was the worst pain of my life. It’s a really painful process, so I’m like you know let's give it one more try. "

    During the ordeal, she discovered that she wasn't making enough progesterone which was "keeping the baby from getting enough nutrition."

    This discovery came before her cancer diagnosis, and at the time she was determined to keep trying for her rainbow baby.  

    She said: “We know what's going on now, and we are taking the right precautions to take care of it and make sure it stays alive and everything like that.” 

    Anna opened up before her death

    In 2021, Anna, also known as Chickadee, spoke exclusively with The U.S. Sun and opened up about the heartbreaking experience of going through so many miscarriages.

    She was trying to have a third child with her long-term boyfriend Eldridge Toney after she had her daughter Kaitlyn Clark, now 11, and Kylee Cardwell, seven- Anna had her youngest with ex-husband Michael.

    She revealed: “We are trying to have a third child, and it’s been difficult. I’ve gone through four miscarriages and a D&C.

    "It's been a long bumpy road for us trying to have baby number three.” 

    Fans pay tribute
