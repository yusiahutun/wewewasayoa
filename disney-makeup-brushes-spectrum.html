    Because who doesn't secretly love a fairytale?

      Here at HELLO!, we love a story about princesses (who would have thought?!), which means that many of us have a  secret obsession with Disney films. They’re all about falling in love, fairytale weddings, gorgeous gowns, and amazing hair - doesn’t this all sound a little familiar? Well, now you can add some grown-up Disney glam to your dressing table with Spectrum’s new The Little Mermaid makeup brushes… and it’s just as magical as you’d expect.

      The Ariel brush set will you dreaming of the ocean

      The standout piece is the Ariel shell 10-piece brush set, where the stunning brushes are in the signature red and turquoise colours, with a downright adorable bag to match. If you’re more into Disney villains, then you’ll LOVE the purple Ursula set - her red lipstick and blue eye shadow is totally on point after all. Although, how does it stay that perfect underwater?! You can also grab a treasure chest of embellished oval brushes, and we’d rather top beauty tools over some old gold, any day (or so we tell ourselves).

      If Ursula is your favourite Disney villain, you'll love this set

      Hannah Pycroft, co-founder of Spectrum Collections says: “We grew up with Disney and for us it represents a magical, colourful and inspirational world. Combined with our own experience of starting in a garage and rising to great success along with how Disney inspires everyone to ‘dream big’, hopefully the collection is inspirational to our customers and fans of both brands. We’re really excited to be able to offer a piece of nostalgia, combined with on-trend design and ethical material choices which we hope will be a hit with consumers spanning generations.”

      These luxurious makeup brushes will totally transform your makeup game

      It seems that mermaid mania is certainly not going away, so if you’re not brave enough to wear glitter and aquamarine tones on your face, then why not do the next best thing and apply your trusty nudes with some metallic brushes?

      Also, 1% of all revenue will go to plastic bank, a charity that pays local communities to clean up plastic waste from oceans, keeping our sea creatures (including mermaids) safe.

      The collection launches on Thursday 31 May - so swim to the shops!
