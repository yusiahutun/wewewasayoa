After completing her studies at the Fiorello H. LaGuardia High School of Music & Art and Performing Arts, she went on to attend the University of North Carolina School of the Arts to further her passion for the entertainment field.

Nell’s education has undoubtedly been beneficial, evident from her rapid growth in the industry despite having only a few movies under her belt. She is incredibly creative and showcases her passion for art through her music and photography.

Career

In 2017, Nell Verlaque made her debut on the big screen with a small part in an episode of the CBS legal series Bull, playing the character of Emily Connor. Following this, she took on a leading role as Sarah Porter in Secrets in a Small Town in 2019.

In 2020, she started to gain recognition for her portrayal of Becky Harlow in The Marijuana Conspiracy. However, it was not until the following year, in 2021, that she truly made a breakthrough with her role in the Disney+ television show Big Shot.

As Louise Gruzinsky, Nell Verlaque was both delightful and engaging starring alongside John Stamos. Her character quickly captured the hearts of viewers in the comedic family show. While viewers thoroughly enjoyed the comedy-drama show, the series was canceled after 2 seasons.

Nell’s musical interests have led to the creation of multiple songs, including In My Dreams, Blush (teaser), Landlines, LA, Babe, and Softserve.
