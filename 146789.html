    Gunna Announces His Bittersweet North American Tour

    08 Jun 2024, 00:00

    LOS ANGELES (CelebrityAccess) — Rapper and songwriter Gunna announced the details of his upcoming “Bittersweet Tour” featuring special guest Flo Milli.

    The Live Nation-produced tour kicks off on May 4th with 16 shows scheduled across North America, including stops in markets such as Chicago, San Francisco, Houston, and Miami before concluding at Atlanta’s State Farm Arena on June 11.

    The tour will also see Gunna perform at the 2024 Roots Picnic Festival in Philadelphia on June 2nd.

    Gunna is touring in support of his latest track “Bittersweet” via YSL/300 Entertainment. The track is the debut single since his chart-topping fourth studio album Gift & a Curse.

    For the tour, Gunna is teaming up with the Black Music Action Coalition (BMAC), Live Nation, and his fans to support his non-profit, Gunna’s Great Giveaway, in his hometown and aims to contribute to the betterment of these communities and transform the economic landscape of Atlanta, GA.
