    Bio:
    Michelle Money (born Michelle Money in Salt Lake City, Utah, on September 28, 1980) is an American reality star, hair stylist, actress, and producer. After appearing on Season 15 of The Bachelor, Michelle remained in the Bachelor family by participating in the first seasons of Bachelor Pad in 2010 and Bachelor in Paradise in 2014. She is working as a hair stylist in Utah, also serving as a host on Good Things Utah.

    Best Known For:
    Michelle is best known for being a fan favorite on Season 15 of The Bachelor with Brad Womack.

    Personal Life:
    After meeting Cody Sattler on Season 1 of Bachelor in Paradise in 2014, Michelle and Cody dated on-and-off in a rather tumultuous relationship that landed them on the show Marriage Boot Camp: Reality Stars. The two split in early 2016. On Aug. 7, 2016, Michelle revealed that she is dating professional golfer Mike Weir in an Instagram post. She has a daughter, Brielle, who she had with her ex-husband. Michelle also revealed in July 2016 that she had gotten pregnant when she was just 15 years old, but gave the baby up for adoption.
