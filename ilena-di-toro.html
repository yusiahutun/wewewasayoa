    Ilena Di Toro is the new contestant on the stage of Jeopardy who dethroned Bryan White and held the title of winning champion on 30th July 2023. By profession, she is a Public Relations Specialist and lives in Philadelphia. Ilena emerged as a final Jeopardy winner after defeating White who entered the show on 28th June 2023, and on the third day of his Jeopardy game, he could not defend his winning streak. 

    Early Life

    A native of Philadelphia, Pennsylvania, Ilena Di Toro was born in 1969 and she is 54 years old. She had her education at Temple University from where she attained a BA in journalism/PR and described herself as a Klein College of Media and Communication graduate at Temple University. Later, she had her post-graduation from St. Joseph’s University with an MBA program in Marketing. She is licensed and received her certification in Public Relations from Universal Accreditation Board. Apart from all her primary education and degrees, she has received a volunteering experience in public relations from the “Neshaminy living history association”.

    Ilena Di Toro Career

    Ilena is based in Philadelphia, Pennsylvania, United States, and only had her professional life there. She started her career in 2001 while working as a public relations and development assistant for LANKENAU INSTITUTE FOR MEDICAL RESEARCH. Then, she worked as the publications & communications coordinator for Salus University (2005 to 2006). There, she was responsible for publishing monthly newsletters, magazines, and reports and spreading the publicity material to the media. In her presence, the value of the press, where she worked for nearly one year, rose to $26,275.

    Ilena left all her office jobs in 2007 and became a freelancer in the niche of public relation specialist until 2012. During that period, she worked for various profit and non-profit organizations having an income of up to 5 million dollars.

    From November 2012 to February 2014, she worked as the Senior Public Relations Specialist for St. Christopher’s Hospital For Children. There she handled internet and intranet communications, consulting services, social media accounts, and translating medical terminologies. Since 2014 she has been working for Debbie Goetz Media Connections, LLC, as Public Relations Specialist. She is responsible for handling media liaison for clients.

    Jeopardy Journey

    The latest episode of Jeopardy aired on 30th June 2023, where Bryan White (two-day winning champion) participated along with two other contestants, Courtney Johnston, and Elena Di Toro. The most astonishing thing was that all three contestants were at score Zero till the first commercial break. After the break, Bryan had 13 correct answers with three wrong answers. Courtney Johnston gave three correct answers and Ilena Di Toro gave two correct answers. Later on, Ilena gave two incorrect answers, which brought her down to a score of zero. After the first round, Ilena had zero scores, with Bryan leading the game at $2,200 and Courtney at $1,200.
