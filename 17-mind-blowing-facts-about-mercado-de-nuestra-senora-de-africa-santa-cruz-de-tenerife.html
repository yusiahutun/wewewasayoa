    FAQs

    1. What is the history of Mercado de Nuestra Señora de África?

    The Mercado de Nuestra Señora de África has a long history that dates back to the early 20th century. It was initially built as an open-air market but was later converted into a covered market in the 1940s. Over the years, it has remained a significant landmark in Santa Cruz de Tenerife.

    2. What can I find at Mercado de Nuestra Señora de África?

    You can find a wide variety of goods at Mercado de Nuestra Señora de África. From fresh fruits, vegetables, and seafood to local delicacies, spices, and handicrafts, the market has something to offer for everyone. Don’t forget to try some of the traditional Canarian dishes available at the market stands.
