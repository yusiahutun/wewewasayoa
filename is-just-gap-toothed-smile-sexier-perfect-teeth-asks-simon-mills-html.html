    Is it just me ...Or is a gap-toothed smile sexier than perfect teeth? asks SIMON MILLS

    Posing on the red carpet at the premiere of her new movie this summer, Dakota Johnson broke into a smile . . . and broke my heart. Her grin revealed newly minted Californian dentistry; the gap in her front teeth that delightful imperfection now corrected.

    31 May 2024, 00:00

    Posing on the red carpet at the premiere of her new movie this summer, Dakota Johnson broke into a smile . . . and broke my heart.

    Her grin revealed newly minted Californian dentistry; the gap in her front teeth — that delightful imperfection — now corrected.

    Bereft and inconsolable at this bland amelioration, I re-watched a charming film short of Dakota putting various items — paper clip, 11 $100 bills, credit card etc. — in between her front teeth to remind me of the Fifty Shades Of Grey star’s happy gappy years.

    You see, for a robust and red-blooded man, a girl’s midline diastema is very, very sexy.

    Women with gappy teeth are the stuff of wayward, nouvelle vague film fantasies; the sort of girls who could get a nice, sensible man into serious trouble. A girl with what the French call les dents de la chance (lucky teeth) will be insolent, passionate, promiscuous and mischievous. In turns, delirious and tempestuous, lush, divine and impossible — like Beatrice Dalle in Betty Blue.

    Madonna, Vanessa Paradis, Brigitte Bardot and Jane Birkin should never surrender to the train-track or Invisalign. Yes, gap-toothed models such as Lauren Hutton, Lara Stone and Georgia May Jagger have all been tempted, but wisely, not yet succumbed.

    Dakota Johnson broke into a smile ... and broke my heart. Californian dentistry! 

    ‘I like the fact I have good old-fashioned British teeth with a big gap,’ says Mick Jagger and Jerry Hall’s daughter. ‘Who wants those gleaming white, cosmetically enhanced American teeth?’

    The good news is that Dakota Johnson is getting a new retainer and her celebrated, sexy diastema is set for a comeback. So vive les dents de la chance!
