    LOS ANGELES (CelebrityAccess) – The multi-platinum rock band Staind is celebrating their second No. 1 single, “Here and Now,” from the highly acclaimed album Confessions of the Fallen.

    The band, comprised of lead vocalist and rhythm guitarist Aaron Lewis, lead guitarist Mike Mushok, bassist and backing vocalist Johnny April, and drummer Sal Giancarelli, emerged from their hometown of Springfield, MA, in 1995. With a musical journey spanning over 25 years, the band has released seven studio albums and earned eight Top 10 singles, achieving sales of over 15 million albums worldwide.

    Their latest single, “Here and Now,” has ascended to the top of the Active Rock chart, following the success of the debut single “Lowest In Me.” This achievement marks the second No. 1 hit from Confessions, their first in twelve years.

    “Here and Now” encapsulates the quintessential Staind sound—an emotionally charged ballad that strikes deep. The accompanying video masterfully captures the essence of the song, exploring the joy and pain of real-life moments that shape us all. You can view the official video HERE.

    Fans can anticipate a thrilling live experience as the boys in the band hit the road beginning April 18 in Augustin, FL, and then begin the Tailgate Tour alongside special guests Seether, Saint Asonia, and Tim Montana, beginning on April 22 in Brandon, MS.
