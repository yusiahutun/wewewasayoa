    A Rich History

    The Budapest Zoo & Botanical Garden, founded in 1866, is one of the oldest zoos in the world. It has stood the test of time to become a cherished landmark in Budapest, attracting millions of visitors each year.

    A Home for Diverse Wildlife

    The zoo is home to over 1,000 animal species, including rare and endangered ones. From majestic lions to playful penguins, visitors can witness the beauty and diversity of the animal kingdom up close.

    Lush Botanical Gardens

    The botanical gardens within the zoo span over 7 hectares and showcase a stunning collection of plants from around the world. Visitors can explore various themed gardens and experience the tranquility of nature.

    The Budapest Zoo & Botanical Garden is actively involved in conservation projects, focusing on protecting endangered species and their natural habitats. They collaborate with international organizations to support global conservation efforts.

    An Impressive Aviary

    The zoo’s Aviary is a haven for bird enthusiasts, housing a diverse range of bird species from all over the world. Visitors can admire the vibrant plumage and listen to the melodious songs of these feathered creatures.

    Interactive Exhibits

    With a focus on education and engagement, the Budapest Zoo & Botanical Garden offers interactive exhibits and programs. Visitors can learn about wildlife conservation, participate in feeding sessions, and even touch certain animals under expert supervision.

    The Butterfly House

    The Butterfly House is a mesmerizing experience, where visitors can wander through a tropical paradise surrounded by fluttering butterflies of various species. It is a magical and ethereal sight that captivates both young and old.

    Special Events and Activities

    The zoo hosts a range of special events and activities throughout the year, including nighttime visits, themed exhibitions, and animal shows. It provides a unique and entertaining experience for visitors of all ages.

    A Successful Breeding Program

    The Budapest Zoo & Botanical Garden has a successful breeding program, contributing to the conservation of endangered species. It takes pride in preserving and propagating various animals, ensuring their survival for future generations.

    Historical Architecture

    The zoo boasts stunning architectural elements, blending historical buildings with modern facilities. The Central Elephant House and the Main Entrance Pavilion are notable examples of architectural gems within the zoo grounds.

    A Family-Friendly Destination

    The zoo provides a memorable experience for families, with dedicated areas for children, including playgrounds and educational exhibits. It is an ideal destination for a day filled with fun and learning.

    A Green Oasis in the City

    The Budapest Zoo & Botanical Garden offers a peaceful retreat from the bustling city life. Its lush greenery and serene ambiance make it a perfect place for relaxation and reconnecting with nature.

    A Cultural Icon

    The Budapest Zoo & Botanical Garden has played a significant role in the city’s culture and has become a symbol of conservation and appreciation for wildlife. It continues to inspire visitors with its dedication to preserving the natural world.

    Conclusion
