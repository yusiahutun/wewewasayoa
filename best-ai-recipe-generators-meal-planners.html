    The 7 Best AI Recipe Generators and Meal Planners

    If youre looking for a little culinary inspiration, an AI recipe generator can be just the thing for you. These platforms can help you with interesting meal ideas that are customized to your preferences and dietary needs. Whats more, they can also make meal planning a lot easier.

    14 Mar 2024, 00:00

    If you’re looking for a little culinary inspiration, an AI recipe generator can be just the thing for you. These platforms can help you with interesting meal ideas that are customized to your preferences and dietary needs. What’s more, they can also make meal planning a lot easier.

    Whether you’re a novice cook or a seasoned pro, these AI tools can help you step up your culinary game.

    1. DishGen

    Easy to use with a straightforward interface, DishGen is a smart recipe assistant that can simplify healthy cooking for you. To use the platform, all you need to do is key in the name of the dish you’d like a recipe for.

    If you don’t have a particular recipe in mind, you can simply key in the ingredients you’d like to use up. The AI tool will generate a recipe based on the ingredients you enter. DishGen also has a number of recipes on its site for you to try, so you can explore exciting recipes from different cuisines that you weren’t familiar with earlier.

    2. Crooked Recipes

    With Crooked Recipes, meal planning becomes a breeze. Instead of spending hours browsing cookbooks and online recipes, you can use this AI platform to look up and cook delicious meals.

    To use the tool, start by listing the ingredients you have in your pantry or the ones you’d like to use. You can choose to further filter the results that show up based on the meal type, cuisine, dietary restrictions, and time available to cook the meal, among other factors.

    So, whether you’re looking for a vegan recipe or one that can be cooked in under 15 minutes, Crooked Recipes’ wide range of recipes can offer the culinary inspiration you need. Furthermore, if you’re not looking to use Crooked Recipes’ AI recipe generator, you can also just scroll through recipes that have already been generated on the platform.

    3. World Cancer Research Fund

    The World Cancer Research Fund’s free AI recipe generator helps you cook easy and delicious meals—perfect for a healthy college student's diet—without spending hours looking for the perfect recipe.

    The best part about the platform is that you can select the ingredients you’d like to use up—this can be a great way to clean out your refrigerator while also saving money by cooking at home.

    The recipes on the World Cancer Research Fund’s website are interesting and easy enough for even novice cooks to create praiseworthy meals. What’s more, the platform itself is extremely user-friendly and intuitive, making it a breeze to navigate and find a recipe.

    4. Be My Chef

    If you’re struggling with adding variety to your meals, Be My Chef’s recipe generator is the perfect solution. The AI recipe platform allows you to generate unlimited recipes that you can tailor to your preferences.

    To generate a recipe, all you need to do is enter keywords like "easy," summer dishes," or "vegan." You can key in multiple keywords at a time and add your dietary requirements, as well. Furthermore, you can specify what ingredients to include and exclude.

    You can pick any of the generated recipes to view the ingredients and cooking instructions. The platform also mentions details like prep time, cook time, and number of servings for the recipe. If you enjoy using the platform, you can join the company’s waitlist to hear about any updates.

    5. MealPractice

    It can be hard to think up new recipes and meal ideas every day. The good news is that with the MealPractice AI Recipe Generator, you don't have to. The platform lets you customize recipes by allowing you to choose your preferred protein, type of diet, and cuisine.

    Once that’s done, you’ll get to view a recipe with details like the ingredients, serving size, and directions. If you like a recipe, MealPractice gives you the option to email it to yourself. This way, you can access the recipe at any point in the future.

    6. Eat This Much

    If you’re on a diet, you know how difficult it can be to keep your body satiated while also trying to meet your health goals. Eat This Much can, fortunately, help you maintain a clean eating lifestyle. The platform turns meal planning into an effortless experience and provides you with tons of delicious recipe options that cater to your specific needs.

    To use Eat This Much, you’ll need to select your preferences and then generate your meal options. While paid users can generate a meal plan with recipes and ingredient lists for up to a week, those who use the platform for free can do so for a day. Still, it’s a great tool for meal planning and tracking your calories. The best part is that you can always regenerate the recipe for a certain meal if it isn’t to your liking or you’ve already eaten it in the past.

    7. My Diet Meal Plan

    There’s nothing worse than browsing one recipe after another to create a healthy meal plan for yourself. If you want to make meal planning easier, My Diet Meal Plan is a great option.

    The website essentially builds a custom meal plan for you based on your current goals, activity level, and dietary and eating preferences. The platform then generates your nutritional targets, but you can edit your calorie count and macros.

    Once this is done, My Diet Meal Plan builds a meal plan for the day within the predetermined caloric limit. Tapping on each meal option will let you view detailed ingredient lists and directions. This way, you can eat mindfully without losing out on variety or taste.

    If you enjoy the meal, you can save the recipe on the website to access it later. Upgrading from the free subscription to a paid one will let you access additional benefits like downloading your meal plan and planning ahead by generating weekly meal plans.

    AI Cooking Apps That Help You Plan Ahead

    Whether you’re trying to eat healthy or you just want to cook more often, these websites can help generate unique recipes for you.

    That said, if you’re a beginner and are finding it hard to follow more complex recipes, consider downloading a few healthy cooking apps that are meant for novice cooks. These apps will help build your cooking skills, allowing you to try out more creative recipes in the future.
