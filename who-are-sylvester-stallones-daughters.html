Who are Sylvester Stallones daughters?

 Posted on September 23, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  542 words  |  Aldo Pusey

The cinema landscape took a quick turn upward with the presence of a new filmmaker in town. From the moment Sylvester Stallone was engrossed in the filmmaking sphere, the actor/director created some of the most emblematic feature films of all time, including Rambo (2008) and most of the Rocky films in the franchise. With a career spanning over five decades, Stallone has rightfully earned a reputation as one of the industry’s most experienced and respected actors and directors.

Matching his half-a-century career, Sylvester Stallone’s personal life has remained a constant subject of scrutiny. Whether he is involved in a new project or caught up in controversial activities, the actor’s private affairs often find themselves in the spotlight. One notable aspect that has garnered attention is Stallone’s romantic relationships, which have resulted in the birth of five children – three daughters and two sons.

Recently, the Stallone sisters made an appearance on the podcast Chicks in the Office to discuss their new reality show, The Family Stallone. The show provides a glimpse into the lives of the daughters of one of Hollywood’s most renowned figures. If you’ve been curious about the Stallone sisters, here’s a quick rundown of their lives.

Sophia Rose Stallone

Sophia Rose Stallone, the eldest sister among the Stallone siblings, was born on August 27, 1996, in Miami, Florida. She is the daughter of Sylvester Stallone and Jennifer Flavin. While Sophia initially pursued studies at the University of California, her career has predominantly focused on working behind the camera. Despite her academic pursuits, she has remained active in the realm of reality television, frequently taking on roles as a judge in various contests, and making occasional appearances on talk shows.

Sistine Stallone

Perhaps one of the most famous Stallone siblings is Sistine Stallone. Born on June 27, 1998, the 24-year-old has already ventured into the industry following in her father’s footsteps. Her acting debut was in Johannes Roberts’ 2018 horror film 47 Meters Down: Uncaged. Aside from her brief acting career, Sistine also became a well-known name in the modeling sphere, participating in several runway shows and magazine covers representing Chanel. Since 2018 the actress and model has been working on getting her degree in communications.

Scarlet Rose Stallone

The youngest Stallone sister is Scarlet Rose Stallone, who was born on May 25, 2002, in Los Angeles, California. Much like her oldest siblings, Scarlet has also ventured into the entertainment world from a young age. In 2014, she participated in the film Reach Me, albeit with a minor role, and in 2022 she was on five episodes of Tulsa King, where she played the barista, Spencer. For the foreseeable future, she will also participate in her family’s reality show, The Family Stallone.

Despite their occasional complaints from constantly being in their dad’s limelight, all the sisters are all incredibly grateful for having been born under such a powerful influence in their lives, and it will all be broadcast on their upcoming show The Family Stallone, which will reach Paramount Plus on May 17.
