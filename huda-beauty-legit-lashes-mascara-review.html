    When you've built a massive beauty empire on the promise of ultra-fluttery false eyelashes, mascara can seem kind of pointless. No mascara—even the ones covered in the logos of iconic fashion houses—can deliver the drama, length, and volume as a set of falsies. Nobody knows false eyelashes better than Huda Kattan, therefore nobody was more equipped to create the next iconic mascara.

    LEGIT LASHES Double-Ended Mascara

    LEGIT LASHES Double-Ended Mascara

    Does the world even need another mascara? There are lash-boosters at all price points for volume, length, curl, color—even a famous one that promises it's better than sex. Some are waterproof, some are easy to wash off at the end of the day. There is little room left for innovation when it comes to a wand and an inky black formula. Which is precisely why it took Kattan and her team five years to finally produce Huda Beauty Legit Lashes—a mascara so good it's actually two mascaras.

    "Honestly, my biggest frustrations with mascaras were the ad campaigns. I think that's like the thing that bothered me so much," Kattan tells BAZAAR exclusively. "There are so many amazing mascaras out there, but some of the ads that they used were just so, in my opinion, not necessarily to the point. A lot of the times, like you would see these little asterisks and they would say, you know, we used false eyelashes with this campaign or there is obviously a lot of photoshopping or they would use models with really beautiful lashes."

    Legit Lashes is the next step in Kattan's efforts toward beauty transparency. The blogger-turned-mogul started her makeup line with covetable sets of false eyelashes in 2013, then added lipsticks and best-selling shadow palettes. When she launched her skincare line Wishful earlier this year, the mega influencer pledged to eliminate all filters, Photoshop, makeup, or Facetune from the brand's messaging. With Huda Beauty's Legit Lashes, the lashes you see on Instagram are the lashes you'll get IRL.

    Related Story

    Legit Lashes is actually two mascaras in one tube. On one side there's a mascara ideal for curl and length, while on the other is a formula ideal for adding volume and length. It addresses head-on mascara's biggest problem: they're not created equal. The mascara you love won't be the mascara I love, because some of us need volume and some of us need length. "I want this to be for everyone. And I want to be the best mascara they've ever used. And I truly feel like we nailed the formula," she says.

    We tested both mascaras over a week, and found that they truly deliver on the promise of instantly insane lashes. The volumizing formula (shown at bottom) is thick without turning clumpy after just one coat, yet it still delivers dramatic length. The lengthening formula (shown in the middle) gently grips lashes to add definition and a long-lasting curl. You can also layer both, starting with the volumizing formula, to create lashes so lush they could actually be mistaken for falsies. The formula sticks around all day without flaking into contacts, but it was easily removed with a gel face cleanser at the end of the day.

    Both formulas utilize special fibers to add Instagram-worthy length, and the formula is very, very black. "The color is vanta, a black that is considered to be so dark that it actually absorbs light. And so it grabs all the attention," says Kattan. "I want the attention to be all my lashes."

    Kattan notes that it took years of iterations—and a manufacturer change—to finally nail the two perfect mascara formulas. "I didn't want to have to do another mascara. It had to be this good, like kind of irreplaceable," she says. "And so that's why we were so particular with them with this mascara, why it took five years. I think that's that's a genuinely long time for mascara."

    It's Kattan's passion for eyelashes real and false—plus years of expertise around launching makeup—that helped Legit Lashes come to life. "I did launch our business with false eyelashes because I've been obsessed with eyelashes. I've been I've always been like an insanely hairy person with no lashes...So I've always been really obsessed with lashes because they're so feminine," she recalls. "I've always just wanted long, beautiful, luscious lashes and never in my life did I ever think that I would feel comfortable not wearing false eyelashes. And for the first time ever, I actually feel like I can with this mascara."

    If you're a fan of Kattan's falsies, you don't need to choose between them and Legit Lashes. The two work in harmony. "The reason why I do like wearing this mascara with my false eyelashes is because it doesn't really smudge as much as many mascaras do. I like to use oil on my skin because I have dry skin. And so for me, like any mascara I use will always run. And this one just kind of stays put. Also, it's so insanely black," she says. The formula sets quickly and stays dark, giving the perfect base foundation for applying your lash glue and lashes.

    "To me, it's a no brainer. It's the best mascara ever," adds Kattan.
