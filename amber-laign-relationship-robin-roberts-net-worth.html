Amber Laign Early Life And Parents

Born on 6 September 1974, in California, USA, Amber is a gay woman. The 47-years-old fashion star was born to parents Paul Laign and Andrea Laign.

Later, she received a license as a massage therapist after her massage training at The Swedish Institute of Health and Sciences in New York City.
