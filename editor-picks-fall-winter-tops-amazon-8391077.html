    I spent four years designing tops; from selecting the fabric to elevating the silhouette, I worked through every step of the process. You may think it's easy to create an everyday top, but there are many factors in crafting the perfect basic — especially for the colder months. So when I shop for tops during the seasonal shift, I look for the same elements I used to design into: the fabric has to be substantial and warm, easy to style, and layerable. I also opt for long sleeves with an oversized fit instead of short-sleeved, cropped styles. Using this criteria, I’ve built a capsule of quality tops that I go back to year after year.

    Want to start building your perfect fall and winter tops collection? Well, I’ve got you covered. I scanned through Amazon’s many pages and rounded up 10 of the best tops to have for the colder months, which include long-sleeve bodysuits, cozy flannels, and more that check all the boxes — and prices start as low as $12.

    Trendy Queen Long-Sleeve Top

    If there is one product I recommend everyone have in their closet, it’s a classic long-sleeve shirt, which is why I plan on adding this Trendy Queen Long-Sleeve Top to my Amazon cart. They’re easy to style; you can layer them under sweaters and flannels for extra warmth, and they’re flattering on every body type. The wardrobe essential comes in 15 colors, including tan, black, blue, brown, and white, in sizes XS through L. It has a fitted silhouette, a classic scoop-neck, and a length that falls just above the natural hip when fully extended, but it can also be worn scrunched for a cropped style.

    Dokotoo Waffle-Knit Button-Down Top

    My uniform before the temperature dropped below 50 degrees was a breezy button-down and a pair of jeans. But now, I’m looking to replace my lightweight button-down with something more substantial, and I’m thinking of Dokotoo’s Waffle-Knit Button-Down Top. It’s the perfect blend of my go-to waffle henley and my summer button-down — meaning I need it in every color.

    The textured button-down comes in 13 colors, including pink, black, tan, brown, yellow, and blue, in sizes S through XXL. It features a warm waffle fabric, a functional button-front placket, and a spacious front pocket. The top has an oversized fit, making it great for layering, and falls at the mid-thigh, so it also pairs well with leggings. 

    Mangopop Square-Neck Bodysuit

    If you’re ever looking for a bunch- and bubble-free look when tucking your top into your favorite jeans or sweatpants, opt for the Mangopop Square-Neck Bodysuit. You get a smooth and flattering fit without all the fuss of adjusting a regular shirt — and the long sleeves will keep you warm. It’s available in 24 colors, such as red, black, burgundy, floral, and animal print, in sizes XS through XXL. It features a flattering square neckline, long sleeves, a snap closure at the crotch, and full-coverage bottoms. The fabric is buttery soft and has great stretch, per reviewers, which gives that seamless finish.

    Wneedu Waffle-Knit Henley Top

    This Wneedu Waffle-Knit Henley Top has been my go-to for years. It’s super soft, easy to style, and perfect for layering. It comes in 28 colors, including gray, red, black, pink, and blue, in sizes XS to 3XL. It features a plush and warm textured waffle fabric, a crew neckline, and a functional button-up closure. The silhouette fits oversized without being too baggy, making it a great layering piece for fall and winter.
