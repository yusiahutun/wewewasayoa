    When you’re short on time in the morning before school or work, it’s not always possible to bust out all the brushes and palettes with bronzers and blushes. That’s why stick products come in so clutch, whether you’re applying them literally on the go or just quickly before running out the door. If you want an option that blends easily with your fingers and quickly warms up your skin, reach for the new Tarte Maneater Silk Stick Bronzer.

    Unlike other bronzers that can get messy in your purse, these bronzer sticks are cute and compact. The silky formula glides right across your skin, on a bare face or over makeup. That’s thanks to a triple-oil blend of antioxidants and essential fatty acids including meadowfoam, avocado and babaco oils.

    There are seven shades covering all skin tones: Nocturnal (for very deep skin tones), Eclipse (deeper skin tones), Midnight (tan-deep skin tones), Nightfall (tan skin tones), After Dark (medium skin tones), Sundown (light skin tones) and Dusk (fair skin tones).

    The Maneater Silk Stick Bronzer will be on Tarte’s website for $26 on June 10. But don’t wait. Grab it at QVC now and get a bronzer brush, too (a $30 value) for just $35 total. Use the brush to blend out a flawless, heavier application.

    Now, if you know Tarte, you know it wouldn’t release bronzer without some pretty blush shades, too. Well, we’ve got you. We have a sneak peek of Tarte Maneater Silk Stick Blush in three gorgeous shades and the same velvety, second-skin finish. Apply a few dots of bronzer and dots of blush and blend each for a quick flawless face.

    These will also retail for $26 on Tarte’s website on June 24. But you can get it early (with a brush!) on QVC on June 9.
