    It's a battle of a Hollywood starlet and a Royal Princess in the same Roksanda Ilincic dress!

    Marisa Tomei wore the blush wool dress to the Berluti Menswear Spring/Summer 2013 show during Paris Fashion Week on June 29. She accessorized the look with a sophisticated updo, two-toned Aperlai peep-toe heels and a tan clutch.

    Princess Beatrice of York donned the long-sleeved blue-cuffed dress just days earlier to the Britain Creates 2012: Fashion & Art Collusion VIP Gala on June 27. She paired the ensemble with matching blue patterned Nicholas Kirkwood pumps and a rose glittered Anya Hindmarch clutch.

    While both the ladies looked lovely, we think Princess Beatrice wins this battle! Her royal blue heels and studded clutch bring nice pop of color without taking away from the dress … while Marisa's two-toned peep-toe pumps are a little over the top for this conservative look.
