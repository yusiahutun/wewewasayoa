Dr. Jan Pol is a television personality who is extremely famous for the Nat Geo Wild television show, “The Incredible Dr. Pol”. It’s basically a reality show like ‘The Curse of Oak Island’ that follows Dr. Pol’s work in rural Weidman, Michigan. Pol and his amazing team work to treat animals and help farmers find their animal business.

Jan, 75, has been a local hero for countless years of service. However, he eventually and deservedly began to be recognized for his work all over the world because of the show. The Nat Geo show began in 2011 and is produced by Jan’s son, Charles. Over the years, the show has had an incredible audience and is constantly breaking records on the Channel. Let’s now meet the amazing Dr. Pol.

Dr. Pol: early life, education and career

Jan Pol was born in Drenthe, the Netherlands on September 4, 1942. He grew up on a dairy farm in a large family along with five other siblings, where he was the youngest; 2 brothers and 3 sisters. Having grown up in the countryside among animals, he always liked animals.

Growing up, Jan wanted to go into farming like the rest of his family. However, at the age of 12, he had the opportunity to help a vet deliver a litter of piglets and it turned out to be a life-changing moment for him. Dr. Pol then wanted to pursue the life of a veterinarian. Jan studied veterinary medicine at Utrecht University and later graduated with a Doctor of Veterinary Medicine (DVM) degree.

He has had an amazing career and has accomplished a lot as a veterinarian. He is currently the owner of ‘Pol Veterinary Services’ and has a team of specialists who help him. His clinic in Weidman, Michigan has been able to provide many different services to more than 19,000 clients. To begin with, Jan began providing services for large animals such as horses, cattle, and sheep. But later, with more experience, he began to take care of the little ones as well.

Dr. Pol is well known and is considered a familiar figure in his field. Jan is a hardworking man and even at 75, he works to make sure the cattle are healthy.

Dr. Pol: Personal Life

Dr. Jan Pol has been married to his wife Daine Pol for over 50 years. It’s a feat in itself to have a long-term committed relationship like them. The couple still have the same love, mutual understanding and spark between them.

These two met in 1961 when Jan was on a foreign exchange program at Mayville High School. She spent a year of high school in Michigan to be closer to her sister who had moved to Canada. In Michigan, she was taken in by a family and Diane was the daughter of that family.

When Pol returned to the Netherlands, he still tried to stay in touch with Diane through letters. In one of his letters Pol invited Diane to visit him and this laid a solid foundation for their relationship.

Later in 1967, Pol married Diane in a small wedding ceremony. After taking their wedding vows, they returned to the Netherlands as Dr. Pol had yet to complete his education; Pol graduated in 1970.

Dr. Pol and Diane continue to be an inspiring couple for many. The story of how they met and the fact that they are still together is a perfect story. The two currently work together for Jan’s veterinary service. They have been married for over 50 years but have no children of their own. The kind couple decided to adopt and give a home to three children, a son and two daughters.

Dr. Pol: Net Worth

Today, Dr. Pol, who gained fame through Nat Geo, is a reality star. Charles, the son of The Incredible Dr. Pol, is also an actor and producer; he was the one who had the idea to turn Dr. Pol’s veterinary business into a television show. When it started, Dr. Pol did not expect the show to make it past the first season, now to his surprise it is currently in its twelfth season.

Pol is part of the cast of the program and also the owner of a “Pol veterinary service”. It is believed that he accumulated a huge sum. According to estimates, his net worth is believed to be around one million dollars.
