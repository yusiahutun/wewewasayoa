    Emma Willis looked fabulous on Monday evening as she returned to Elstree Studios to present the first of the news series of Big Brother. The popular TV host wore a crisp white bandage plunge gown by Australian designer Nicholas The Label, who are renown for their clean lines and subtle easy to wear pieces. The fishtail dress, which retails for £540 online, look gorgeous on the 41-year-old, and featured a lace trim, plunging neckline and ruffled hem.

    Emma teamed the design with a pair of leg lengthening nude Christian Louboutins, while statement hoops ensured that all eyes were on her while she took to her presenting duties.

    Emma Willis looked sensational in a white summer-ready cocktail dress 

    Beauty-wise, Emma’s short, elfin haircut was swept back in a sleek ‘do, while a glowy base enhanced her striking complexion. A bold outline of liner added a touch of edge to her outfit, while a strong brow and nude pink lip completed her look beautifully.

    Emma wore another cool white dress while hosting The Voice UK last month

    And it appears that Emma is a fan of monochromatic dressing this season – and rightly so. Last month, she wore another stylish design while hosting The Voice UK – a form-fitting, long sleeved, white cocktail dress by British designer Suzanne Neville. The floor-length design was once again acessorised with a pair of go-to Christian Louboutins and elegant drop earrings.

    On social media, Emma often posts photos of her outfits alongside the details of where each piece is from, allowing for them to replicate her look too – something other popular presenters such as Rochelle Humes and Holly Willoughby are also prone to doing.

    Emma's ultra-cool birthday present - a pair of Charlotte Olympia kitty flats

    Emma, who is married to musician Matt Willis, also shares photos of her family life. Back in March, the mother-of-three posted a photograph with her young son, Ace, showcasing her oh-so-cool birthday present – a pair of Charlotte Olympia kitty flats.
