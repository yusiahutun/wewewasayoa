    Meet Derek Richard Thomas, Rumer Willis Boyfriend

    Derek first came to light as Rumer Willis’ boyfriend in September 2022 after the two were spotted locking lips during a shopping trip in New York City’s SoHo neighborhood.

    Two months later, Rumer then made their relationship IG official as she shared a series of sweet snaps. Over one of the images, Rumer even planted a kiss on Derek as he smiled from ear to ear.

    Sadly, we don’t know when the lovebirds actually started dating. But it’s clear that the two were an item before August as Derek paid tribute to the Dancing With The Star alum then.

    “In the short time we’ve been together you have filled my life with joy and peace,” he captioned an IG post of Rumer. Furthermore, the star mentioned that everything was easy with her. “We sing, we dance, we play and most of all we laugh. Thank you for being you. You’re a magical human I’m so glad you came into my life,” he added.

    Back in May 2022, the actress also once hinted at their romance as she share a video of Rumer performing. “@derekrichardthomas and @thehailmaries are the most talented singers and songwriters I have heard in a very long time (maybe ever),” she said in the post.

    Also, it seems that Rumer had the chance to perform with them that night.

    And now, Derek and Rumer are pregnant! To share the good news, the actress took to her IG to flaunt her baby bump in Dereks’ embrace. Naturally, friends and family flooded to comment section.

    Rumer’s sister, Scout Willis wrote, “I feel so good. What a joyful hard launch party,” while Tallulah Willis, exclaimed. “EEEEEEEE!!!! we stan this mommy!!” Even the trio’s mother joined in, “Entering my hot kooky unhinged grandma era.”

    Derek Richard Thomas Age

    Derek Richard Thomas was born in May 1994. That made him 28 years of age in 2022.

    He is 6 years younger than his baby-mother Rumer Willis.

    Fun Fact: Derek weighed 4.33 kg (9 lbs 9 oz) when he was born.

    Derek Richard Thomas Family

    Derek comes from a family of at least five. Sadly, his parents are separated.

    Now, his mother, Vicki Reed leads a single life in Sacramento, CA. She turned 61 in July 2022. Here’s her IG @vickireed03 and FB @vickireed03.

    Likewise, Derek’s father lived alone in Santa Cruz, CA. he turned 64 in April 2022.

    On mother’s day 2022, Derek took to his IG to feature her captioned, “Happy Mother’s Day to my Momma, my Nanna, and my Sister.”

    As for his siblings, his brother Andrew Thomas turned 34 on November 4, 2022. He worked at FPI Management and Verizon and resided in Sacramento, California. Here’s his IG @andrewreedthomas. On the other hand, Derek’s sister Gina Caples was already married with three kids. She is a Butte College business graduate who turned 31 in May 2022.

    Sadly, Derek’s maternal grandfather Richard R. Reed and uncle Rick Reed have long passed away. Likewise, his paternal grandfather Andrew P. Thomas took his last breath in October 2020. He passed away at the age of 86 after a courageous battle with cancer.

    How Much Is Derek Richard Thomas Net Worth?

    Derek Richard Thomas garnered a net worth of under $400 thousand by 2022. On the other hand, his baby mama Rumer Willis was worth a stunning $4 million then.

    A musician by profession, Derek is the guitarist and singer of the indie band Vista Kicks. The band comprised of Derek, Trevor Sutton, and Nolan Le Vine was pretty well known locally and they even had the opportunity to perform at the British Summer Time Hyde Park concert in June 2022. It seems they opened for The Rolling Stones and Phoebe Bridgers. 

    “Well this is unbelievable,” Derek announced his achievement on IG.

    Besides singing, Derek’s also a music producer. He’s produced music for the band The Hail Maries.

    Find Derek’s music on Spotify. Some of his popular solo releases were Pacific Coast Highway, Jesus On A Grey Hound Bus, How Many Years, and Real F*cking Lucky.
