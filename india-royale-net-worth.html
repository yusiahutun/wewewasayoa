What is India Royale’s net worth? How many kids does India Royale have?

Introduction

India Royale is an American entrepreneur and social media influencer who experienced her rise to fame following her swift growth on Instagram.

She is known for none other than posting selfies. In addition, she is a fashion and lifestyle blogger and a well-known YouTuber.

In 2019, she became engaged to the famous American rapper Lil Durk, with whom she shares a daughter. 

The Origin Story

India Royale was born on March 9, 1995, in Chicago, Illinois, the United States, to a Christian family. 

Her birth name is India Cox.

She grew up interested in fashion and beauty from a young age and began wearing makeup and doing her hair.

She was also particular with the clothing she allowed her parents to dress her in. And this trend only grew more prominent as she grew into herself.

Career

Following her high school graduation, it was always her goal to pursue a career in beauty. And like most other beauticians, she took to social media to start her career.

Due in part to her outgoing personality, she was a huge hit out of the gates and quickly garnered a substantially large following.

She went from posting photos on Instagram to building a multimillionaire net worth through dedication to her passion.

Royale was always ambitious and had a strong desire to become a business owner in addition to becoming a beauty blogger.

As such, she successfully runs two e-commerce businesses that both do relatively well, largely due to her online fame.

Instagram

But far before she had the popularity and online presence to even consider becoming a digital beauty retailer, she began her journey on Instagram.

From the early days of 2012, she has grown her page to over 4.2 million followers, many of which are celebrities, influencers, and big brand fashion stores.

One of the fashion brands that follow her is Fashion Nova, and that’s because she is one of their chosen ambassadors.

Although she has many other avenues in which she earns income, a small portion of her lucrative net worth is derived from her partnership with Fashion Nova.

Fiancé/Husband

In response to her social media fame, she began to rub elbows with celebrities and musicians since she was invited to many award shows and exclusive events.

As such, she met her now fiancé, Lil Durk, an American rapper from her hometown of Chicago. Durk was previously married to Nicole Covone.

The two met in 2017 and have since become engaged. Lil proposed to Royale onstage at the Chicago Concert: Watch.

Children

YouTube

In addition to her Instagram career, she is also quite popular on YouTube, classifying her as a successful YouTuber.

Her channel went live on March 6, 2018, and she has only posted 20 videos so far.

Despite the lack of regular content, she still manages to get over 1 million views on most of her content. Her content on this platform is particularly centered around clothing hauls and reviewing beauty products.

TikTok

And like all other social media stars, when the famous Chinese-based platform TikTok became famous in the American market, she jumped on board.

She created an account in 2020, and since then, it has amassed over 1.9 million followers and over 19.1 million likes.

Her TikTok account is different from all others but more of a hybrid of them all.

She uploads a lot of family-related content that includes her fiancé and two children, and a family dog.

You can find her poting beauty and fashion content, and the traditional lipsynching and dancing. 

In addition to her business life which is largely social media based, she is also a very family-oriented mother who spends a lot of her time with her family.

A lot of her content includes her children, but she does talk about how she is still very close with her parents.

She doesn’t include them in her content, likely to give them tier privacy, but she does talk about them from time to time. She also enjoys traveling, which she posts a lot of content on, and brings her children with her everywhere she goes, including overseas.
