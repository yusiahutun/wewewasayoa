Rebecca Liddicoat gained popularity as a famous person’s spouse. Robert Griffin III, a former quarterback for the Washington Redskins, Cleveland Browns, and Baltimore Ravens, was her husband. When she was married to the football player, she was a stay-at-home mom. For seven seasons, Robert Griffin III was a player in the National Football League (NFL). When Rebecca Liddicoat was his wife, she was his staunchest ally and advocate.

Who Is Rebecca Liddicoat?

Rebecca Liddicoat was born on August 13, 1998, in Boulder, Colorado, United States of America. Her parents are Edward and Laura Liddicoat. She also has an elder sister named Lindsey. Liddicoat went to High School at Copperas Cove. Following her high school graduation, she studied in biology at Baylor University. During her time at the university, she also served as president of the Chi Service Club.

The Instagram page for Rebecca Liddicoat is not accessible since she is not active on social media. When she has leisure time, she likes to travel, and her ideal location is Switzerland. She enjoys continental cuisine as well as seafood, which is her favourite food. She has a pet dog and cat and adores animals in general. She routinely visits the gym to stay in shape.

Rebecca Liddicoat’s Career

When Liddicoat and Griffin III were married, she was a stay-at-home mom. She dabbled in uncharted business ventures following their divorce.

Rebecca Liddicoat And Robert Griffin III

Robert Griffin III, a former NFL quarterback, was Liddicoat’s husband. The couple first went on a date in 2009 while attending Baylor University. Gryphon played collegiate football, and Liddicoat presided over her university’s Chi Service Club.

A mutual friend brought the couple together. Liddicoat claimed that Griffin’s “weird” sense of style did not impress her, therefore it was not love at first sight. He had on brightly coloured socks including SpongeBob SquarePants, Cookie Monster, and Scooby-Doo.

“When I first met him, I thought he was a little weird. I was a little nervous, and I asked him why he was wearing girls’ socks. He had on those weird socks he always wears,” she said.

Notwithstanding, Griffin’s modest demeanour won her over, and they ended up being college sweethearts. Following a game against Kansas State in 2010, in front of family and friends, Griffin proposed to her. During her engagement party, he allegedly serenaded her with songs he had written. 

On July 6, 2013, they were married at the Four Seasons in Denver, which is not far from Boulder, Colorado, Liddicoat’s hometown. Families, friends, and NFL stars DeAngelo Hall, Kirk Cousins, and Santana Moss were all there at the wedding.

Reese Ann Griffin, the couple’s daughter, was born on May 21, 2015. However, a few months after the birth of their daughter, Liddicoat discovered the details of Griffin’s purported extramarital affair. There were rumours that he was having an extramarital affair with track athlete Grete Šadeiko. It is said that in January 2016, just seven months after the birth of his daughter with Liddicoat, Gryphon III and Adeiko connected on Instagram.

On August 16, 2016, their divorce was finally completed. Sources claim that the court awarded her full custody of their daughter Reese Ann along with a payment of $1.1 million. In addition, the divorce agreement stipulated that Griffin would give their daughter $36,000 a month in child support and that her ex-husband would contribute half of the Texas home’s value.

Griffin moved on with Šadeiko shortly after his divorce in 2016, and the two have been together ever since. Gloria and Gameya Griffin, the couple’s two children, were born in 2018 after they were married. It is said that a third child is on the way.
