Early Life & Education

The exact birth date of Kristine Saryan is not known, but it is said that she was born in April 1984. She grew up in Los Angeles, California, with her two siblings, Diane and Michael.

Kristine completed her education at Haddonfield Memorial High School and then spent two years at Joane Baron DW Brown’s Studio of Drama and Theater, where she focused on honing her skills in comedic and dramatic acting. It is worth noting that she is also a graduate of the Meisner Program.

Career

With nearly 20 years of experience, Kristine is an accomplished actress best known for her portrayal of Chrissy in the beloved television series Gilmore Girls. Over the course of seven years, she also served as Miss Patty’s dance assistant on the show.

The original series concluded in 2007, but a follow-up miniseries titled Gilmore Girls: A Year In The Life premiered in 2016. In the second episode, Spring, she made an appearance as a 38-year-old woman who was waiting for an interview.

In addition, Kristine played the role of Nicole McKinnon, the wife of the lead detective, in Kidnapped: The Hannah Anderson Story.

Personal Life

Kristine Saryan is a contentedly married individual whose husband is named Scott Patterson. He is a renowned American actor and musician, most recognized for playing Luke Danes on the popular show Gilmore Girls. It is speculated that the couple began their relationship in 2001.

There is contradictory information regarding the circumstances of their first encounter. Some speculate that they first crossed paths while working on Gilmore Girls, while others claim they were acquainted before their roles on the show. The pair, who played a romantic couple in the series, tied the knot in a private ceremony in 2014, surrounded by loved ones and close friends.

In July 2014, Kristine and Scott welcomed their son, Nicholas Patterson, into the world. Scott was overcome with happiness at his son’s birth and shared the news on social media. Ever since he has been sharing his joyful experiences with his son on his Instagram account.

Despite the 24-year age difference between them, their love has continued to flourish. They reside in the luxurious Beverly Hills neighborhood of Los Angeles.
