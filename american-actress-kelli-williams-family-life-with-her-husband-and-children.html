    Kelli Williams's Marriage Life With Husband Ajay Sahgal

    Kelli Williams was happily married to writer and producer Ajay Sahgal in 1996. Williams and Sahgal were a happy couple as a loving wife and caring husband for more than two decades though there is very little information regarding their conjugal life. Despite being a celebrity, they stayed away from mainstream media and enjoyed quite a private married life. The couple rarely appeared in public, so fans rarely saw the pair together. 

    Unfortunately, according to IMDb, the couple ended their 21-year-long marriage in 2017. It came as a total shock, cause they seemed perfectly fine before the news broke out. There is no information about the reason behind their divorce as the pair has not spoken regarding their split and no media reported about their separation. We can only assume that Williams and Sahgal decided on a different path as things didn't work out, and they chose not to talk about it in the media to avoid controversies.

    Kelli Williams's Career 

    Kelli Williams is best known for her character of Lindsay Dole in an American legal drama The Practice (1997-2003), created by David E. Kelly. She also starred in a medical drama Medical Investigation (2004-2005), a crime drama Lie To Me(2009-2011), and another TV series Army Wives(2012-2013).

    Beautiful Hollywood actress Kelli Williams

    Williams began her career in television in 1998 on the CBS series Beauty and the Beast followed by The Case of the Hillside Stranglers (1998) and Day by Day (1998). During her 29-year career, she worked in many series and movies. To name a few, Mr. Jones (1993), There Goes My Baby (1994), Earth 2 (1995), The Lyon's Den (2003), The Fosters (2016), and many more.

    Kelli Williams is a gorgeous actress who managed to achieve a successful career through her hard work and dedication. She is still determined to work harder and give the best of her to fans.
