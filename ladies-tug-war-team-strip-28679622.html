    A female sports team has warned a building site is nowhere to be nude after posing in one for a naked calendar, as risky photoshoots drew "unexpected onlookers".

    The Melton Mowbray Ladies Tug Of War team decided a great way to make money to keep the club running would be to star in their own 2023 calendar.

    What started as a joke soon developed into them all stripping off in fields with animals and even scaffolding, treating passers-by to an eyeful.

    "Obviously taking nude photos in some of these places comes with a bit of risk.

    "FYI a building site is no place to be naked! We have had some unexpected onlookers."

    The need for extra funds is to cover costs of the team travelling up and down the country to compete each week, buying kit and paying for equipment to train which Rachel assures gets "pretty expensive".

    In a cunning business savvy move, the team got 12 different local Leicestershire companies to sponsor a month each and shot a racy photo based on the work they do.

    Rachel said: "We have builders, farm shops, agricultural businesses. All people who wanted to support us in the venture.

    "Some of the girls were more up for stripping off than others. I was very keen as I thought it would be fun and a great way to raise money.

    "With a little persuasion, we got those less keen into some of the photos but organising getting 15 girls together to do anything is difficult, but we pulled together and made it happen.

    "We started taking photos up and down the county from October and finally finished at the end of November."

    The various photoshoots were not without their obstacles thanks to not always being in as discreet a location as first thought.

    Rachel continued: "The hedge at one of the locations wasn't quite thick enough and a group of motorists got a good eyeful.

    "Also an old man was minding his own business in his garden on a Sunday afternoon when we appeared naked on the scaffolding of the house next door.

    "We have livestock in some of our photos... wrangling sheep in the nude is quite stressful."

    The hard work and occasional embarrassment is already proving to have been worth it as the calendar has received a positive response far and wide.

    Customers so far have included rival tug of war teams in the UK and even surprise fans in Sweden and the United States.

    Rachel explained that the club competing in the unusual sport, was born out of victory at a Young Farmers National Competition.

    The women decided they loved the sport so much they wanted to become an affiliated team with the English tug of war association.

    There are eight women on the rope when pulling out of a squad of around 15 who pull in different weight categories.

    The Melton Mowbray Ladies Tug Of War represented England at the Tug Of War World Championships in Holland in September which was fully funded by the women themselves.
