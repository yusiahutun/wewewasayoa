    15 Aug 2024, 00:00

    We are so happy to announce that Erin and Ben welcomed a healthy baby girl, Mae Napier, into the world last Friday, May 28th. After the hardest year of filming, a pandemic, and being very pregnant, this has been a bright spot that has changed everything for the Napier family!

    We can’t wait to celebrate Baby Mae with Erin and Ben, and we know you can’t wait either! What’s great about our Home Town family from all across the world is that we are able to celebrate and support one another as one big community. We’re convinced there’s no better group of people that support Erin and Ben, the show, and our small town.

    For instance, Erin recently hosted a secret, virtual baby shower on Instagram for her sister and brother-in-law, Jesse and Lauren. Every item on their Amazon wish list was purchased and delivered to Jesse and Lauren’s house. They were completely blown away by the generosity of our Home Town family!

    In celebration of Baby Mae, Erin is hosting an even BIGGER virtual baby shower and calling on our neighbors far and wide to support Choices Clinic of Laurel by purchasing items on their Amazon Wish List or making a general donation.

    Choices Clinic of Laurel serves our community by empowering individuals to make life-affirming decisions through confidential medical services, education, and emotional support while compassionately sharing Biblical truth and Christ’s love.

    These gifts and donations will provide new parents free and confidential pregnancy testing, limited ultrasounds, STI testing and treatment, pregnancy options counseling and essential supplies.

    Erin says, “My favorite thing is that the Choices Clinic has parenting courses that prepare them for what’s to come. Plus, they earn points from attending and these points allow them to shop the Choices’ Blessing Center for the baby.”

    The Choices Clinic has been such a blessing to women and men in our community. We hope that our support will encourage and empower families to grow and know that they’re not alone.
