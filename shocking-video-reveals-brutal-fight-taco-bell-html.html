    Video reveals fight at Taco Bell in Houston, Texas

    A video has surfaced showing a middle school student repeatedly kicking and punching two other students. One altercation - which involved three students from the nearby school called Sugar Grove Academy - unfolded at Taco Bell while another was on a sidewalk just outside the food chain in Houston, Texas.

    21 Apr 2024, 00:00

    A video has surfaced showing a middle school student repeatedly kicking and punching two other students.

    One altercation - which involved three students from the nearby school called Sugar Grove Academy - unfolded at Taco Bell while another was on a sidewalk just outside the food chain in Houston, Texas.

    'The girls used to be friends and then they weren't friends,' said Andrea Authorlee, whose daughter was beaten at the sidewalk. 

     Scroll down for video 

    The video shows a middle school student - dressed in a pink blouse and jeans -  repeatedly kicking and punching a fellow student. This altercation happened just outside Taco Bell in Houston, Texas and was 'instigated' by the attacker's mother

    The same girl brutally attacked another one of the students - who all attend Sugar Grove Academy - inside the local Taco Bell. The mother of the girl in purple (pictured) said: 'I would never imagine hurting someone else's child. I just can't do it.'

    Authorlee told Channel 2 that the fight began while the three students were in school. She also says the girl seen in the video pummeling the other two students was encouraged by her mother - who works at the school -  to confront the other two girls.  She apparently asked the two victims if they wanted to fight her daughter.

    Authorlee claims the woman was 'making threats ' to the other students while volunteering at the school all three girls involved attend.

    'She's there encouraging her child to assault my child,' Alice Jones - the mother of the girl beaten at Taco Bell - said.  'I would never imagine hurting someone else's child. I just can't do it.'

    The mother who 'instigated' the fight has been arrested and her daughter has received 'appropriate disciplinary action'.

    After the altercation, both girls (pictured) are suffering from headaches. The girl attacked in Taco Bell (right ) is also encountering serious health problems.  Her mother said: 'She's living in fear,' Jones said. 'She walks home from school but is scared to walk home now.'

    This is the Taco Bell branch where one of the altercations occurred, which was just a few blocks from the students' school, Sugar Grove Academy

    The Houston Independent School District - Texas's largest public school system which Sugar Grove Academy is a part of - told Channel 2: 'HISD is aware of an altercation that took place off campus between a Sugar Grove Academy parent, her child and another student.

    'Authorities were immediately notified. The parent was arrested and the student involved is receiving appropriate disciplinary action as outlined in the HISD Student Code of Conduct.'
