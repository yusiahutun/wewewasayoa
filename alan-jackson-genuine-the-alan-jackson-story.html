    08 Oct 2024, 00:00

    Alan Jackson continues to surprise fans with new music, 25 years into his career. The country singer has revealed the release of a three-album collection of music titled Genuine: The Alan Jackson Story, which will be available exclusively through Walmart on Nov. 6.

    Genuine: The Alan Jackson Story has 59 recordings from his time with Arista Nashville. Eight of the tracks in the collection are previously unreleased, including a live version of Jackson performing the Eagles' "Seven Bridges Road," a rare live rendition of "The Star Spangled Banner," as well as previously unreleased songs "Born Too Late," "If Tears Could Talk," "Wings," "Seguro Que Hell Yes," "Love Is Hard" and "Ain't Just a Southern Thing."
