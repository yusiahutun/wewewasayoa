Kansas City Chiefs' Quest for Threepeat, $1,000,000 Thrilling Season Opener Opponents Revealed

 Posted on March 10, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  502 words  |  Aldo Pusey

The stage is set for the Kansas City Chiefs to defend their back-to-back Super Bowl victories, with the full 2024 NFL schedule set for release on May 15 at 8 p.m. ET. With Patrick Mahomes leading the charge, anticipation is high for who will face the reigning champs at Arrowhead Stadium in Week 1. Let’s rank the top three thrilling opponents who could make for an electrifying season opener against the Chiefs.

Baltimore Ravens Rematch of Titans

A clash between Lamar Jackson and Patrick Mahomes would provide a captivating showdown for any football fan. Fresh off a heartbreaking loss to the Chiefs in last year’s AFC Championship Game, the Baltimore Ravens are eager to start strong this season.

Jackson, the reigning NFL MVP, will seek redemption against Mahomes in what could be a thrilling rematch between two of the best quarterbacks in the league

With a fortified roster and the addition of four-time Pro Bowl running back Derrick Henry, the Ravens are primed for a high-stakes battle with the Chiefs. This season opener would set the tone for the rest of the AFC and showcase two offensive powerhouses hungry for glory.

Cincinnati Bengals Renewed Rivalry with Joe Burrow

The Cincinnati Bengals have been a consistent thorn in the Chiefs’ side, and their heated rivalry is sure to reignite in 2024. Franchise quarterback Joe Burrow is back after recovering from wrist surgery, ready to resume his thrilling battles with Mahomes.

These teams have met in two of the last three AFC Championship games, and each encounter has been nothing short of dramatic.

When Burrow and the Bengals traveled to Kansas City last season, they almost secured a victory with backup Jake Browning leading the charge. Now, with Burrow’s return and Pro Bowl wide receiver Ja’Marr Chase declaring Kansas City’s secondary unimpressive, this Week 1 matchup could be a must-watch showdown.

Will Houston Texans Rising Stars Meet Kansas City Chiefs

The Houston Texans are on everyone’s radar after their remarkable first season under head coach Demeco Ryans and star quarterback C.J. Stroud. The 2023 AP Offensive Rookie of the Year dazzled fans with his playmaking abilities, and with the addition of Stefon Diggs and Joe Mixon, this Texans offense could pose a serious challenge to the Chiefs.

Facing the reigning Super Bowl champions at Arrowhead Stadium would allow the Texans to make a bold statement. Mahomes and Stroud have yet to face each other, and their inaugural meeting would create an electric atmosphere to kick off the 2024 season.

The countdown to the NFL schedule release is on, and football fans can’t wait to see who will face the Kansas City Chiefs in Week 1. Whether it’s the Ravens, Bengals, or Texans, any of these matchups would provide a thrilling start to the new season.
