    09 Aug 2024, 00:00

    Supermodel Candice Swanepoel stepped out in New York City this week, and demonstrated why the classic black blazer is an enduring wardrobe hero that every woman could use in her day-to-day life.

    The South African beauty, who was pictured leaving an event in Tribeca, had put an eveningwear spin on the piece of tailoring, teaming it with a pair of tiny leather shorts, a black bralette and statement heels.

    It's an ensemble that proves the versatile appeal of a black blazer, which can be styled in a myriad of ways.

    The look

    Swanepoel wore an oversized black blazer, a black bralette, leather shorts and gold chain shoes by Giuseppe Zanotti.

    Why it works

    Swanepoel's styling is a fresh take on a simple wardrobe hero, giving an edge to the tailored piece by revealing some skin and wearing it with slinky sandals, but offsetting this with an oversized fit, which helps it to feel relaxed and not overdone.

    Gotham//Getty Images

    Why it's a forever classic

    The blazer is one of the most versatile items in a women's wardrobe and is a design that will never go out of style because it is so classic and flattering – and, because it can truly be worn for any occasion, from work to the weekend, day to night.

    Where to shop it

    There are an endless number of chic black blazers available to shop right now, but here are some of our favourites; Stella McCartney's sustainable take on the classic, Jacquemus' relaxed fit, an affordable option from Vince – and a true investment piece in the form of Dior's Bar Jacket.

    How to style it

    For the evening, take your cure from Swanepoel and flash some flesh, teaming your oversized black blazer with shorts or a mini dress. Alternatively, dress the look down by wearing with jeans and trainers, or make it officewear-appropriate by styling your blazer with some matching tailored trousers and a simple white T-shirt, as expertly demonstrated here by the Duchess of Sussex.
