Stunning Fan Art Makes Us Wish Millie Bobby Brown Was Supergirl

 Posted on July 13, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  536 words  |  Aldo Pusey

The Rise of Superhero Cinema

Superhero narratives have undoubtedly taken the cinematic world by storm. From riveting action sequences to complex character arcs, the audience has embraced the unfolding drama of their favorite comic book icons. At the center of this ever-expanding universe stands DC, with fresh leadership and vision in the form of co-CEOs James Gunn and Peter Safran. Their mission? To redefine and reintroduce some of the most iconic characters of the DC realm.

Who’s the Next Supergirl?

Speculation runs rampant as fans eagerly discuss potential actors for pivotal roles in upcoming DC productions. Amidst the noise, one particular name echoes louder: Millie Bobby Brown. And if you think the “Stranger Things” starlet might be an unlikely choice for the cape-wearing Kryptonian, a recent fan art portrayal might change your mind.

“I had no idea I was interested in a Millie Bobby Brown Supergirl until I saw stunning fan art, and now I’m completely sold.”

A Fresh Take on a Familiar Face

While Sasha Calle’s portrayal of Supergirl in “The Flash” received accolades, DC’s upcoming plans hint at a new narrative direction, offering the possibility for a fresh face. Enter Millie Bobby Brown, whose powerful performance as Eleven has already showcased her depth and versatility as an actress. A glance at the fan-created rendition reveals her sporting the iconic Supergirl attire – and it’s nothing short of striking.

Artistry Behind the Vision

The mesmerizing portrayal of Millie as Supergirl comes courtesy of Buffy2ville, an Instagram-based digital artist. Garnering a following of over 8k, the artist frequently delves into DC’s realm, offering fans a glimpse into what could be. This specific piece not only brings out Millie’s inherent strength but also presents a surprising avatar of the actress: blonde. Although Millie did once don a wig in “Stranger Things”, this rendition gives a whole new dimension to her look.

“Aside from the fact that Millie Bobby Brown looks awesome in Supergirl’s suit and cape, this image is sure to turn a few heads because we’ve never seen the Stranger Things star as a blonde.”

The Future of the DC Universe

As DC gears up to launch “Superman: Legacy” and the intriguing “Gods and Monsters” series, fans are left wondering where Supergirl fits in. The possibility of Millie Bobby Brown stepping into the role becomes even more plausible considering the conclusion of her stint with “Stranger Things“. With the series coming to a close, this might just be the perfect opportunity for Millie to soar high as the next Supergirl.

In Anticipation

As we await more announcements from DC and speculate on potential castings, there’s no denying the excitement that such fan-made renditions bring. Whether or not we see Millie Bobby Brown don the Supergirl cape, the fandom’s enthusiasm is a testament to the love and anticipation for the world of superheroes.

“The next DC movie hitting theaters is Aquaman and the Lost Kingdom on December 20th. In the meantime, check out the 2023 movie release dates to plan your next movie experience.”

So, as the lines between fan fiction and reality blur, one thing remains certain: the world of DC has plenty of surprises up its sleeve, and we’re here for all of it.
