The Soldier Statuette is another short secondary quest available in the city of Novigrad.

Key Facts

Location:Novigrad
Category:Side Quest

Walkthrough

Following contains quest spoilers. You’ve been warned

Talk to Triss about the statuette.

In the Secondary quest "A Dangerous Game", you will come into possession of a Jade Figurine in Caesar Bilzen’s townhouse close to Hierarch Square. Take the figurine to Triss.

She can be found in one of the following two sites:

  • The Crooked House – her hideout in The Bits
  • The Rosemary and Thyme
Talk to her and ask her to look at the figurine. She will inspect it for a moment and tell you that there is a soldier magically trapped inside it.

She will take you into The Bits, where you will ask her to lift the spell. A surprised Niflgaardian soldier will then appear and start talking about about a battle fought long ago.

It appears that he was trapped inside the figurine by Coral – the sorceress Lytta Neyd. Tell him to go and report to the van Attres, and the quest will be completed.
