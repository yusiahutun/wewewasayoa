    Best Striped Sweater Overall

    Kule The Betty

    Best Striped Sweater Overall

    Kule The Betty

    Credit: Kule

    Kule’s striped sweaters have a habit of working their way into my conversations with other editors. It’s half because Kule has one of the widest ranges of stripe colors to choose from, half because the sweaters keep their fresh-from-the-box quality even after dozens of wears. (The same standard applies to the brand’s best T-shirts.) Kule’s stripes have even landed on Oprah’s Favorite Things list.

    Sizes: XS–XL

    Colors: Cream/navy, navy/cream, black/cream, heather gray/navy, cream multi, and more

    Best Button-Front Striped Sweater

    Sézane Léotine Sweater

    Best Button-Front Striped Sweater

    Sézane Léotine Sweater

    Credit: Sézane

    Sézane thrives at understated basics with a little je ne sais quoi. The Léotine Breton striped sweater is a perfect example. It’s knit from organic cotton with a relaxed (but not oversize) fit, and comes in the most timeless stripe variations with an ecru base. I wear mine with at least the first few buttons undone—and I like to think Jane Birkin would approve.

    Sizes: XXS–XXXL

    Colors: Ecru/camel, ecru/navy, navy/ecru, ecru/coral

    Best Multicolor Striped Sweater

    Christopher John Rogers Striped Sweater

    Best Multicolor Striped Sweater

    Christopher John Rogers Striped Sweater

    Credit: Net-a-Porter

    Christopher John Rogers landed on the 2021 Bazaar 100 power list with a rainbow-striped knit dress. Two years later, I’m just as obsessed with the designer’s vibrant pullover sweaters. Here, a version from the latest collection that can bring sunshine to a cloudy winter day.

    Sizes: XS–XXL

    Color: Yellow multi

    Best Convertible Striped Sweater

    Alex Mill Button-Back Crewneck Sweater

    Best Convertible Striped Sweater

    Alex Mill Button-Back Crewneck Sweater

    Credit: Alex Mill

    Alex Mill is one of my favorite brands for timeless classics like a navy-and-ecru striped sweater. But there’s a playful catch: This sweater can actually be worn at least three ways, with its line of buttons done all the way up in the back, unbuttoned partially to the front, or totally undone as a layering cardigan. I’ll also add a fourth method to the mix: tying these stripes over a second ivory sweater for some stripe-on-stripe action.

    Sizes: XS–XL

    Colors: Dark navy/ivory, ivory/dark navy

    What reviewers are saying: “Perfect 2 in 1 sweater! Such a comfy and stylish piece! I have this sweater in navy as well and have gotten numerous compliments on both colorways!”

    Best Polo Striped Sweater

    Reformation Beckie Stripe Cashmere Sweater

    Best Polo Striped Sweater

    Reformation Beckie Stripe Cashmere Sweater

    Now 70% Off

    Credit: Nordstrom

    It was love at first sight when I first came across this sweater’s thin stripes and neutral hues. Reviewers’ notes about how perfectly proportioned its cropped hem and V neckline are only made it more of a must-have.

    Sizes: XS–XL

    Colors: Barley/black, panko

    What reviewers are saying: “The material is very soft and drapes well, while the ribbing at the bottom helps to keep it from being shapeless and is actually pretty flattering. The V neck isn’t too low either!”

    Best Oversize Crewneck Striped Sweater

    Guizio Oversized Crewneck

    Best Oversize Crewneck Striped Sweater

    Guizio Oversized Crewneck

    Credit: Guizio

    Get comfortable and colorful in this crewneck by Guizio. Its longer long sleeves and dreamy 1970s colorways have the range to pair with everything from the label’s insider-favorite jeans to a party-ready pailette miniskirt.

    Sizes: XS/S, M/L

    Colors: Green multi, orange multi, blue multi

    Best Striped Cardigan

    Everlane SuperFuzz Alpaca Cardigan

    Best Striped Cardigan

    Everlane SuperFuzz Alpaca Cardigan

    Now 30% Off

    Credit: Everlane

    Ask me to imagine my dream winter outfit, and I’ll picture something like this Everlane striped cardigan with a black turtleneck underneath and wide-leg jeans to finish the look. It has the fuzzy handfeel and slightly oversize fit of a reliable desk sweater—crafted from responsibly sourced alpaca wool—plus a versatile palette that works in fall 2023’s reigning chocolate brown. Translation: It feels as good as it looks.

    Sizes: XXS–XL

    Colors: Bone/fudge/oatmeal, light oat, dark charcoal, bone/fuchsia pink

    Best Thin Stripe Sweater

    Acne Studios Stripe Sweater

    Best Thin Stripe Sweater

    Acne Studios Stripe Sweater

    Now 40% Off

    Credit: FWRD

    You can see the influence of a classic Breton sweater in this Acne Studios design, but it takes every traditional detail as a chance to add a twist. The stripes are unevenly placed on the oversize sleeves and torso, and they’re set above a tie-dye background with sunset tones.

    Sizes: XS–L

    Colors: Blue multi

    Best Zip-Up Striped Sweater

    Rag & Bone Pierced Stripe Cashmere Sweater

    Best Zip-Up Striped Sweater

    Rag & Bone Pierced Stripe Cashmere Sweater

    Half-zip sweaters are e-v-e-r-y-w-h-e-r-e right now, but this is the one worth shopping. It has a slightly oversize fit, cozy ribbed fabric, cool neutral stripes, and a collar that can be worn all the way zipped up or nonchalantly rolled down.

    Sizes: XS–XL

    Colors: Ivory/multi

    Best Wide Striped Sweater

    Guest in Residence Stripe Crew

    Best Wide Striped Sweater

    Guest in Residence Stripe Crew

    Credit: Guest In Residence

    There’s no denying that Guest in Residence, the knitwear label founded by Gigi Hadid, has some of fashion’s cuddliest cashmere. Now, it can add eye-catching stripes to its list of shop-worthy qualities. This striped sweater is a little collegiate rugby player, a little off-duty downtown model. The balance comes from its ultra-wide, contrasting stripes and oversize, unisex fit.

    Sizes: XXS–XXL

    Colors: Midnight/honey/cherry, magenta/cherry, forest/cobalt/midnight

    Best Contrasting Stripe Sweater

    Stine Goya Shea Stripe Sweater in Multicolour Stripe

    Best Contrasting Stripe Sweater

    Stine Goya Shea Stripe Sweater in Multicolour Stripe

    Now 50% Off

    Credit: Nordstrom

    One sweater, six variations on neutral stripes. This knit from standout Copenhagen-based brand Stine Goya is just unconventional enough to break up your rotation of basic winter knitwear. Better yet, the fit is ever-so-slightly oversize, to make a leather midi skirt or tailored pant feel like pajama dressing—and to make at-home lounging a little more elevated.

    Sizes: XXS–L

    Colors: Multicolor stripe

    What reviewers are saying: “Five stars.”

    Best Embellished Striped Sweater

    Miu Miu Embellished Striped Wool Cardigan

    Best Embellished Striped Sweater

    Miu Miu Embellished Striped Wool Cardigan

    Credit: Moda Operandi

    Miu Miu’s button-up cardigan restored my faith in the power of crystalline embellishments. With scattered gems of different sizes, it looks like fairy dust was sprinkled on this striped cardigan’s shoulders.

    Sizes: 0–6

    Colors: Gray/blue

    Most Popular Striped Sweater

    Gap 24/7 Split-Hem Turtleneck Sweater

    Most Popular Striped Sweater

    Gap 24/7 Split-Hem Turtleneck Sweater

    Credit: GAP

    Search “Gap sweater” on TikTok, and your feed will be filled with rave reviews for this striped knit. The sleeves and torso flare out ever so slightly to achieve an oversize—but not overwhelming—shape. And a style tip from the chronically online shoppers who’ve worn it first: Try scrunching the collar down instead of folding it over.

    Sizes: XXS–XXL

    Colors: Camel stripe, black/beige stripe, black/white stripe, deep green, black

    What reviewers are saying: “Very soft material feels nice on the skin. A classic look. Cannot wait to wear it.”

    Best Textured Striped Cardigan

    La Ligne Rickrack Cardigan

    Best Textured Striped Cardigan

    La Ligne Rickrack Cardigan

    Now 55% Off

    Credit: Nordstrom

    It’s impossible to be in a bad mood when you’re wearing this cardigan. La Ligne embroidered the classic collarless style with cheery rows of rainbow rickrack stripes.

    Sizes: XS–L

    Colors: Cream/rainbow

    What reviewers are saying: “Love the quality, style and fit. Super cute with a pair wide leg jeans.”

    Best Luxury Polo Striped Sweater

    Khaite Franklin Striped Cashmere Sweater

    Best Luxury Polo Striped Sweater

    Khaite Franklin Striped Cashmere Sweater

    Credit: Net-a-Porter

    Necklaces and rings aren’t the only fashion items you can stack. When you’re piling a knit on top of another knit, and then adding a statement coat to tie it together, a base layer with a collar is a must. Specifically, splurge on Khaite’s cashmere blend with a preppy polo collar that can peek out from beneath a chunkier pullover. It also hits at just the right length to wear alone with a high-waist jean.

    Sizes: XS–XL

    Colors: White/navy

    Best Mohair Striped Sweater

    COS Striped Textured Mohair Sweater

    Best Mohair Striped Sweater

    COS Striped Textured Mohair Sweater

    When I asked senior fashion editor Tara González to share her best striped sweater, she hesitated for the beat of a hummingbird’s wings before directing me to this COS pullover. Its shaggy material will give you all the warm and fuzzies while sticking to a reliably neutral palette.

    Sizes: XS–L

    Colors: Dark gray/stripe, white/stripe

    Best Logo Stripe Sweater

    Loewe Striped Anagram Knit Sweater

    Best Logo Stripe Sweater

    Loewe Striped Anagram Knit Sweater

    Credit: Bergdorf Goodman

    There’s a lot going on with this Loewe striped sweater. The cloud blue and white stripes have varying widths, and Loewe’s anagram logo is cheekily embroidered with the hand-spun feel of a family quilt. It all comes together in a way that’s just right, and worthy of cuddling up in all winter long.

    Sizes: XS–L

    Colors: Blue/white

    Best Oversize Turtleneck Striped Sweater

    Toteme Signature Stripe Turtleneck Sweater

    Best Oversize Turtleneck Striped Sweater

    Toteme Signature Stripe Turtleneck Sweater

    Toteme’s flowy sweater, however, is the absolute best when a loose fit is your top criterion. (Even the sleeves have a semi-exaggerated flare.) To play up the shape, Badiyi suggests pairing it with “a more intentionally tailored bottom and even a bit of a heel to bring in more length and height.” My suggestion: Try this sweater with straight-leg jeans and one of Toteme’s own pointed boots.

    Sizes: XXS–XL

    Colors: Cream/black, black/cream
