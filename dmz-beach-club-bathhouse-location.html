Where is the Beach Club Bathhouse location on the Ashika Island map in DMZ? Warzone 2 Season 2 is finally here, and it comes packed with excellent content across all modes. Naturally, there are also a lot of new things to explore in DMZ, including a brand new map called Ashika Island. In this guide, we explain where to find the Beach Club Bathhouse key location in DMZ.

Beach Club Bathhouse Key DMZ Warzone 2

Where is Beach Club Bathhouse Location in Ashika Island DMZ

As with the original DMZ map Al Mazrah, Ashika Island also hides numerous new doors and locations for players to unlock and discover. Also, in line with Al Mazrah, most of these keys are entirely random to get. You’ll get them as random drops either from ground loot or eliminated enemies. One such key is the Beach Club Bathhouse key. As the name suggests, you’ll need to go to the Beach Club POI. This point of interest is located at the 7E coordinates.

The location itself is called Beach Club, and it is at the southwesternmost corner of the map. Once there, you’ll have to look for the three isolated houses directly south of the main Beach Clubhouse. There, you’ll see a building with a yellow corrugated roof. And that’s the building we’re looking for! Once there, simply approach the door with the “female toilet” sign, and you will see that you have arrived at the “Beach Club Bathhouse”. Now use your key and enter inside to loot the room. With that said, our guide is completed.
