NASCAR truck series winner Cale Gale headlines night of racing at Jennerstown

 Posted on April 7, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  80 words  |  Aldo Pusey

JENNERSTOWN — Cale Gale has competed at some of the highest levels of racing.

He's a NASCAR truck series winner. And, he has three top-ten finishes in what is now called the NASCAR Xfinity Series. Now, he's a winner in the Late Models class at Jennerstown Speedway Complex.
