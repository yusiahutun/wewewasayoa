    'I'm so hot I'm hit on 20 times a day even my doctor fancies me and it's scary'

    A model believes being good looking is a blessing and a curse. Conny Hawk says shes hit on 20 times a day, which can be uncomfortable at times. The 29-year-old, from Vienna, Austria, gets scared when male admirers react angrily to being rejected.

    26 Mar 2024, 00:00

    A model believes being good looking is a blessing and a curse.

    Conny Hawk says she’s hit on 20 times a day, which can be “uncomfortable” at times.

    The 29-year-old, from Vienna, Austria, gets scared when male admirers react angrily to being rejected.

    She said: “I try to be nice about it but it does make me feel uncomfortable 99% of the time.

    “Especially because a lot of guys are super persistent and don't give up. Sometimes when I say no they start being rude and aggressive and will say things like 'you're ugly anyway.'

    "It can be scary, especially when it's in the evening or at night time."

    Conny can’t even go for a health check-up without being flirted with.

    One time, she was horrified when her doctor made advances towards her during an appointment.

    The model recalled: “Once I had to go to hospital because I had an allergic reaction and the doctor treating me started hitting on me.

    “My ears were super swollen and red, I looked awful. As he was flirting with me I was just thinking: 'Jeesh, how is this possible?'

    "I didn't end up complaining about the doctor but I definitely won't be going back there."

    While some may think all the attention is flattering, Conny is tired of it.

    The Instagram influencer, who has more than 390,000 followers on her @connyhawk page, said: “It’ll be in grocery shops or in the gym, on the tube, just anywhere where I'm usually in a rush and don't want to be approached.

    “No one wants to be chatted up when you’re doing a sweaty workout or having a nose in your shopping basket.

    “Sometimes I'm waiting for someone and before that person arrives, three guys have already come and tried to talk to me.

    “It takes courage to approach a girl just like that but they should understand when no means no."

    Even men in relationships don’t seem to respect Conny’s boundaries.

    She said: “Once there was a couple waiting for the tube and they started kissing.

    "While he was doing that he opened his eyes and gave me a wink.

    "It made me feel sick and disgusted.

    "I can't stand it when I see guys doing that while they're with their girlfriends. I would never go out with a guy who was taken.”
