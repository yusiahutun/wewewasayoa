    Pamela Anderson, 56, has revealed how police reacted after a stalker broke into her house.

    In 2001, obsessive fan Christine Evelyn Roth, 27, was charged with a misdemeanour trespassing charge of unauthorised entry of a dwelling after she was found sleeping in one of Pamela's beds at the star's Malibu home. 

    In a new interview, Pamela - who found fame wearing the iconic red Baywatch swimsuit- has recalled how the cops asked if she wanted her red bikini back after the stalker wore it in her bed.

    The Baywatch star also revealed that the police told her she 'wasn't in much danger' because the trespasser was a woman.

    Christine was subsequently ordered to return to her native France and seek counselling.
