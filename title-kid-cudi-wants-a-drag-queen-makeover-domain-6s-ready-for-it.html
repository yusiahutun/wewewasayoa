    After Kid Cudi expressed his desire to try drag, Twitter was immediately ready to embrace the rapper’s inner queen.

    Cudi sent out a tweet to his 2.03 million followers on Wednesday (June 5) that read, “Ok so I saw Antoni from queer eye get a drag make over and i want one too. Would I look bad in drag? Hahahaha thoughts? I wanna feel fabulous man!!’

    Ok so i saw Antoni from queer eye get a drag make over and i want one too. Would i look bad in drag? Hahaha thoughts? I wanna feel fabulous man!!

    “Let’s do it…” Couleé replied.
