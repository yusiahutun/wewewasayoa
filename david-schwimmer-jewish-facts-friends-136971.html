    Calling all David Schwimmer fans! You may think you know everything about the 50-year-old former Friends star, but just in case you still have a few questions about the actor, we’ve rounded up some fascinating facts about David. Scroll down to read more!

    Is David Jewish?

    Yes, according to several outlets! Both of David’s parents, Arthur Schwimmer and Arlene Coleman-Schwimmer, are reportedly Jewish and raised their son in the faith. The actor is also thought to be of German descent.

    MORE: David Schwimmer on His ‘Friends’ Fame — “It Made Me Want to Hide Under a Baseball Cap”

    Is he married?

    Yes, sort of! David tied the knot with British artist Zoë Buckman back in June 2010. After six years of marriage, the couple announced in April 2017 that they were “taking some time apart.”

    “It is with great love, respect, and friendship that we have decided to take some time apart while we determine the future of our relationship,” the duo said in a statement. Though neither party has yet to file for divorce, the two are presumably still separated today.

    MORE: David Schwimmer Talks His Career Comeback — “I Was Feeling a Real Itch to Get Back to Work” (EXCLUSIVE)

    Does David have any kids?

    Yes, the actor is a father-of-one. He and his estranged wife, Zoë, welcomed their daugter, Cleo Buckman Schwimmer, in May 2011. In a 2016 interview, David admitted there is one Friends episode in particular that he can’t wait for his daughter to watch. “‘The One With the Holiday Armadillo’ was fun! I mean, it was really fun,” he said during a past Television Critics Association press event. “I hope to one day show my daughter that episode.” So cute!

    David with his estranged wife, Zoë, and daughter Cleo. (Photo Credit: Getty Images)

    What is his net worth?

    According to several outlets, David’s estimated net worth is $85 million thanks to his roles on Friends and American Crime Story: The People v. O. J. Simpson as well as his work as a Hollywood producer.
