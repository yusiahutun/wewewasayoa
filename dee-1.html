    Who is Dee-1:

    Dee-1 is a famous Rapper. He was born on April 10, 1989 and his birthplace is Louisiana. Dee-1 is also well known as, Southern rapper who made a name for himself in the underground rap scene with self-released mixtapes like 2008’s I Am Who I Am, 2013’s Psalms of David. He was named Artist of the Year at the 2010 NOLA Underground Hip Hop Awards.

    Dee-1 is originated from United States. He toured with prominent southern rapper Killer Mike in 2011.
