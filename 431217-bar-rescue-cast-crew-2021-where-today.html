    Since 2011, Bar Rescue has attempted to turn around the fortunes of hundreds of bars across America. Unfortunately, many bars have had their pitfalls, putting a lot of stress on the owners. From insects in liquor bottles to unpaid staff, high debt levels, and owners who do not understand what they are doing, the businesses have little going on and can shut down anytime. But Bar Rescue, through its host Jon Taffer comes in to salvage the situation before it is too late. However, Jon is not alone since many more Bar Rescue cast members have appeared in the television show for ten years.

    Bar Rescue is a reality television show that has been running on Paramount since 2011. In each episode, Jon Taffer offers his professional advice and expertise on reviving a failing bar. He also provides the necessary equipment needed by the bar owners to help them continue working instead of closing the business. Since it is a reality television show with over 200 episodes, Bar Rescue cast members are diverse, with only a few appearing in more than 50 episodes.

    Who are the celebrities on Bar Rescue?

    Bar Rescue has introduced many celebrities over the years. For example, in season six, episode 19, Jon Taffer and his crew went to Puerto Rico to rescue a family-owned beach bar after Hurricane Maria destroyed it. The episode stars many celebrities, including Mark Cuban, J.J. Barea, Bethenny Frankel, and Luis Guzmán.

    Who are the experts on Bar Rescue? Bar Rescue has different types of experts. Some of them are trainers, and others deal with customer service. In each episode, Jon comes with his team of experts to perform reconnaissance and surveillance on a struggling bar. They try to determine why a bar is struggling by looking at service and operational weaknesses.

    The experts come from different fields. For example, experts for the bar are LisaMarie Joyce, Mia Mastroianni, Russell Davis, Phil Wills, and Sean Ford. The experts in the kitchen are Aaron McCargo, Vic Vegas, Tiffany Derry, Chef Pink, Jason Santos, and Nick Liberato.

    1. Jon Taffer

    Jon Taffer, born Jonathan Peter Taffer, is an American entrepreneur and television personality. He is the host of Bar Rescue and has been with the show since the beginning. Taffer uses his knowledge and skills in the hospitality industry and business to revitalise failing bars and nightclubs in the United States.

    Taffer started his career in the 1970s as a bartender in West Hollywood. He then got into bar management in the late 1970s and was given complete control of a bar in the early 1980s. In 1989, Taffer opened his first bar. As of 2021, Taffer continues to host Bar Rescue in its eighth season. The entrepreneur lives in Las Vegas with his wife Nicole, whom he married in 2000 and appears alongside him in Bar Rescue.

    2. Nicole Taffer

    Nicole Taffer was born on 16 February 1973 in Ingleside, Illinois, United States. She is a reality television star and the wife of Jon Taffer. Nicole occasionally appears in Bar Rescue alongside her husband of 21 years. In Bar Rescue, Nicole sometimes pretends to be a mystery shopper at a failing restaurant.

    4. Russell Davis

    Russell Davis is a celebrity bartender from Austin, Texas, United States. He has appeared in many reality television shows, including The View (2015) and Bar Rescue: Back to the Bar (2015). Between 2013 and 2015, Russell appeared in Bar Rescue as an expert mixologist.

    Russell Davis is an expert in crafting cocktails. He also runs his watering hole in the Texas capital. Davis has won many awards in the hospitality industry and has an international consulting company.

    5. Phil Wills

    Phil Wills has worked in the bar business for over 15 years. He has lots of experience working for different establishments, including corporate restaurants, nightclubs, and independent local bars. In Bar Rescue, Phil is a trainer and an experienced mixologist.

    Wills joined the show in 2015 and became a series regular, appearing in seasons 3, 4, 5, and 8. In 2011, he started a consultancy firm known as The Spirits In Motion. As of 2021, Wills still runs the establishment located in Los Angeles, California, United States.

    6. P.J. King

    P.J. King was born in Connecticut, United States. He is a veteran and versatile actor but well known in the entertainment circles as a voiceover actor. In Bar Rescue, P.J. King is the narrator of the entire show, beginning from the first episode.

    As of 2021, King continues to work on Bar Rescue. He lives in Los Angeles and works on the East Coast and elsewhere.

    Tom Bonello was born in Leechburg, Pennsylvania, United States and moved to Las Vegas in 2000 and spent seven years working as a professional player. Bonello has appeared in over 30 movies and TV shows as an additional crew. In 2016, he made his appearance in Bar Rescue as and the team’s bus driver.

    8. Peter O’Connor

    Peter O’Connor was among the first experts to appear in Bar Rescue in 2011. His speciality on the show is Whiskey, and he has worked alongside Jon Taffer to help other bar owners improve their service. Although his work was outstanding, he did not appear in many episodes and was subsequently absent in 2013. As of 2021, O’Connor works as the European Brand Ambassador of Roe & Co Irish Whiskey.

    9. Jessie Barnes

    Jessie Barnes was born on 21 July 1991 in Chicago, United States. She started her career as a manager of her father’s nightclub. The knowledge and experience of working at a nightclub and in the service industry led her to join Job Taffer in Bar Rescue.

    Barnes first appeared in Bar Rescue in 2012 as a bottle service expert. Her work is to train the parties on service and also give her input on renovations. As of 2021, Jessie Barnes is no longer in Bar Rescue.

    10. Joseph Brooke

    Joseph Brooke was born on 3 July 1979 in Suffern, New York, United States. He is an actor and TV personality known for appearing in many short films and reality television shows. Between 2009 and 2010, he appeared in the TV show On the Rocks: The Search for America's Top Bartender as a contestant and later as a judge.

    In 2012, Brooke appeared in Bar Rescue as a mixology expert. His work was to train bartenders on the best ways of preparing cocktails and improving service. Apart from reality TV, Brooke appeared in the television series Numb3rs in 2006 as a bartender. As of 2021, Joseph Brooke lives in Los Angeles, where he works in the hospitality industry.
