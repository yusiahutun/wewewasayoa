    Kanye West and his wife Bianca Censori have made shockwaves over their risqué outfits and despite the latter’s recent modest style, she’s receiving backlash for looking like a toilet unblocker.

    New year, new Ye? The rapper, formerly known as Kanye West, is currently on an Instagram streak with his recent string of posts dedicated to his wife Bianca Censori. Perhaps it’s a New Year resolution to increase his social media engagement. Whatever the reason, leave it to Ye to have the world gossiping about his uploads with photos of Censori in nearly naked outfits.

    Kanye’s wife Bianca is giving ‘toilet plunger’ chic

    In an unexpected turn of events, Bianca, 29, sported a more covered-up fashion look than usual. Ditching the previous thong leotards and corsets with minimal material to cover her chest, Censori opted for a monochrome outfit of a black latex balaclava-like headpiece exposing only her face, along with a latex coat.

    The style matched the all-black interior of the large vehicle she was busily driving. It’s not the outfit we imagine while behind the wheel, but it’s probably just another day in the West household.

    Fans compared the vehicle to the Batmobile, while more than 25,000 users agreed Bianca is “dressed like a plunger”. Now that’s one comparison we’ve never heard before.

    Yeezy designer Bianca sported the same latex head-covering in previous photos, though she paired it with a nude leotard that bared her derrière and side boob.

    See Bianca before marriage to Kanye

    Bianca was thrown into the public eye after media claimed the pair secretly tied the knot in a non-legal ceremony in January 2023 as they had yet to file for a license. Daily Mail confirmed in October the Wests attained a confidential marriage license in December 2022, one month after his divorce from Kim Kardashian was finalized.

    It’s been one year of wacky outfits and questionable Venice boat ride antics, so you may have forgotten what Bianca looked like before the Ye makeover.
