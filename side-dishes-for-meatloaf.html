    Meatloaf is a comfort food classic, and for good reason: One bite, and it’s like we’re instantly transported back to grandma’s warm and inviting kitchen. And what would meatloaf be without the sides! Creamy mashed potatoes, cheesy casseroles … they only make the meal more delicious. So while that meatloaf is cooking, why not get creative with its pairings? We at the First for Women test kitchen have gathered our best meatloaf recipe, along with the most delicious side options. Scroll through to find your perfect match!

    Our best-ever meatloaf recipe

    There are so many ways to make meatloaf, and here’s the favorite we always return to in the First for Women test kitchen. We used two kinds of meat to make it extra-flavorful, while a hint of mustard gives it a little kick.

    Mustard-Glazed Meatloaf

    Feel free to tweak the seasonings however you’d like, and try different glazes. We like using chili sauce or barbecue sauce, or you could top with marinara and shredded cheese just before broiling. For a spicy kick, try our recipe for Tuscan Heat Spice Mix to stir in. And then there’s classic just plain ketchup!
