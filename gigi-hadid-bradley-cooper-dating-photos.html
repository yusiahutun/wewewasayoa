    A romance indeed seems to be blooming between Gigi Hadid and Bradley Cooper. The two were spotted out together again over the weekend and looked right at home in each other’s company.

    Hadid and Cooper were seen driving into New York City together Sunday with light luggage, after seemingly going on a weekend getaway—just days after they were photographed on a dinner date at Via Carota in the West Village.

    Hadid wore a low-key fall look composed of a cream-colored crewneck sweatshirt, straight-leg gray jeans, black leather ballet flats with a tiny bow in the front by Mansur Gavriel, and a small beige leather pouch bag. She accessorized with a gold chain necklace, gold hoop earrings, and brown-tinted sunglasses. The model kept her makeup to a minimum and wore her hair in a messy ballerina bun tied with a scrunchy. She was seen getting a large Louis Vuitton duffel bag and a woven Prada tote bag out of the trunk as Cooper dropped her off at her apartment.

    TheImageDirect.com

    Cooper also stayed casual in a light blue T-shirt from the One Tribe Foundation, blue jeans, black sneakers, a black-and-gray hat, and dark sunglasses. He was seen carrying a duffel bag and a backpack.

    The actor and the model’s budding romance comes after Hadid was briefly linked to Leonardo DiCaprio as recently as last month. Cooper, meanwhile, has had an on-and-off relationship with model Irina Shayk, who is also the mother of his child. Shayk, however, recently moved on with Gisele Bündchen’s ex-husband, Tom Brady.

    Neither Hadid nor Cooper have publicly spoken of their relationship.
