What is Debi Mazar’s net worth? Who is Debi Mazar’s husband?

Introduction

Debi Mazar is an American actress who performed in Goodfellas, Entourage, Singles, Malcolm X, Batman Forever, and Little Man Tate.

Biography

She was born on August 13, 1964, in Queens, New York City, New York, USA, as Deborah Anne Mazar.

Her father, Harry Mazar, was born in Latvia.

Her parents annulled their marriage shortly after she was born. ”I didn’t have a conventional upbringing, that’s for sure,” Debi said in an interview. ”My mom had me at 15. She was single for a while, very strong, independent, kind of my hero.”

Friendship with Madonna

She is a close friend of Madonna. They met in a nightclub located in New York City. Later, Madonna hired Debi to do her makeup for the music video for “Everybody.”

”I met her when she came to New York, and we became dear friends, and then she was like, ‘I’m gonna do a video,’ and I was like, ‘I do makeup. I’ll do your makeup,’” Mazar explained for HuffPost in 2015. She added: ”And it was very much like that, and then she became a huge star.”

@Getty Images

”When we met, she kind of looked like a punk rock midwestern girl,” Debi recalled. ”I plucked her eyebrows a little bit and gave her eyeliner.”

Mazar also appeared in music videos by Madonna for the songs Justify My Love, Deeper and Deeper, True Blue, Papa Don’t Preach, and Music.

In 1984, Mazar made her first television appearance on the TV show ”Graffiti Rock.”

In 1988, she made the makeup and hair for David Mamet’s satire Speed-the-Plow.

Goodfellas

In 1990, Mazar made her film debut in Martin Scorsese’s gangster classic Goodfellas as Ray Liotta’s junkie mistress.

The film also stars Robert De Niro, Paul Sorvino, Lorraine Bracco, and Joe Pesci. It grossed more than $47 million on the box office. The film is based on New York reporter Nicholas Pileggi’s book Wiseguy.

”I think what we had to snort was called inositol, which is a laxative,” Mazar said on PeopleTV’s Couch Surfing regarding what was in the white powder she and Ray Liotta snorted on set. ”It felt really good.”

In 1992, she portrayed Peg in Malcolm X.

In 1995, Debi performed as one half of Sugar & Spice alongside Drew Barrymore in Batman Forever.

From 2004 to 2011, Mazar performed as Shauna in 50 episodes of the TV series ”Entourage.”

In 2008, Debi made a guest appearance in one episode of Law & Order: Special Victims Unit.

In 2014, Mazar started to appear in the Cooking Channel series, Extra Virgin.

In August 2017, Mazar appeared in the ABC game show The $100,000 Pyramid.

Husband

On March 16, 2002, Debi Mazar married Gabriele Corcos.

@Getty Images

Actress Ellen Burstyn was their officiant.

The two first met in 2001 in Florence.

”He asked me to have a cigarette in the piazza,” Mazar told Tasting Table. She went on to say: ”We ended up talking for three hours and came back to the party as changed people.”

”We had a hot summer, love, and that’s always a big gamble,” Gabriele said. ”She was passing by for two nights, and we just happened to meet each other at the right time.”

Less than a year later, they were married.

@Getty Images

Note – Corcos is an Italian celebrity cook and the producer of Extra Virgin on the Cooking Channel. He was honored Knight by the Italian President of the Republic in 2019.

In 2014, the couple published a cookbook titled – ”Recipes & Love From Our Tuscan Kitchen.” The cookbook contains 120 recipes.

Debi and Gabriele also hosted an internet show, Under The Tuscan Gun.

Mazar previously dated actor Paul Reubens, who credits her for ending the depression resulting from his 1991 arrest for masturbating in an adult theater.
