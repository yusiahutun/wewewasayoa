Destiny 2 Prestige Leviathan Raid Armor Drops &amp; Perks

 Posted on September 14, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  464 words  |  Larita Shotwell

The Destiny 2 Leviathan Raid doesn’t yet offer the option to complete it on Prestige difficulty, but it will soon. It’ll be way more difficult than the normal raid, but the challenge just makes it more enticing for hard-boiled Guardians. Naturally, the loot you’ll get for completing the Leviathan Raid on Prestige will be there to sweeten the deal. You’ll be able to get the Nohr, Feltroc and Sekris Legendary armor, depending on your class, and they come with some sweet perks.

Leviathan Raid Nohr, Feltroc, Sekris Legendary Armor Sets & Perks

The Leviathan Raid has been in full swing for a good while now. Many players have already beaten its trials and defeated the exiled Emperor Calus. Naturally, they’ve collected the luscious Leviathan Raid loot – armor, weapons, Calus tokens, and other stuff. However, if you truly master it and beat it on Prestige difficulty, once Bungie makes that mode available, you’ll be able to pick up even better Legendary gear. Every class will get a set of armor – Nohr Armor for Titans, Feltroc Armor for Hunters, and Sekris Armor for Warlocks. These armors have been floating around, but they haven’t appeared as drops just yet. Here’s which armor pieces come in the sets and their perks:
