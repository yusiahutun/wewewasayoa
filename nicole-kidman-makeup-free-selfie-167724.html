    01 Sep 2024, 00:00

    She’s letting her hair down! In a gorgeous new selfie posted by Nicole Kidman via Instagram on Monday, Oct. 8, the 51-year-old Big Little Lies actress can be seen going makeup-free during a visit to Cabo San Lucas, Mexico.

    “Hanging in Cabo ?,” Nicole captioned the beautiful pic and her followers, of course, thought she looked beyone stunning in the snap. “Beautiful! Love your hair like this!” one fan commented and another similarly added, “You are radiant!!” A third even wrote, “You never age, @NicoleKidman!”

    Now that she’s in her 50s, Nicole has admitted she’s no longer taking anything in life for granted. In the September 2017 issue of Vogue, she recalled how much her life has changed now that she’s a bit older. “Everything becomes more meaningful. [My family] is the thing that gives me more joy and is the basis for everything,” she said. 

    Though she’s a successful actress, Nicole revealed she would rather spend all of her free time just hanging out with her husband, Keith Urban, and their young daughters, Sunday, 10, and Faith, 7, than to go to Hollywood parties. “We’re a very tight-knit family,” she explained. “We get anxious if we’re separated more than two days. We try to have sit-down dinners every night we’re here. Friends from school come over. Sometimes we’ll have nine kids for dinner. I can live out my fantasy of having lots of children.”

    Nicole is also a proud mom to her two adult kids, son Connor Cruise, 23, and daughter Isabella Cruise, 25, that she shares with her ex-husband, Tom Cruise. “I’m very, very happy I have that,” Nicole said about her large family. “I’ve wanted that for a long time. It’s the thing that gives me more joy than anything and is the basis of it all for me.”
