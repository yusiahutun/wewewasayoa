Disney Villainous 2024 Reboot: Slimmer, More Affordable Expansions

 Posted on February 23, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  410 words  |  Larita Shotwell

A Fresh Take on Beloved Franchises

Ravensburger has set the board game community abuzz with the announcement of a new format for its acclaimed Villainous series, set to debut in 2024. This strategic move is not just a design revamp but comes with an attractive new price point that is sure to draw in both long-time fans and new players. The publisher has revealed plans for two stand-alone expansions, referred to as “expandalones,” in both the Disney Villainous series and the Star Wars Villainous spinoff.

Streamlined Adventures with Disney and Star Wars

Disney Villainous: Sugar and Spite

The Disney Villainous expansion, titled “Disney Villainous: Sugar and Spite,” promises a delightful twist with characters King Candy from Wreck-it Ralph and Shere Khan from The Jungle Book. Slated for release in June, this expansion brings the essence of mischief and strategy at a new price of $19.99. Fans can look forward to a special edition with candy-themed packaging and an exclusive finish on King Candy’s mover, available exclusively at Target.

Mysterious Expansions in a Galaxy Far, Far Away

The Star Wars Villainous universe is also expanding with a new expansion set to launch in the summer of 2024. While the title and details remain shrouded in mystery, anticipation is high for what characters and stories will unfold in this beloved galaxy.

Introduction to Evil: A Gateway to Villainy

Disney Villainous Makes a Comeback

In addition to the new expansions, Ravensburger is set to re-introduce the base game with “Disney Villainous: Introduction to Evil.” This August release is a reimagined version of the original 2018 Disney Villainous: The Worst Takes it All. Tailored as a more accessible entry with streamlined gameplay and content, this version features iconic villains Maleficent, Captain Hook, Ursula, and Prince John. Priced at $29.99, it’s designed to be an enticing option for those new to the game or looking for a more concise version of the classic.

A Year of Villainous Ventures Awaits

As 2024 approaches, the anticipation for the new Villainous expansions grows. Ravensburger’s commitment to innovation, coupled with a nod to affordability, signals an exciting year for board game enthusiasts. Whether delving into the wicked worlds of Disney or Star Wars, or introducing friends to the joys of villainy through the streamlined base game, these new offerings promise hours of engaging gameplay and strategy. Keep an eye out for these villainous ventures, as they are poised to bring fresh excitement and challenges to game nights everywhere.
