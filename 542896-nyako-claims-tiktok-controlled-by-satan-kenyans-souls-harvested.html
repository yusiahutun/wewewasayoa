    TikToker Nyako has voiced a stark warning to the Kenyan TikTok community.

    Why should Kenyan TikTokers be careful?

    In a video that has since gained traction, the self-proclaimed president of TikTok cautioned that the platform may become perilous for its users.

    The TikToker, who recently made headlines, suggested that the devil could infiltrate the platform disguised as enticing offerings such as money.

    Her claim is that these temptations will not be recognised for what they truly are; instead, they will be celebrated and welcomed by the masses.
