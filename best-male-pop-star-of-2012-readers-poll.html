Best Male Pop Star of 2012 Readers Poll

 Posted on July 10, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  261 words  |  Larita Shotwell

It's been a very big debate as to who could be classified as the Best Male Pop Star of 2012, so we were hoping you, the readers, can settle this one for us! So far in 2012, males like the members of One Direction, Chris Brown, Justin Bieber, Adam Levine of Maroon 5 and the guys of the Wanted have been blowing up the charts and invading our brains with their catchy tunes.

When we weren't busy trying to get songs like 1D's 'What Makes You Beautiful' and Bieber's 'Boyfriend' out of ears, we've been grooving along to Usher's sweet sounds, shuffling the night away to the party jams of LMFAO, singing along to Bruno Mars, trying to rap with Lil Wayne (most unsuccessfully) or dancing along to the riveting vocals of Adam Lambert. Speaking of dance music, Big Sean had us bouncing our booties to his hit singles, as did fellow rappers B.o.B, Drake, Flo Rida and Pitbull.

Meanwhile, on the teen pop front, Jaden Smith has been dropping solo songs in 2012, while Tyler James Williams (who you probably know from 'Everybody Hates Chris') has also been finding success in music. Austin Mahone is another newcomer whose been making waves in pop this year, while Sammy Adams has been climbing the charts with his catchy hip-pop sound.

There are a ton of dudes for you to choose from on this list, so be sure to cast your vote (you can vote every hour) for the artist you think deserves the title of Best Male Pop Star of 2012.
