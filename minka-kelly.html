    Minka Kelly whose birth name is Minka Dufay Kelly is an American actress and model famously known for her roles in the movies “Friday night lights” and “the roommate”. She is also a businesswoman and owns a lifestyle brand named ABLE.

    Early Life

    Minka was born on 24th June 1980, Los Angeles, California. She is of mixed ethnicity as she has French, Irish, Dutch as well as Indonesian ancestry. Her father is a former Aerosmith guitarist Rick Dufay and in the late Maureen Dumont Kelly. Minka had to grow up with her mother because her parents separated early when she was young. In order to take responsibility to take care of Minka, her mother had to do various jobs and move to various communities. They finally settled in Albuquerque, New Mexico when Minka was in Junior high. Her mother died in 2008 due to colon cancer. Minka’s grandfather Richard Ney was also a famous actor in his time.

    There is not much information available about Minka’s education and background but it is believed that she completed her elementary to high school education at Valley high school in Albuquerque, New Mexico. After completing high school, Minka moved to Los Angeles where she was offered to work as a receptionist at a surgeon’s office who would provide her a breast surgery in exchange for her hours of work. She later refused and went on to attend a medical school to become a surgical assistant. Although the name of the school has not been disclosed. She also trained at Pflugerville high school for her role in Friday night lights. Due to her interest in cooking and baking, she took up a culinary course in New school of Cooking in Culver City, California in 2015.

    Minka Kelly Career

    Minka started working as a receptionist at a surgeon’s clinic but later left to pursue studies. She later secured a job as a scrub technician and also kept auditioning for various acting roles. Minka made her debut as an extra in 2003 with the short film The Turbo Charged Prelude which was a six-minute introduction to 2Fast 2 Furious movies. Later she gave a brief appearance on the American show, Cracking up. She also appeared in other shows like American dreams, Entourage, Drake, and josh, etc. In the show, What I like about you, she secured a role for three episodes.

    Minka rose to fame when she appeared as a cheerleader in the show Friday night lights in 2006. Her performance was highly praised. The same year she starred in the horror movie The pumpkin Karver. In 2009 she appeared in the final scene of 500 days of summer. She also appeared in the show Body Politic but the sown did not run for long. Again, she appeared in the show Mad love but as the show started picking up, she was replaced. In the following years she appeared in various tv shows and movies like “Parenthood”, “Entourage”, “charlie’s angels”, “The kingdom”, “just go with it”, “the butler” etc.

    Fame

    In 2011, she appeared in the movie “the roommate” in the role of Sara Matthews. The movie was well appreciated and accolated and was a huge success at the box office. She was later cast in the lead role in a mystery-comedy movie Searching for sonny. She also appeared in the music video of the Maroon 5 song “one more night”. In 2017, she was announced to be cast in the role of Dawn Granger in the DC universe show Titans and the following year she appeared in the video game Detroit: become human as the right-hand woman of Markus. Currently, Minka ventured into business and opened a lifestyle brand under the name ABLE. The brand focuses on ending generational poverty by working with women who have often overcome extraordinary circumstances.
