York PSP arrest people engaging in intercourse with animals, recording it

 Posted on July 16, 2024  |  1 minutes  |  176 words  |  Jenniffer Sheldon

The Criminal Investigation Unit belonging to the Pennsylvania State Police in York executed a search warrant in Codorus Township on July 11, 2023, after conducting an ongoing investigation against the residents.

While executing the search warrant, police seized a Micro SD digital storage device for a Samsung Pro 256 bedroom camera.

When officials reviewed the contents of the SD card, video evidence was recovered that depicted three individuals: Ryan Peters, Jadzia Martin, and Britney Martin.

These three individuals were engaged in approximately an hour-and-a-half of ill-treatment of a dog.

More specifically, the three accused were engaging in conduct involving sexual intercourse, according to police documents, with the dog.

According to the video timestamps, this offense occurred on July 9, 2023.

According to documents, Ryan Peters and Britney Martin engaged in sexual intercourse with the dog.

Jadzia Martin, conspired in the course of conduct of sexual intercourse with the dog by physically manipulating their legs.

39-year-old Ryan Peters and 25-year-old Jadzia were arrested earlier this year, when they were accused of sexually assaulting a 16-year-old girl.
