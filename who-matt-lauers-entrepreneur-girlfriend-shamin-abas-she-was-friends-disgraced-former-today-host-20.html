    Shamin Abas works in the luxury industry

    Shamin Abas is an experienced businesswoman who works in the luxury sector. According to her company website, she has been running her eponymous experiential marketing and communications firm for 18 years. Her clients have included Ferrari, Maserati, Rolls-Royce and Aston Martin, famous chef Daniel Boulud, Christie’s auction house, and space travel company Space Perspective.

    She has co-founded multiple companies

    Abas seems to have a thing for high-end cars. As seen on her website profile, she is also co-founder of The Bridge – a yearly, invitation-only classic car exhibition, and is a co-founder at CarCoterie, which debuted its first classic car event on Florida’s Amelia Island last year.

    Besides her companies Shamin Abas, The Bridge and CarCoterie, she also co-founded The Ultra-High-Net-Worth Symposium and sits on The Luxury Council at NYU Stern, according to her website.

    As you might expect, working in the luxury event space has given Abas access to plenty of high-net-worth clients over the years, including celebrities. She was pictured with Kimora Lee Simmons’ ex-husband Russell Simmons at a 2006 Baby Phat party, been papped alongside Gossip Girl’s Kelly Rutherford, and at a The Bridge event with Jon Bon Jovi in 2o22, to name a few.

    She’s been married twice

    Before dating Lauer, Abas was married twice, per People. According to the source, her first marriage was to a Detroit-based dentist, and she later also tied the knot with nightclub entrepreneur Frank Cilione.

    How did Shamin Abas meet Matt Lauer?

    According to People, Abas and Lauer met over two decades ago through work and became friends, but didn’t start dating until 2019. News of their relationship came after his divorce from ex-wife Annette Roque the same year.
