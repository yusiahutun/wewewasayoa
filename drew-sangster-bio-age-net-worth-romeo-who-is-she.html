    While some celebs let the whole world know they’re about to be a parent, others only drop the bomb after their newborn arrives in this world. Well, the rapper Romeo and his girlfriend Drew Sangster certainly were the latter one. The duo kept their pregnancy a secret until their “Baby R” was born in Feb 2022.

    So, who is Romeo’s baby-mother/girlfriend? Let’s learn all about her as this Drew Sangster Bio proceeds. 

    Romeo And Drew Sangster’s Relationship

    Drew Sangster and Romeo made their relationship official in November 2020 after sharing an interview with The Shade Room.

    “I’ve been single-single for like four years and I told God no matter how good I think someone is, I’ll let him reveal who he knows is good for me. My prayers always consist of clear signs. I know I had to be still and observant.” Romeo opened up.

    And to explain how it started, the rapper added, “We’ve been cool for a long time. But after sending me a book to read each month, going to the pandemic, handwritten letter on 138 reasons why I’m happy, simply met you and that thanksgiving. I think that was pretty clear. That was my sign… At least to me!”

    But the thing he liked the most was that it wasn’t planned.” It was… I mean… I guess the best things happen in life, they just happen,” he explained.

    The lovebirds then got pregnant the very next year and welcome a baby girl in Feb 2022. So, why did they hide their pregnancy? According to Romeo, they wanted to make sure that their daughter got here safe and healthy before making any announcements to the world “during this sacred journey.”

    Also, Romeo quoted his grandmother, “if you live long enough, you’ll realize that everything (and the best things) are on God’s time, never yours.” No wonder, he waited patiently and surprised his fans with the big news on Valentines’ day.

    Before dating Drew, Romeo was in a relationship with Normani from May 2019 to November 2019. During one of the rapper’s appearances on Nick Cannon’s radio show, he even proudly sported a t-shirt with Normani’s face on it.
