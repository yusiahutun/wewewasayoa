    Prince Harry's new fiancée's glowing engagement face

      All eyes were on Meghan Markle as she posed for her official engagement photo with Prince Harry on Monday afternoon. And the bride-to-be looked utterly stunning as she greeted the press with her Prince by her side, with her chestnut brown hair styled to perfection, and flawless make-up highlighting her radiant complexion. We spoke to Bridget O’Keeffe, who is founder of Blush + Blow London, and a make-up artist, to get her comments on Meghan's engagement look...

      The bride-to-be looked utterly stunning

      "Meghan has the most amazing flawless skin. Her big smile and dark eyes lend themselves to her classical look of timeless beauty. Fresh faced and in love was the theme of the make-up.

      Her lovely warm undertone means that radiant products and warm blushers work perfectly for her colour palette. Her look was most likely achieved with products like Laura Mercier's radiant primer, Jane Iredale's Liquid Mineral foundation with a dash of Charlotte Tilbury's Wonderglow mixed in with it. Meghan's barely-there coverage compliments her lips which have a lick of sheer glossy lipstick. Her cheeks are warmed up with a peach blusher in a colour similar to NARS's Orgasm."

      The happy couple beamed for the cameras at the official photocall 

      We know that the stunning former actress favours a range of reasonably-priced beauty products, including MAC's Teddy eyeliner and Nivea Body Firming lotion, which is priced at just under £4! It will be interesting to see what beauty look she opts for on her big day, which is set to take place next spring, Kensington Palace has confirmed.
