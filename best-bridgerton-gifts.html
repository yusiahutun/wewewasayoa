13 Bridgerton gifts: What to buy for fans who burn for the show

 Posted on September 23, 2024  |  6 minutes  |  1123 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

    Bridgerton season three part two is now on Netflix - but we don't need to tell you that, because it's all everyone is talking about. I binged the first four episodes and plan on doing the same for the next part - don't tell me I'm alone in that? I'm a fully paid up member of the 'Polin' club, and can't wait to see what drama unfolds between Colin (Luke Newton) and Penelope (Nicola Coughlan). 

    I definitely missed the 'ton, and the tea, and once this series is over I don't know how I'll cope with the Regency shaped hole I'll have in my life. One way we can all cope is by indulging in some gifts, a way to keep the Shondaland show going IRL even if you're not watching it on the TV.
