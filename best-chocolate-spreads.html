    These chocolate spreads are delicious!

      Pancake Day is almost here! Colder weather equals comfort food, and there's nothing sweeter – quite literally – than indulging in a spoonful of chocolate spread. Whether you're looking to add something extra to your morning slice of toast or searching for the perfect pancake topping, there are plenty of sumptuous treats ready to shop from Marks & Spencer, Lindt, Cadbury's, and more.

      From salted caramel to white chocolate, vegan alternatives to raspberry unicorn spread, this roundup is full of the best and boldest chocolatey jars, just in time for Pancake Day.

      Marks & Spencer Milk & Cookies Chocolate Spread

      M&S Milk & Cookies Chocolate Spread, £2.65, Ocado

      Marks & Spencer's Milk & Cookies Chocolate Spread sounds incredible! Grab a jar for £2.65!

      Cadbury Crunchie Chocolate Spread

      Cadbury Crunchie Spread, £5.50, Amazon

      SHOP NOW

      Anyone with a sweet tooth will love this milk chocolate spread complete with honeycomb pieces. Use this crunchy mix as a cheesecake topping or get creative and blend it into a creamy milkshake.

      Lotus Caramelised Biscuit Spread

      Lotus Caramelised Biscuit Spread, £2, Morrisons

      SHOP NOW

      Ok, so it's not exactly a chocolate spread but we couldn't leave out Lotus' Biscoff spread! Sweet and crunchy, it's also suitable for vegans & vegetarians.

      Vego Fine Hazelnut Crunchy Chocolate Spread

      Vego Hazlenut Chocolate Spread, £3.99, Holland & Barrett

      SHOP NOW

      Calling all vegans! This dairy-free spread is fair trade and organic, plus you can reuse the container as a drinking glass with the Vego logo. Spread this crunchy hazelnut mix into porridge or onto a pancake for maximum deliciousness. 

      Cadbury Caramel Chocolate Spread

      Cadbury Caramel Chocolate Spread, from £6.99, Amazon

      SHOP NOW

      Another sumptuous addition from Cadbury's, this caramel chocolate spread is a thing of dreams! Come winter, sandwich it between biscuits and marshmallows and you'll have the ultimate fire-side smores. 

      Lindt Hazelnut Chocolate Spread

      Lindt Hazelnut Chocolate Spread, £10.90, Amazon

      SHOP NOW

      Love Lindt? The luxury chocolatier has launched a hazelnut spread. 

      ManiLife Rich Cocoa Peanut Butter 

      ManiLife Rich Cocoa Peanut Butter, £3, Ocado

      SHOP NOW

      Okay so whilst not a chocolate spread, we are still counting this one as it is just too good not to! If you are a peanut butter lover who also loves chocolate, you can't go wrong with ManiLife's cocoa peanut butter infusion - it really is as dreamy as it sounds...

      Asda Raspberry Flavour Unicorn Spread

      Raspberry Flavour Unicorn Spread, £1.50, Asda

      SHOP NOW

      Asda's Raspberry Unicorn Spread is out of this world! Extremely Instagrammable thanks to its Parisian pink colour, it's filled with fun popping candy pieces. 

      Lindt Dark Chocolate Spread

      Lindt Dark Chocolate Spread, £3.50, Amazon

      SHOP NOW

      Dark chocolate fans can add this luxurious spread to their pancakes. Polish off with strawberries and you have yourself the ultimate sweet treat. 

      Asda Duo Chocolate Spread

      Duo Chocolate Spread, £1.30, Asda

      SHOP NOW

      Perfect for Pancake Day, Asda's Duo Chocolate Swirl Spread quite literally gives you the best of both worlds as milk and white chocolate interweave in one jar. 

      Gü's Chocolate & Salted Caramel Velvety Spread

      Gü's Chocolate & Salted Caramel Velvety Spread, £2, Morrisons

      We're adding Gü's chocolate and salted caramel spread to our baskets this year.
