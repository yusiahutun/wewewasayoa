What to Know About Window Well Covers

 Posted on April 26, 2024  |  3 minutes  |  598 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

What Is a Window Well Cover? Window wells are reinforced holes in the ground around below-ground or partially below-ground basement windows. Window wells are designed to hold surrounding earth away from the windows so they can let in light and be opened for ventilation. Window well covers are sheets of material, usually metal or plastic, designed to fit over window wells and prevent people or animals from falling in. They’re also meant to keep rainwater and snow from collecting in window wells, where they could cause basement flooding. [Read More]
