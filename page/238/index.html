Live Nation, OVG To Break Ground On The New Moody Center In Austin

19 Mar 2024, 00:00

AUSTIN, TX (CelebrityAccess) — The University of Texas at Austin, the Oak View Group and Live Nation, announced plans for the official groundbreaking and start of construction on the Moody Center, a new multi-purpose arena in Austin. The new $338 million, 15,000-seat will be privately funded and is intended to replace the aging 42-year-old Frank C. Erwin Jr. Center at The University of Texas at Austin campus. When it opens, the arena will host Texas men’s and women’s basketball games, graduations, as well as university, community, high school, and other events for the Austin community, as well as concerts by nationally touring artists.
