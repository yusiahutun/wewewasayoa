Charice Pempengco (Jake Zyrus) Biography

Jake Zyrus was born Charmaine Clarice Relucio Pempengco on May 10, 1992. Charice Pempengco became an internet sensation and also rose to international prominence in 2007 when a series of her performances posted on YouTube garnered no less than 15 million views. Her online fame has opened a wider door for her as she has been invited to perform on several notable platforms even beyond the shores of her home country. She has performed on the South Korean talent show Star King, on The Ellen DeGeneres Show, The Oprah Winfrey Show, Good Morning America as well as in The Paul O’Grady Show in London. She also performed for the President of the Philippines, Gloria Macapagal Arroyo.

The album entered the Billboard 200 at No. 8 and as a result, Charice became the first Asian artist to have a song in the top 10 of the table.

Charice Pempengco released a Christmas EP Adult Christmas List in November 2010. Her second international album Infinite was released in October 2011 and featured contributions from international music stars such as Bruno Mars, Nick Jonas and Jason Derulo. She also released 2 other albums Chapter 10 and Catharsis in September 2013 and October 2016 respectively.
