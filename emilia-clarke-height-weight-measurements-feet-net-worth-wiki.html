    Emilia Clarke Wiki

    The English actress was born on 23 October 1986 as Emilia Isabelle Euphemia Rose Clarke. She grew up with her brother in Berkshire. Her father, who died in 2016, was a theatre sound engineer, while her mother Jennifer Clarke was a businesswoman. She was also the director of the charity The Anima Foundation. Because of the nature of her father’s work, Emilia attended the theatre and fell in love with acting and musical theatre.

    Does Emilia attend the Rye St. Antony School in Headington? She completed her secondary education at St Edward’s in Oxford and from there she went on stage while studying at the Drama Centre London. After graduating in 2009 she began to apply for auditions. She also took on several jobs to make ends meet. Emilia worked as a licensed real estate agent, bar saleswoman, waitress, and call center employee, among other things.

    Clarke made her very first television appearance in 2009 in an episode of the British soap opera Doctors. Her film debut was Spike Island in 2012 and Emilia also appeared in the film Triassic Attack. As a voice actress, she has appeared on shows such as Futurama, Robot Chicken, and Animals.

    Before her big breakthrough with the role of Daenerys Targaryen in Play of Thrones, the popular actress took on smaller television roles. She was not the first choice for this role. Emilia was used to replacing Tamzin Merchant, who was the original cast for the role of Daenerys Targaryen.

    The role gave her the biggest name recognition in the industry and opened further doors in her acting career. In 2015 Emilia played the spirited and tough role of Sarah Connor in the sci-fi movie Terminator Genisys. In interpreting this role, Emilia trained to be armed, to shoot, and to fight; she received praise from the legendary Arnold Schwarzenegger for her outstanding performance in the film. Now, for someone whose favorite sports are running, skiing, horseback riding, ice skating, and swimming, the sense of hard roles is perhaps to be expected.

    The star actress showed her sweet side and played the role of Louisa Clark in the romantic movie Me Before You. She also played a leading role in the English-language adaptation of the 2015 Korean romantic comedy The Beauty Inside. Emilia’s fans are also eager to see her in the female lead role in the film Solo: A Star Wars Story. The film is scheduled for release on May 25, 2018.

    If Emily were not an actress, she would have ended up either as a graphic designer, architect, or singer. Emilia loves singing very much. Acting did not seem to be the wrong choice for the British actress so far. For her amazing role in the play of thrones, she won an Emmy Award for best-supporting actress in a drama and some other award nominations.
