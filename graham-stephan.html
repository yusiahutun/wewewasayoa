Introduction

Graham Stephan is an American real estate agent, YouTuber, and one of the agents at The Oppenheim Group.

His famous clients include Wale, LaVar Arrington, Suki Waterhouse, Chloe Moretz, and Orlando Bloom.

Biography

He was born on April 21, 1990, in the USA.

Graham obtained his Real Estate license in 2008. Later, he joined Coldwell Banker Previews International. He worked there for six years.

The Oppenheim Group

In 2015, Stephan started working as a realtor associate at The Oppenheim Group, a brokerage that sells luxury property across Los Angeles.

”Graham was the first agent we brought onto the Oppenheim Group,” said Jason Oppenheim, the President and Founder of The Oppenheim Group. He added: ”It’s fun to watch his success.”

YouTube

@Getty

On December 25, 2016, Graham started his self-entitled YouTube channel.

At the time of writing, Stephan’s channel has over 4.6 million subscribers. 

”Once I started doing about two videos a week, the growth really just exploded,” Graham told CNBC. 

”I love making videos and sharing information, and I’m obsessed with investing and saving money and personal finance and credit cards,” Stephan explained in another interview.

In 2017, he represented Orlando Bloom, who bought a 4,000-square-foot house that dates to 1959 in Beverly Hills for $7 million.

In March 2019, he launched a second YouTube channel, The Graham Stephan Show. The first video was an interview with real estate tycoon Ben Mallah.

In 2019, Graham was featured in Selling Sunset, a reality TV series that follows a group of agents from the Oppenheim Group’s real estate brokerage firm.

In 2020, Stephan and Jack Selby started a podcast called – ”The Iced Coffee Hour.”

Fiancée


Graham Stephan is dating YouTuber Savannah Smiles/Macy.

She wrote in her channel description: ”You can call me Macy, Savannah, Gigi, and/or Graham’s Girlfriend. I don’t care.”

”In case you guys didn’t know, we legally changed my name to Graham’s Girlfriend,” she joked in an August 2019 video.

Graham and Macy got engaged in 2022.
