    Isabelle Fuhrman is best known for her portrayal of the twisted character Esther in 2009's psychological thriller Orphan. Also, she has appeared as ruthless Clove in The Hunger Games (2012) and as Max in Dear Eleanor (2014).

    Additionally, Isabelle has also done magazine spreads for Marie Claire, Vanity Fair, and Teen Vogue. And, she has worked in commercials for Pizza Hut and Kmart too. 

    Dating Life

    Isabelle is dating Nicky Leonard. 

    Isabelle Fuhrman poses with boyfriend Nicky Leonard in April 2019 (Photo: Nicky Leonard's Instagram)

    The actress mentioned her boyfriend on her 2020 new year post on Instagram and shared that Nicky has truly changed her life. 

    Bio: Age, Family And Education Details

    The Don't Let Me Go (2013) actress was born on 25 February 1997 in Washington, District of Columbia, USA.

    Her parents are Nick Fuhrman and Elina Fuhrman. Isabelle's father is a former political candidate and a business consultant while her mother is a journalist and has worked for CNN. 

    Also, she has one older sibling named Madeline Fuhrman; she is a singer and songwriter. 

    About Another American Actress:- Who Is America Ferrera Husband? Wedding, Baby & Family Details

    Isabelle went to Buckley School in Sherman Oaks. She further attended the Royal Academy of Dramatic Art (RADA) and also briefly studied at The Westminster Schools in Georgia.

    In 2015, she graduated from the Stanford University Online High School. 

    Career

    The actress was first discovered at the age of seven, by a casting agent for the Cartoon Network and was cast for the show Cartoon Fridays.

    However, she was noticed through her breathtaking performance in the movie Orphan (2009). She later went on to star in movies like Hounddog (2007), From Up on Poppy Hill (2011), All the Wilderness (2014), Cell (2016), and Good Girls Get High (2018).

    Also, she has lent her voice for the 2014 animated movie Snow Queen 2.

    Further, Isabelle is on the Advisory Board of the Love & Art Kids Foundation, which is a Los Angeles based non-profit organization.

    Some Interesting Facts About Isabelle Fuhrman

    • The Orphan (2009) actress stands at the height of 5 feet 3 inches(1.6 m).
    • Her ethnicity is Russian Jewish, with an American nationality.
    • She was featured as one of the fifty faces of the future by Nylon Magazine Young Hollywood Issue.
    • Her mother is an immigrant from the Russian Soviet Federative Socialist Republic.
    • She is good friends with Don't Let Me Go (2013) and Dear Eleanor (2016) co-star Joel Courtney.
