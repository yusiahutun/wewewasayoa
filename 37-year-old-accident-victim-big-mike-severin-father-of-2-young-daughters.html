37-Year-Old Accident Victim, 'Big Mike' Severin, Father Of 2 Young Daughters

 Posted on June 10, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  218 words  |  Larita Shotwell

Severin, 37, died Saturday in a single-car accident in Eagleswood. 

He was born on Nov. 20, 1982, in Point Pleasant and grew up in Brick.

He worked for Ocean County Utilities Authority in West Creek. He was actively involved in the Free Masons and his union. 

"Big Mike" as he was known to friends and family is survived by his wife, Jennifer; their daughters Liliana, who is four and Adalina, who is two; his parents, Leonard and Joan Severin; two brothers, Scott and Brian Severin; three sisters, Robin, Bridget and Colleen Severin; and nieces and nephews.

He also is survived by his two grandfathers. Leonard Severin and Robert Byrnes, and his faithful dog, Charlie. As well as his mother-in-law AnnMarie Rizzo, brother-in-law Joey Rizzo and his wife, Kait Rizzo and sister-in-law, Christina Rizzo.

Calling hours are from 4 p.m. to 8 p.m. on Wednesday at Timothy E. Ryan Home for Funerals, 145 St. Catherine Blvd. in Toms River. 

Funeral Services will be held at the funeral home at 10 a.m. on Thursday with burial to follow at St. Joseph Cemetery in Toms River.

Condolences may be expressed to the family by visiting: www.ryanfuneralhome.com
