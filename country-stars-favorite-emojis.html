    A middle finger from Tyler Farr and a pile of poop from Trace Adkins are two emojis you can count on if you’re in either guy's texting circle. The “Better in Boots” singer says he’s texted the bird to another famous superstar fairly recently. Adkins is one of a number of country singers who loves the smiling brown pile, and they’re not all men!

    During CMA Awards week in Nashville, Taste of Country Nights host Sam Alex asked dozens of country singers what their favorite emoji was, and the answers were varied, to say the least. Chris Young and Mo Pitney impersonated their go-to character, while Brantley Gilbert is convinced his favorite emoji looks like him. At least two groups admitted a fondness for the purple eggplant emoji. Does this mean they’re vegetarians?

    Watch as Scotty McCreery, Florida Georgia Line, Jason Aldean, Jana Kramer, Thomas Rhett and more reveal their favorite emojis. We asked Lee Brice too, but … well, you’ll have to see his reply to believe it.
