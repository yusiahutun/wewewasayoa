    17 Apr 2024, 00:00

    Award season is just around the corner, and as always, the industry is kicking things off with the annual Academy Museum Gala to raise money for the museum. And this year, the stars were starring. Pretty much everyone one who is anyone was in attendance at the swanky event from Kendall Jenner and Hailey Bieber to Leonardo DiCaprio, Meryl Streep, and Dua Lipa.

    The third annual gala took place on Sunday, Dec. 3, at the Academy Museum of Motion Pictures in Los Angeles. This year's event also honored Meryl Streep, Michael B. Jordan, Oprah Winfrey, and Sofia Coppola, for the impact they have each had on the industry.

    The many A-listers in attendance walked the carpet before heading inside, and because there were just too many good looks to count, we narrowed it down to the best of the best. Keep scrolling of our favorite 'fits of the evening, and you're welcome in advance.

    Kaia Gerber

    The model arrived in a custom Celine black strapless ballgown with silver polka dots.

    Gemma Chan

    Chan brought the glam in a glitzy Louis Vuitton column dress.

    Charles Melton

    The May December star had us swooning in a black suit and silk button up.

    Salma Hayek

    Hayek oozed Old Hollywood glamour in a plunging ivory gown.

    Margaret Qualley

    Qualley looked whimsical in Chanel.

    Hari Nef

    The Barbie star brought the heat in a sexy ivory-and-black cut-out two-piece.

    Angela Bassett

    She sure did the thing in a gorgeous red Schiaparelli gown with draping sleeves and a hood.

    Keke Palmer

    The actress looked regal in a navy blue strapless ballgown by Monique Lhuillier.

    Rachel Zegler

    Zegler glowed in a glitzy gold corseted gown from Vivienne Westwood, Yvonne Leon jewelry, and matching strappy Jimmy Choo shoes.

    Christina Ricci

    The Wednesday star wore a sparkly silver-and-purple naked dress by Fendi with Martin Katz jewelry.

    America Ferrera

    The Barbie star arrived in a tweed Chanel gown with strapless neckline and a flouncy mermaid-style skirt.

    Zoë Kravitz

    The actress (and newly-minted fiancée) wore one of the season's hottest colors by sporting a rich brown slinky silk dress from Saint Laurent.

    Cara Jade Myers

    The Killers of the Flower Moon star wore a shimmery green gown with dramatic shoulders and a keyhole cutout.

    Zooey Deschanel

    The newly engaged actress wore her something blue in a sequin Shushutong two piece.

    Camila Morrone

    The Daisy Jones & the Six actress looked ethereal in a white Chanel tulle ballgown.

    Sandra Oh

    Oh arrived in a simple and sophisticated black silk dress.

    Natalie Portman

    Portman shimmered in a stunning black-and-gold Dior gown.

    Lupita Nyong'o

    Nyong'o arrived to the event in a dainty floral lace Chanel couture frock with a high-low skirt and layers of ruffles. She completed the look with a sweet flower crown made to look like Baby's-breath.

    Greta Gerwig

    The Barbie director arrived to the event in a lace Chanel gown with silver-and-gold embellishments along the neckline and shoulders.

    Demi Moore

    Moore looked absolutely breathtaking in a rose gold sequined Tamara Ralph gown with a giant rosette embellishment on her chest.

    Simu Liu

    Liu looked like the Ken he is in a dapper navy suit.

    Tracee Ellis Ross

    The actress added a pop of color to her black-and-white Richard Quinn dress with a green clutch and matching earrings.

    Michelle Williams

    The Oscar-nominated actress was striking in an ornate charcoal-colored Louis Vuitton dress with silver fringe detailing.

    LaKeith Stanfield

    The Get Out actor wore a fun cheetah-print button up and black trousers from Saint Laurent.

    Greta Lee

    Lee arrived looking ready for the holidays in a red Loewe dress with an off-the-shoulder neckline and dramatic statement sleeves.

    Ava Duvernay

    Duvernay looked like Hollywood royalty in a bespoke floor-length sequined cape.

    Olivia Wilde

    Wilde wore a black lycra Alexandre Vauthier dress, which she paired with a bedazzled choker.

    Ke Huy Quan

    The Oscar-winning actor looked dapper in a navy blue Dior tuxedo.

    Jodie Turner-Smith

    The actress put a whole new spin on the trench coat trend by arriving in a massive ballgown with a bodice resembling the jacket style.

    Regina Hall

    Another red look to add to your holiday moodboard. Hall wore a crimson ruched long-sleeve Monique Lhuillier dress.

    Lily Gladstone

    The Killers of the Flower Moon actress looked like a ray of sunshine in an orange Marni dress with a layered skirt.

    Julianne Moore

    The actress was the picture of sophistication in an emerald floor-sweeping gown with a built in cape and keyhole cutout.

    Hailey Bieber

    The model arrived with her bff Kendall Jenner and showed off some side boob in her black sequin Saint Laurent gown.

    Saoirse Ronan

    Ronan wore an ivory pleated two-piece set from Louis Vuitton that included a sculptural top and a matching maxiskirt skirt with a thigh-high leg slit.

    Meryl Streep

    Streep opted for a classy little black dress that had a surprise cutout, bow, and sequin detailing on the back.

    Meghann Fahy

    The White Lotus star taught a masterclass in making simplicity interesting in a black Givenchy column gown with a lace top that wrapped around the front of her neck.

    Dua Lipa

    Never one to shy away from a bold fashion statement, the singer arrived in a black sheer lace dress, which she layered over a bralette and matching thong.

    Sofia Coppola

    The barrier-breaking filmmaker was polished in a navy button-up dress.

    Selena Gomez

    Billie Eilish

    Nicola Peltz and Brooklyn Beckham

    The fashion power couple arrived with Peltz wearing a pink strapless gown with a rosette appliqué and a neck scarf, while Brooklyn let his leading lady take the spotlight by wearing a simple black suit.

    Michaela J. Rodriguez

    The Pose star wore a lilac-color Versace gown comprised of silk and lace.

    Julia Garner

    Garner looked vibrant in a bright yellow see-through 3.1 Phillip Lim dress.

    Chrissy Teigen

    Teigen arrived to the event in an ivory naked dress with a sculptural neckline alongside her husband John Legend.

    Sheryl Lee Ralph

    Cara Delevingne

    The model's approach to goth-girl glamour included a black tulle ballgown from Del Core.

    Natasha Lyonne

    The Poker Face star looked effortlessly cool in a Miu Miu look consisting of a bedazzled sheer shirt layered under an overall-style maxidress.
