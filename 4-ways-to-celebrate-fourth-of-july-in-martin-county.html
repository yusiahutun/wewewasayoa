    A Family Fourth of July and Golf Cart Parade

    The City of Stuart will host a family-friendly evening of food trucks, children’s activities, live music, fireworks, and more at Downtown Stuart Flagler Park (201 S.W. Flagler Avenue) from 4 to 10 p.m. Raquel Renner and Dottie Kelly and Rock the House Band will both bring unstoppable energy to the Riverwalk Stage from 5 to 10 p.m. Fireworks begin at 9 p.m.

    For the first time ever, the City of Stuart will also welcome locals to register and climb aboard their golf carts for the inaugural Fourth of July Golf Cart Parade, kicking off at 10 a.m. at the Tenth Street Community Center. Decorate your ride for a chance to win prizes. Registration is $25.

    For more information about both events, call 772-288-5340.

    Fun at the Farm

    Happy Place Ranch (7151 S.W. 42nd Street) in Palm City will welcome revelers for a day on the farm with the animals, from 10 a.m. to 2 p.m. For $5, guests will enjoy pony and hay rides, as well as visits with the farm’s goats, sheep, donkeys, emu, tortoises.

    Picnic Under the Big Tent

    Head to First Baptist Stuart’s Reborn Farm (6050 S. Kanner Highway) for a festive barbecue lunch, complete with games, a bounce house for children, snow cones and other sweet treats, and more family fun.

    For more information, call 772-287-7422.
