MW3 Blackcell Season 3 Purchase Not Showing, Not Unlocked

 Posted on February 5, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  337 words  |  Reinaldo Massengill

If you bought Call of Duty Modern Warfare 3 BlackCell Season 3, but it is now showing in-game, you are not alone. The second Call of Duty season of 2024 is now live across multiplayer, Zombies and Warzone. However, instead of fighting zombies and other enemies, many players are fighting with all kinds of bugs. In this guide, we explain what to do if MW3 Blackcell Season 3 is not unlocked in-game after you purchase it on Steam or other platforms.

MW3 Blackcell Season 3 Purchase Not Showing, Not Unlocked

MW3 Blackcell Season 3 Purchase Not Showing In-Game,

New Call of Duty season means new content, new features, new modes… and a lot of new bugs. All bugs are annoying, but those that cost you money are especially infuriating. And that’s exactly what is currently happening to many players. Namely, since the launch of Season 3, a number of players started reporting about an issue with this season’s battle pass. Namely, it seems that MW3 Blackcell Season 3, if purchased via Steam, will not show in-game. “Purchased it via Steam. I see it in DLC for my game when I am on the screen to start the game, but when I go into the game, nothing is unlocked for me. Is anyone else having this issue,” asks Reddit user Proof12x. And he is just one reporting about the same problem.

Unfortunately, to the best of our knowledge, there’s currently not much you can do about it. Players tried restarting the game and PC, logging in and out of their accounts, and other usual methods. However, none of these seems to work. Hence, for now, if you bought MW3 Blackcell Season 3, but it is not showing in the game, the best thing you can do is to report the issue immediately to Activision support. Explain to them exactly what happened. Hopefully, they will resolve your issue quickly. For now, that’s all we know. Nevertheless, we will update the article once we have more information.
