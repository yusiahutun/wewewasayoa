    What does Luke Benward do?

    Luke Benward is an actor and singer. According to his interview on The Italian Rêve, he has been acting since childhood, though his earliest memories of acting remain vague. “Honestly, I was so little, but I remember being able to go through the bootcamp course of the military base we were shooting on,” he said, referring to his acting debut on Mel Gibson We Were Soldiers in 2002, in which he played the star’s son.He has since starred in TV shows and films such as Good Luck Charlie (2013), Cloud 9 (2014) and Dolly Parton’s Dumplin’ with Jennifer Aniston. The multitalented 28-year-old also released an album in 2009 when he was still a teen, titled Let Your Love Out.

They are believed to have started dating in 2019, after they were seen getting cosy together outside a club in Hollywood, Cosmopolitan reported.
