    News anchors play an integral role in disseminating information, and such value is hard to measure. Of course, there is no definite figure for this role, but it is primarily associated with their skills, audience appeal of the individual and their reputation. And Fox News has numerous big names. So, who are the lowest paid Fox News anchors?

    How much do Fox News contributors make?

    The public got wind of Fox News contributors’ salaries after some former employees were forced to file financial disclosure forms. Ten had to open up about their wages, bringing insight into Fox News on-air contributors' remuneration.

    From the financial disclosures, the average Fox News contributor salary is about $141,000, ranging from a low of $31,336 for personalities like Georgette Mosbacher (who served as an ambassador to Poland) to a high of $569,423 for John Bolton. John served as Donald Trump’s national security advisor.

    Another employee of Fox News, former Sen. Scott Brown, disclosed that he earned a salary of $175,000 between 2013 and 2017. Other salaries include $80,000 for Richard Grenell, who worked as a Fox News contributor for three and a half years.

    K.T. McFarland received $64,000 annually while working for the news network. He worked as a national security analyst.

    According to various salary websites like Zippia, the average earnings of Fox News contributors is $56,921 per year. This means that one can make around $27 per hour. The most significant known salary of a Fox News employee is of Bill Shine. He earned an income of $1.46 million while acting as the co-president of Fox News.

    How much do Fox News anchors make and who is the highest-paid?

    Bill’s co-president role is one of the highest-paying roles at Fox News, the highest amount one can earn at the news channel. On the other hand, the lowest paying role is an assistant receptionist, about $20,000 annually.

    Production assistants earn around $27,000 annually, and a camera operator can earn $150,000 annually at Fox News.

    Despite these figures being public for quite some time, Fox News still is not as open as people want about their contributors’ salaries.

    How does Fox News calculate salaries?

    Even though Fox News is yet to come out clear about Fox News personalities' salaries, professionals in the industry have a good idea. News networks closely guard these rates and consider them as trade secrets. Popular contributors start with a per-appearance rate agreement with a guaranteed retainer for agreeing to be exclusive contributors on Fox News.

    If, in future, the individual on a per-appearance rate manages to attract a massive audience and increase’s the cable news network’s ratings, they can get a flat-fee contract paid annually.

    Who are the highest-paid on Fox News?

    Tucker Carlson is likely the highest-paid on Fox News, with a salary of $35 million annually. This massive income from the cable news network makes him one of the wealthiest television hosts in the world. Tucker has hosted the nightly political talk show, “Tucker Carlson Tonight on Fox News.

    Who is the highest-paid female on Fox News?

    Ainsley Earnhardt is likely the highest-paid female on Fox News, earning around $400,000 annually. She started working at Fox News Channel in 2007. She became the host of Fox & Friends in 2016. Ainsley has interviewed numerous famous personalities, including Donald Trump, which makes her one of the most sought-after in the media industry.
