Airport Tycoon can be a pretty demanding Roblox game, as there are plenty of upgrades that you would need to purchase to ensure your airport empire is known to everyone. Players are always looking out for ways to expand and upgrade their airports, and our codes can help you with that. Like most Roblox games, Airport Tycoon allows players to input special reward codes, and in this game, most of them will grant you important Cash that lets you greatly expand the number of employees and rooms of your airport.

How to redeem codes

Redeeming your Airport Tycoon codes is very similar to other Roblox titles. Follow these simple steps and you’ll be ready in no time:

  • Log into the Airport Tycoon game.
  • On the left side of your screen, you will find a Twitter icon under the Shop menu. Click on it.
  • Copy and paste or type in the codes.
  • Click on the ‘Redeem’ button and any active codes will be automatically added to your account.
