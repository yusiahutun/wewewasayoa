Raquel Leviss Said She Is Ready To Date Again After Breaking Off Her Engagement With James Kennedy

 Posted on March 24, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  277 words  |  Martina Birk

Raquel Leviss is ready to date again. Four months ago, she called off her engagement to James Kennedy. The celebrity revealed the news to Katie Maloney on her show You’re Gonna Love Me. She said she had cocktails with Peter Madrigal. “I had a great time,” Raquel told Katie, adding that this was her “first formal date after the breakup.” Then she disclosed that peter was the first to ask her out, to which she said, “Ok, sure.” Her date was “scary yet invigorating.”

A month after their split, James was seen dating Ally Lewber. Raquel said on the podcast that she believes it was “fast,” but she doesn’t criticize him for it. She told her listeners on the show that she thinks being single isn’t all awful and may help people heal.

James Kennedy’s history with Raquel Leviss

Raquel Leviss met James Kennedy in 2016 at a New Year’s Eve party. She featured in five episodes of Vanderpump Rules Season 5 in November 2016. Many ladies on the program accused James of cheating on Raquel with them, but Raquel always stayed by her lover. James just loves Leviss, according to Doute’s friend Hope, because he can do everything he wants without consequence.

Raquel answered yes when James proposed at the Iconic Empire Polo Fields in May 2021, but they broke up in 2021. Raquel made her split extremely public, including organizing a ‘Un engagement’ party at the Vanderpump Rules reunion. Raquel isn’t the only one dating again. The 2022 iHeartRadio Music Awards saw James Kennedy make his romance “red carpet official.” While their quick break-up stunned many, it seems both are doing a lot better today.
